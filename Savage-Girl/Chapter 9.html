Chapter 9: Scenario

“Confession, that was unfortunate.”

Perhaps it was after the prom that will likely be talked about for the next few years.

In the student council room, Seira, dressed in pitch-black attire, spoke.

『I like Kagisaka-kun just like everyone else, but I want to remain friends. And it’s not fair to confess your feelings in front of such a big crowd, it feels like you’re pushing through on impulse? If I were to give it a score, maybe it’s 35 points. If I were to be confessed to, I’d prefer a romantic atmosphere when we’re alone.』

That was what Tomori added onstage after she had just cut me off.

“But Kimitaka’s reaction was good.”

“Is that so?”

“『At least it wasn’t a complete failure. I guess I’ll join Azusa Tomori’s Heartbroken Club.』 Didn’t you say something like that? Clearly, everyone’s perspective changed after that. “

“True, that was funny.”

“Of course, and to top it all off, you said, 『Ah, what a shame. I even went as far as playing a violin recording and pretending to play it onstage to make myself look cool.』 He was playing the jester role quite convincingly.”

“You remember every word quite well.”

“Well, even among the third-year students, I have the best grades, you can’t surpass me in excellence. Nobody noticed, but you were actually playing and not using a recording, right? That performance was splendid. I’m sure everything that happened tonight was just as you wanted it to be.”

“Seira, did you figure everything out after all?”

Performing a confession act and getting rejected by Tomori in front of a crowd.

That was my plan. That way, the antagonist on the underground website would be relieved.

Because even though they were rejected by Tomori, many people still couldn’t give up on her.

So, I thought sympathy and camaraderie would gather around me, and it worked out well.

【That guy K got rejected, LOL.】

【But it was amazing, right? Even though the performance used a recording, that confession was truly heartfelt.】

【You can’t confess on a prom stage! Isn’t that common sense?】

【Maybe I’ll allow him to join. Welcome to the A • T Heartbroken Club.!】

The underground website is buzzing with activity.

In reality, my classmates encouraged me, and there were many who praised the courage it took to confess on stage.

(Well, above all else)

The slander and defamation against Chifuyu on the underground website have disappeared.

Tomori and her performed a duet on stage and showed that they reconciled.

Furthermore, thanks to the performance that rivaled professionals, Chifuyu’s evaluation has soared.

As a senpai, I couldn’t be happier.

“Your confession was really good though. I can’t believe someone who hates standing out as much as you do could come up with something like that.”

“I guess.”

“Huh? ‘I guess’… Wait, could it be…”

“That confession was something Tomori and I worked on together.”

It was a collaborative effort we made on the night I stayed over at Tomori’s house.

『I-Is this for real!? Even if it’s a lie, who asks for help with their confession dialogue from the one they’re confessing to?”

『It’s a rational decision. ‘Everyone’s Friends’ is more likely to craft a confession that resonates with the students than I am. Remember, you boasted before? Your Japanese grades were better than mine.』

『That’s true, but…』

『The deadline is tomorrow morning. Come on, let’s do our best, model student. I won’t let you sleep until it’s done. If you doze off, I’ll physically wake you up.』

『You jerk! You really are a true jerk!』

She complained plenty, but she still helped me until morning.

At the same time, I thought it was the best way to cheer Tomori up when she was down.

(It seems like I’m taking advantage of her overly kind nature, but the hero-like ‘Everyone’s Friends’ gets fired up when someone relies on them.)

And… well, you know.

I thought it would be awkward and wouldn’t go well if I tried to come up with a confession dialogue for Tomori on my own.

“I see. By the way, did you also suggest to Azusa-chan that she play a duet with Chifuyu-chan, Kimitaka?”

“Well, yeah.”

Based on my experience talking with Chifuyu as a senpai, I knew that she often played duets with Tomori.

I thought that through the performance, she might find a way to reconcile with her.

“Impressive, Kimitaka.”

She said while crafting a perfectly executed smile like a former child actor.

‘Everyone’s Admiration’ starts to weave her words.

“Dealing with the slander against you on the underground site and also addressing Azusa-chan and Chifuyu-chan’s relationship, you’re resolving two problems simultaneously.”

” ….. “

“Furthermore, you’ve also solved Azusa’s weakness, haven’t you? Azusa-chan and Chifuyu-chan should have become even closer than before, and from now on, Azusa-chan will open her heart not only to you but also to Chifuyu-chan. It’s exactly like the proverbial ‘After the rain falls, earth hardens’! Truly amazing! Everything is going well thanks to Kimitaka—”

” You say that so well, huh? But this whole incident went pretty much according to your scenario, Hiroto-san.”

It was the moment I mentioned a nickname that I had never been used within the academy.

“——You’re truly sharp, as expected, Kei.”

As if responding to her former comrade. The current leader of Kazarina and the girl known as ‘Hiroto’ wore a quiet smile.

 

“How long have you known that?”

With a remarkably mature attitude, Seira Hirowatari, dressed in a black dress, inquired.

“I had a feeling you were up to something when you asked me for a favor right from the start. It became decisively strange when I met you at the pool.”

“Oh, I also thought it was a little unnatural.”

“Right, it’s a weird coincidence that Seira showed up just when Kazami and I were together, and she was with Sadaoka, who used to bully Kazami in the past.”

Seira probably has a collaborator.

She likely used that person to figure out that Tomori and I, and even Chifuyu, would be going to Yomisaka Land.

Given the circumstances, it’s the only logical conclusion.

Furthermore, using that collaborator—.

“After I became Tomori’s partner, you found out that Kazami was troubled.”

” ….. “

“And you decided to use that fact. You intentionally said things that would provoke Kazami, getting her into a fight. And by having Sadaoka present beforehand, I escalated the situation.”

” ….. “

“Sadaoka is interested in Seira, and he had a history with Kazami. You anticipated that if you manipulated things properly, Kazami would clash with him. Furthermore, didn’t you even predict the scenario where Tomori would stop the fight?”

Of course, this is just one of the storylines Seira came up with.

(She must have prepared many possibilities.)

Among those, she chose the one that best suited the situation and manipulated the stage.

Yes, Seira Hirowatari is the complete opposite of Azusa Tomori.

She moves others with meticulously crafted strategies and honed acting skills to solve problems.

Like a puppeteer controlling a puppet show.

Just as she manipulates puppets with strings, she deftly moves others according to her intentions.

It is this kind of talent that allowed her to take over the leadership after I left Kazarina. Truly, Kazarina’s brain.

Her combat abilities might be low, but it’s okay because there are others who specialize in that area.

“After the rain falls, earth hardens.”

She said that earlier, but Seira’s goal is to break Tomori and Kazami’s relationship once and then mend it. And to create a stronger bond than before. If she does that—.

“Azusa-chan will be able to open her heart to Chifuyu-chan. It’s a rational decision, don’t you think?”

“Ah… but spreading rumors all around the academy that Kazami and Tomori had a fight, isn’t that going a bit too far?”

“… I was surprised. To think that it was noticed to that extent.”

“The speed at which the rumors spread was excessively fast, no matter how you look at it.”

“You’re truly perceptive, Kei. I don’t want you to misunderstand, but I—”

” I know you weren’t trying to belittle anyone. You wanted to put Tomori’s best friend in a crisis, and you hoped that would make me move even more desperately to save the partner’s best friend.”

“——Everything is correct.”

Applause resonated, like offering praise.

“I had a lot of other plans to patch things up, but I’m glad you’re on board.”

“Don’t talk nonsense. You should have been convinced that I would definitely move.”

“Kimitaka is kind, after all. As the student council president, I just wanted to solve the problems that the academy’s hero faced. Azusa-chan had no friends she could open her heart to except you. It would have been a major issue for Aisu Academy if she had collapsed.”

“But is it really okay for the student council president to engage in these covert activities?”

“You’re equally guilty, aren’t you, Troublemaker-kun? You perfectly saw through my plan yet didn’t expose Azusa-chan and Chifuyu-chan. Instead, you used them to make them reconcile.”

“That is…”

“It’s a cool-headed judgment to the point where I want to hug and praise you immediately. Hey, Kei? Why don’t you come back to Kazarina? The whole team misses you. Of course, I miss you the most.”

” ….. “

“As a childhood friend, as the student council president, as the leader of Kazarina, I sincerely believe this. Your intelligence is too precious to let smolder away at the academy.”

“Sorry, Hiroto-san.”

I can’t go back to Kazarina.

Even if everyone on the team forgives me, I can’t forgive myself.

For the mistakes I made, the ones Kei made. And besides—

“Right now, I am Azusa Tomori’s partner.”

I state it clearly.

Upon hearing my answer, Seira seems to accept it, nodding with a gentle expression.

“Got it. But if you ever feel like coming back, just let me know, okay? We’ll give you a grand welcome. Big sis will be happy to have you back.”

“I’ll keep that in mind.”

“By the way… sigh. Azusa-chan is so lucky. To have Kimitaka feel this strongly about her. I’m so jealous.”

“Then how about you settle it with a fight?”

“Huh? Are you suggesting a duel for Aiyu’s sake? Like I said before, Azusa-chan—”

“Don’t worry. Tomori will… No, she’ll become Seira’s opponents soon.”

“What…!?”

For the first time today, Seira’s expression falters.

She realized that my words are an unmistakable declaration of war.

“I see. I didn’t predict this development.”

“You said it’s a waste to let me smolder away at the academy, but from now on, it won’t be so easy.”

“Indeed, Kimitaka. Tonight has been quite eventful, and it’s a pity I couldn’t dance with you at the prom…”

‘I’m sure there will be more exciting things to come.’

‘Everyone’s Admiration’ said with a gorgeous smile on her face.
