(Ah, that reminds me......)

The road that went on forever. The three of them walked side by side, holding on to their hopes and anxieties.

(That was the future we were hoping for......)

Their first wish, and dreams that will never be fulfilled. Seeing this last glimpse, Ken's consciousness faded and vanished along with his envious resignation, a hint of loneliness, and a smile of contentment.
