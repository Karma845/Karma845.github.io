(LN) The Ordinary Head of the Family Who Became the Strongest Through Divine Trials, Returning to the Pre-Calamity World to Dominate

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

“That Incompetent Subdues Even Gods and Annihilates His Enemies!”

Due to the unprecedented calamity known as the “Miasma Deluge,” a boy named Hachiman loses his magical power and memories. As the last survivor of the most powerful onmyoji clan, the Kuzuryu Family, he is nothing more than an incapable, ornamental figurehead. Living peacefully with his adoptive mother and sister, who cherished him despite his shortcomings, Hachiman’s world is shattered when a massive outbreak of monsters claims their lives.

Overwhelmed with grief, he stands at the brink of despair—until a fateful encounter changes everything.

“At the culmination of your trials, I shall return you to the world before the calamity.”

Striking a divine pact with a god at the depths of the abyss, Hachiman undergoes rigorous training, mastering the ultimate spirit arts and the Kuzuryu Family’s unparalleled martial techniques. Reborn into the tranquil days before the worst catastrophe, he vows to eradicate the root of all evil! To protect what he holds dear, Hachiman rises as the unstoppable protagonist in this tale of second chances and the ascent of the ultimate onmyoji!

Genre:

Action, Fantasy, Harem, Supernatural.
