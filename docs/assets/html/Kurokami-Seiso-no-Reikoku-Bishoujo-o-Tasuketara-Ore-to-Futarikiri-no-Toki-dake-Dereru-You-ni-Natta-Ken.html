(LN) After I Rescued a Cold-Hearted, Black-Haired, Beautiful Girl, She Started Acting Sweet Only When We Were Alone

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

A love chosen by someone else doesn’t always lead to unhappiness. In fact, perhaps it’s precisely because it was arranged that it can begin the right way.

Born as the eldest son of the prestigious former noble Ichijo family, high schooler Minato is suddenly informed by his father that his fiancée has been decided. As the heir to a distinguished household, freedom was never part of the equation — something he had accepted long ago. With a dry sense of resignation, Minato follows his father’s instructions and heads to the arranged meeting.

“I’m Nanase Rei, eldest daughter of the Nanase family. I look forward to our life together, dear husband.”

But the girl waiting for him is none other than Nanase Rei — the cold-hearted beauty from his class, known for her icy demeanor.

Thus begins their shared life, along with Rei’s relentless and unexpected affection.

Though Minato is bewildered by the sudden turn of events, he soon realizes that this new chapter may not be so bad after all — and slowly, he begins to feel happiness in the life he shares with Rei.

Genre:

Comedy, Romance, School Life.
