(WN) The ‘Prince’ of the Girls’ School Shows Her ‘Maiden’ Side Only to Me at Our Part-Time Job

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

The protagonist, Yamabuki Natsuki, attends the all-boys’ school Aotaka High School, where a certain local tradition has been passed down.

That tradition dictates that dating a student from the neighboring all-girls’ school, Suzakura High School, is considered the ultimate status symbol. Aotaka High School and Suzakura High School have produced many couples over the years, fostering a good relationship between the two schools.

However, this long-standing tradition is shaken by the presence of a single female student.

Her name is Tachibana Iori, the “Prince of Suzakura.”

With her overwhelming good looks and a personality so flamboyant that she seems like the reincarnation of a princely character, Iori has completely captivated the hearts of Suzakura students.

The students of Aotaka High School, finding themselves largely ignored, face a crisis—until a turning point arrives.

Tachibana Iori starts working at the family restaurant Cyberia, where Natsuki also works.

Assigned as Iori’s mentor, Natsuki finds himself dealing with the swarm of Suzakura students flocking to the restaurant just to see her in action, as well as a junior so obsessed with Iori that they follow her to her workplace.

Amidst all this, Natsuki begins to notice an unexpected side to the “Prince” that no one else has seen before.

Genre:

Romance, School Life, Slice of Life.
