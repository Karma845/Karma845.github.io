(LN) Exhausted with Life, I Arrived at a Village Where Beautiful Women Won’t Let Me Go

Status:

Ongoing

Synopsis:

“Dinner, a bath, or even sex… I’ll do it all for you, so won’t you stay here forever?”

Sumuragi Takuya, a former office worker utterly exhausted after quitting his job at a “black company,” flees the city with barely any luggage and finds himself in a secluded rural area called Minorisato Village.

“It’s raining quite hard outside. If you’d like, why don’t we take a break together?”

Unable to refuse the sweet invitation from Anzai Mina—the beautiful woman he befriended while taking shelter from the rain—Takuya ends up staying at her home as a live-in guest. However…

“Once you’re out of the bath, my mother’s delicious cooking will be waiting for you.”

“If it’s what you want, Takuya-san, you can stay here forever, you know?”

“…I’ll even help you release your s*xual desires like this.”

In an effort to keep Takuya from ever leaving the countryside, the pampering and “healing” begin to accelerate at an overwhelming pace!

It’s a self-indulgent, erotic, rural harem romantic comedy where no one will get in your way!

Genre:

Adult, Ecchi, Harem, R-18, Romance, Slice of Life.
