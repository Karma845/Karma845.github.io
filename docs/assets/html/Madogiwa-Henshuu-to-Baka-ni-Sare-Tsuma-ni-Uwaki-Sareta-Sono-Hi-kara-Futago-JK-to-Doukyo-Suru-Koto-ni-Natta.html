(LN) From the Day I Was Ridiculed as a Window Editor and Cheated on by My Wife, I Lived With The Twin JK's

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

I’m Mitsuhiko Okaya, 29 years old.

I’m an editor at a publishing company.

The day I witnessed my wife cheating on me, twin JKs suddenly came to my house.

“Uncle, if you let me stay tonight, you can do …… naughty things ♡”

“That …… please …… you’re the only person I can rely on …….”

Akari, a blonde gal, and Nanako, a neat, black-haired girl, a pair of high school girls I’ve never met, but they look vaguely familiar.

After my wife cheated on me, I moved to a new publishing company and started a strange life together with these two high school girls.

Akari, the younger sister, who aggressively seduces me, and Nanako, the older sister, who is reserved but actually very naughty.

The two of them heal me, and together we heal my wounded heart……

One day, my ex-wife apologizes to me. In addition, the person she cheated on me with and my old boss came to me on their knees.

She said that she realized that I was actually competent at my previous publishing company and came to apologize.

But it was too late. I’m enjoying my new life with the beautiful JKs and my white workplace.

Genre:

Comedy, Ecchi, Harem, Romance, Seinen, Slice of Life.
