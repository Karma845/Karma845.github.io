(Doujinshi) Otaku no Boku ga Ichigun Gal to Tsukiaerumade no Hanashi

Status:

Ongoing

Synopsis:

Suzuki knows that Ayase-san is way out of his league—an elite "first-string" gal he could never hope to reach. Yet, ever since the day she picked up his keychain, he hasn't been able to get her out of his head.

In a desperate bid to close the distance between them, Suzuki tries out a bold new hairstyle, only to become the laughingstock of the class. But to his surprise, Ayase-san finds it charmingly hilarious. She gives him the goofy nickname "Chomu," and from that moment on, the wall between them begins to crumble.

Their friendship deepens until one day, Suzuki is invited to her house. As they chat away like usual, Ayase-san suddenly falls silent, her eyes locked onto his.

> "Hey Chomu... do you have a girlfriend?"

Alone in her room, she draws closer. Suzuki’s body betrays him, reacting instantly to her proximity. With a mischievous look that is far too cute to handle, she reaches out.

"It’s okay. I’ll take care of it for you..."

As she touches him there, Suzuki realizes there’s no turning back.

Genre:

Adult, Ecchi, R-18, Romance, School Life.
