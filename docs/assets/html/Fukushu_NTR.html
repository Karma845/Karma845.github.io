(Doujinshi) Revenge NTR — I’ve Had Enough! Like Mother, Like Daughter, Dating Total Losers!

Status:

Ongoing

Synopsis:

Back in my student days, I was bullied by a thug named Gen'iwan. Shirase-san was the only one who ever stepped in to stop it. I harbored a faint crush on her, only to discover the truth: she was actually dating Gen'iwan, and she only acted that way to help boost his student evaluation record. Humiliated, a fire of revenge was lit within me. Years have passed since then. I poured everything into self-improvement—my physique, my face, everything—to the point where not a trace of my former self remains. I’m now a handsome man with a respectable career as a teacher. And now, I have a student named Hiyori Gen'iwan.

As it turns out, she’s the daughter of Shirase and Gen'iwan... and she’s already sleeping around with another thug. Like mother, like daughter—always opening their legs for lowlifes! I can't forgive them!!

Using every trick in the book, I'm going to entrap them both and make them submit to their own base instincts!
