(WN) What If The Original Strongest Final Boss Became a Companion Of The Protagonist?

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

Unjust. Arrogant. Merciless.

In every aspect blessed, the most formidable figure in the story, Yughram Albane Kushana, the Third Prince, had been graced with everything except for one crucial thing – humanity.

As the impending future of destruction looms closer, the newly reincarnated 15-year-old Yughram swiftly makes a decision.

—Alright, I’m going into exile.

“What if I joined the protagonist’s side? Wouldn’t I be the strongest?”

Ethics? Conscience? He left such things back in his homeland.

Utilizing his cheat-like abilities to the fullest, Yughram intends to revel in happiness and freedom—according to his plan.

Genre:

Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Romance.
