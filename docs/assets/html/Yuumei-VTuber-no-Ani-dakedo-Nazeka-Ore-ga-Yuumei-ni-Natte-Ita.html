(LN) I’m a Famous VTuber’s Big Brother, but Somehow I’m the One Who Got Famous

Status:

Ongoing

Synopsis:

“Onii-chan, you’re a streaming genius!”

Due to my little sister’s streaming accident, somehow I’m making my VTuber debut?!

I have a stepsister named Isuzu. She is the massively popular VTuber [Isuzu Wine] , who has surpassed one million subscribers.

But one day, she forgot to end her stream and broadcast our completely unguarded, private conversation to the whole world!

On stream, [Isuzu Wine] plays the character of a cheeky, sassy brat, but the real Isuzu is the exact opposite. She’s actually a clingy shut-in who publicly declares, _”I can’t live without my Onii-chan!_”

Clips of the streaming accident went viral instantly, and then—

Somehow, it was decided that I would debut as a VTuber too?!

I accepted the VTuber gig with a light heart just to help with the household bills, but no matter how many times I stream, it’s nothing but disasters.

I mess up even when collaborating with cute senior VTubers, and to top it off, my sister falls asleep mid-stream!

…And yet, I keep getting a streak of high ratings every time!?!?

Surpassing 300,000 subscribers before my official debut?!

“[Wine no Aniki] accidents are art”… wait, what the hell are you guys expecting from me?!

Originating from ‘Shousetsuka ni Narou’, a rising-star VTuber Romcom that begins with a sister’s streaming accident!

Genre:

Comedy, Ecchi, Harem, Romance, Slice of Life.
