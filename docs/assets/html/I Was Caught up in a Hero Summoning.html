(LN) I Was Caught up in a Hero Summoning

Status:

Ongoing

Synopsis:

It all happened so abruptly. After finally grasping my situation, I found myself in an entirely different world. Looking around, I noticed others in the same predicament… Could this possibly be one of those Hero developments?

The Demon Lord was slain a thousand years ago, and 800 years had passed since the last war. The nobles didn’t treat us summoned like trash; instead, we were kindly cared for. This world is at peace, and I wasn't even the hero! Just an unintentional summon. Now, I plan to enjoy this world to its fullest before returning home.

Genre:

Comedy, Fantasy, Harem, Romance, Shounen, Slice of Life
