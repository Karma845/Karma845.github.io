(LN) Saikyō Rakudai Kizoku no Kenma Kiwameshi Antō Tan

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

The Sword Saint and the Grand Sage’s true identity is a failing student at the academy!? A new clandestine fantasy begins!

The apathetic Roy Luvel, a minor noble on the outskirts of a small country, is known as a poor student at the academy, earning the nickname “The Failing Noble.” However, he harbors a certain secret.

【The Guardian of the Sword Nation, the White Sword Saint, Cloud】

【The Guardian of the Magic Empire, the Black Grand Sage, Eclipse】

The true identities of the most praised individuals in these two great nations are none other than Roy, who pretends to be incompetent!

To protect his family, Roy hides his identity and leads a triple life, secretly working in each country and enjoying freedom at the academy. Moreover, he catches the interest of beautiful girls at the academy who aspire to become Sword Saint and Grand Sage… Roy’s extraordinary daily life and battles are fiercely intense!

“I’d love to quit being a guardian, but until a successor appears, I guess I’ll keep protecting the world.”

The new clandestine fantasy of the ultimate noble who has mastered both sword and magic begins here!!

Genre:

Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Harem, Martial Arts, Mature, Mystery, Romance, School Life, Supernatural.
