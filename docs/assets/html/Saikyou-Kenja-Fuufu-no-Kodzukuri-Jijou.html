Synopsis:

For generations, the Suzaku and Byakko clans have been locked in a relentless struggle over their territories. The Suzaku leader, Shiranui, and the Byakko leader, Aura, have repeatedly dueled each other, always ending in a draw.

“Do you know how men and women can produce a child…?”
