(WN) A Beautiful Model In My Class Comes To The Coffee Shop Where I Work Part-time Only When I'm On Shift

Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

After school, I'm the only one who knows what my classmate truly feel when she visit the café.

A love story connecting a coffee-loving high school boy and a cool, beautiful model.

Shizuku Kamisaka, the beautiful model currently trending everywhere. Cool and composed. And—my classmate.

She's popular both in school and in the public eye.

Meanwhile, Juntaro, whose only notable trait is working part-time at a café after school, lives in a completely different world.

They were two people who were never supposed to cross paths—but then…

"Welcome… wait, Kamisaka?"

"Mi… Mikage-kun?"

One day, Shizuku bursts into the café with tears in her eyes—!?

This is a love story about a coffee-loving high school boy and the cool, beautiful model from his class, brought together over a single cup of coffee.

Genre:

Drama, Romance, School Life.
