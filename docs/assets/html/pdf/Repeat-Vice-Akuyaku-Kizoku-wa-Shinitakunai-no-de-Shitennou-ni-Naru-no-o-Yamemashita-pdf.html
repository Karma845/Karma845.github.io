Synopsis:

“Popular work of the ‘Vice Story’, a fantasy school-themed RPG

Its antagonist(protagonist) is Rofus, one of the Four Heavenly Kings serving the Demon Lord, a master of dark magic known as the ‘Shadow Wolf’.

In the game’s second chapter, he wields dark magic with considerable force, only to be easily defeated by the protagonist party… that was the scenario.

However, three years before the story begins, before he becomes one of the Four Heavenly Kings, Rofus has a dream. It reveals the entire plot of the game and his gruesome death.

“…No, this can’t be. I refuse to accept it. I, to meet such an undignified end. That must be prevented at all costs.”

“If that nightmare could become a reality, I will crush it with all my being.”

This is the story of Rofus, who bears the title of ‘Shadow Wolf’, an antagonist who doesn’t shine in the story, aiming to rewrite the original story for his survival.

And the gears of the story gradually begin to go awry.”
