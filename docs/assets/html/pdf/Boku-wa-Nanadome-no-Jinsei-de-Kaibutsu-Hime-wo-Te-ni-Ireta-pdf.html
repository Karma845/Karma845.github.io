Status:

Ongoing.

Synopsis:

Born the eldest son of the Marquess of Grenville, one of the numerous aristocrats of Southern Crane Empire, ‘Hugo’ had a spectacular past…, no life.

Every time he died, he would always return to the same day when he was fourteen years old. And each time he would be killed by a member of his family.

Even so, he had never been loved by his family ever since he can remember, and he continued to seek their love and affection.

He finally realized something at the end of his sixth life. He realised that the love of family is only an illusion.

Thus he vowed. If he had to go back to the day when he was 14 years old again, he’ll sever everything.

And so, Hugo begins his seventh life. This time, he left his love for his family behind.

To take his revenge, Hugo sold himself to the Grand Duke of Woodstock, the most powerful family after the Imperial Family.

There, he met Meltreza, the daughter of a mixed-race human-vampire rumored to be a “monster”.

This is the story of a man who despaired in six lives and finally finds happiness in his seventh.

Genre:

Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Romance, School Life.
