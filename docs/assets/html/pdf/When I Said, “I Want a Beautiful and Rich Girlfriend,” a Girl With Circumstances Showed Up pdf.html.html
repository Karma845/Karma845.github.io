Status:

“Just being beautiful is not love.” This is a slightly mature adolescent romantic comedy questioning the meaning of falling in love!

Shino Iori, a college student in Fukuoka, becomes engaged to a famous figure on campus, Tendou Tsukasa, after his careless remark he slipped out to his family.

She is a beauty and rich—however, Iori is put off by his fiancée, who is rumored to be ‘will sleep with anyone,’ and Tsukasa is aggressively coming onto him… where will their future go!?

This is the start of a ‘slightly mature’ romantic comedy of a breaking-off-engagement genre that questions the meaning of falling in love!!

Adult, Comedy, Drama, Romance, Slice of Life.
