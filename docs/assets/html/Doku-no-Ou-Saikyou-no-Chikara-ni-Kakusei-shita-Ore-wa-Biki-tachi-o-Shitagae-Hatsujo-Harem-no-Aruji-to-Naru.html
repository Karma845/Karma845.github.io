Synopsis:

From the moment of his birth, Caim is a cursed boy whose entire body is invaded by poison. Hated and despised, he suffers persecution even from his own father and sister. Caim unleashes his inherited talent for martial arts and decides to leave his hometown behind, living freely. However, during his journey, he inadvertently arouses the lust of a troubled beauty and a stunning woman he helps along the way with the aphrodisiac effects of his toxins…!?
