There’s a guy at my high school who’s like a rom-com protagonist. He’s completely unremarkable, yet for some reason, he’s constantly surrounded by beautiful girls, he’s an absolute anomaly.

I found myself back on the morning of my first year of high school entrance ceremony two years ago.

In this miraculous second chance at life, this time, I definitely won’t let that rom-com──
