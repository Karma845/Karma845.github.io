──Bring in the most “playable” person in this era?

I’m sure this story started with such a word from the former god.

Nice to meet you, my name is Hosone Kei.

What do you think of this story, “God is Hungry for Games”?

How was it?

The omniscient and omnipotent gods are serious wherever they are and propose the most wonderful brain strategic games to them. This would be the first time for me to challenge the genre of strategy games, and I got really excited while drawing an image of it in my mind of this work.

Humans and gods, both have a mindset to hate to lose, while playing every single game challenging each other. I hope I can continue to take pleasure while depicting such a world in my work in the upcoming future as well.
