「Don’t say selfish things. In the first place, why do I have to return to the way I used to be for you?」

「…Yeah, you’re right. Sorry, I said something selfish.」

Pressed by Tsuyu’s pressure, Kamome speaks out in remorse.

In contrast, Tsuyu seems to be unable to suppress her agitated emotions.

「…Don’t be silly.」

She raised her fist and slammed it into the concrete wall right behind her.

The skin on her fingers is torn and bleeding.

「Tsuyu…」

He certainly may have said something rude and insensitive.

However, the way she gets angry is not normal.

Now she has her hands over her face, her head drooping, and looking somewhat in pain.

Did his words hurt her that much?

「Tsuyu, I…」

Unable to watch, Kamome takes a step toward Tsuyu.

「…Ugh.」

At that moment, he heard her moan.

Looking down, he notices that Tsuyu’s right leg, around her ankle, is red and swollen.

「Tsuyu, you’re leg…」

「…Don’t look.」

Tsuyu hastily hid her foot there.

「It twisted while running up here. Because you suddenly started to run. Come on, it’s enough, isn’t it? Get out of here. If you’re too persistent, this time I’ll make a fuss about that you hurt me.」

「…Sorry.」

Seeing her glaring at him with hostility, he couldn’t say anything more.

Kamome turns his back on Tsuyu and runs away.

「…Haa.」

When Kamome was gone, Tsuyu let out a sigh and leaned her weight against the wall behind her.

Then, she just stares silently into the void──

──A few minutes later.

「Sorry to keep you waiting, Tsuyu.」

「…Ha?」

Tsuyu turns around at the sound of Kamome’s voice.

It seems that she couldn’t hide her surprise when Kamome, who should have left just a few minutes ago, reappeared.

「No, why are you back…?」

「I bought a compress and water at the pharmacy.」

Kamome offers the plastic bag in his hand to Tsuyu.

Just as he said, there was a compress and a plastic bottle of natural water inside.

「I’m sorry I hurt you. I’ll stay with you until you can walk again.」

In front of such Kamome, for a moment, Tsuyu’s eyebrows drooped, and made a face that looked like she was about to cry.

「Go home…」

Then, she immediately turns her face away and says without looking directly at Kamome.

「Go home, please…」

「…Understood.」

Since she was injured, he had no choice but to withdraw obediently as he was told.

Thinking so, Kamome leaves the place this time.

「…When the time comes for us to talk properly again. At that time, I want to make up with you again.」

That’s all he said.

◇◆◇◆◇◆

「……」

Only the miscellaneous sounds of the main street flow in without hesitation.

Left alone in the back alley, Tsuyu lowered her gaze.

『I used to admire the old Tsuyu.』

『I want you to remain the Tsuyu of those days.』

The words Kamome said stuck in his head, circling around and not disappearing.

「Don’t say selfish things…」

She clenched her teeth so hard that it could be heard the sound of them snapping.

In those eyes, there is anger at the pure feelings of a younger childhood friend who knows nothing.

…And a bit of sadness.

「…Kamome.」

There was chaotic light, different from anger, mixed with intense feelings.
