Because of embarrassment and a variety of idle thoughts.

But now──

「I want to walk a little, hand in hand with Himawari.」

Like that, Kamome told Himawari how he honestly feels right now.

「…Yes.」

Himawari nodded modestly and responded to Kamome’s request.

The two walk hand in hand through the park.

Just walking with the cheers of the attractions as background music.

Even though it’s just that, it feels like a special moment.

「Um, you know… Sorry.」

There, Himawari mutters.

Her face and neck turned bright red, her eyes slightly moistened, and she was looking down.

「After all, I’m sweating because I’m nervous, so….」

Her intertwined hand was hot, and her palm was moist and humid.

Himawari is concerned about that.

It’s not about herself, but about what Kamome thinks of her.

「…I’m sorry, too. For suddenly saying I want to hold your hand.」

「…Eh?」

But it was the same for Kamome.

Is it too much force? Am I hurting her hand?

I’m sweating too.

Isn’t it uncomfortable?

The two of them were concerned about each other, and various thoughts were going through their minds.

In their heated minds, they asked and answered themselves persistently.

──But still.

Neither Kamome nor Himawari let go of each other’s hands as they walked together through the amusement park until the closing time was announced.

 

◇◆◇◆◇◆

 

Afterwards ── after finishing today’s date and leaving the amusement park, Kamome decided to take Himawari home.

「Thanks for today, I really enjoyed it.」

「Yeah, me too.」

Kamome looks up at Himawari’s house.

No lights are on.

Nobody seems to be back yet except Himawari… not even Tsuyu.

「There was a lot that happened along the way, but I guess it’s good to say that it all worked out.」

「I guess so. In the end, maybe we should thank Tsuyu-san.」

“Ahaha”, Himawari laughs as she says that.

After all that has happened, Himawari is amazing to be able to say that, Kamome honestly thought.

「If you have any worries about Tsuyu, I want you to talk to me.」

Her older sister, who lives under the same roof, acted like that.

It might make their relationship even more strained from now on.

Thinking so, Kamome made a suggestion to Himawari.

「…Yeah」

To this, Himawari replies in a slightly implied tone.

「I’ll send you a message when I get home.」

「Yeah, I still want to talk a lot about today…」

Laughing at each other, Kamome waves her hand to leaves Himawari’s house──

「…Kamome-kun.」

But then, that’s when it happened.

Himawari stopped Kamome.

「There is one thing I would like to ask about Tsuyu-san.」

「Eh?」

「…The other day, when Kamome-kun met Tsuyu-san again at my house.」

The light at the entrance became backlit.

Himawari, who has an overall atmosphere of being in the shadows, stares at Kamome.

「Did really… nothing happened?」

「……」

For a moment, he thought his heart would stop beating.

The question posed by Himawari.

What does this… mean?

On that day too, Kamome was kissed by Tsuyu.

Is Himawari aware of that?

Does she suspect why he doesn’t tell her honestly?

(…Why now, at this timing?)

…No.

Himawari’s expression right now is one of great anxiety.

There was no such meaning behind it, now, she probably just wanted to clear the doubts that had been inside of her.

「……」

For a moment, Kamome thinks.

What should he do?

There, Kamome’s mind recalled the shocked look on her face when she told that he was kissed by Tsuyu today.

It was a look of despair, so painful to watch.

Repeatedly telling the truth may be too much of a shock.

Especially when she’s in such a mood.

Today, they were able to spend a cheerful and joyful time, fulfilling each other’s hearts.

Himawari told Kamome how special he is to her.

「…Really, nothing happened.」

Someday I will confess.

So now, please let me lie.

「I can understand Himawari being anxious. But nothing happened that day. It’s true.」

Again, a cramping pain runs through the back of his neck.

Maybe it’s guilt, maybe it’s disgust.

Kamome told Himawari, clutching the back of her neck.

「…I see. I’m sorry, for asking strange things over and over again as if I doubted Kamome-kun.」

Saying so, Himawari apologized, returned to a refreshed expression, and once again said goodbye to Kamome.

Kamome leaves the Shishido house.

Himawari held up the stuffed shark he got for her at the amusement park’s arcade until Kamome was out of sight.

(…How cute, Himawari.)

On his way home, Kamome reflected on the day.

Himawari confided in him what was on her mind.

Her strong feelings for him were clearly conveyed, and at the same time, her presence in Kamome’s heart grew.

「……」

…On the other hand, he can’t help but think of Tsuyu.

Her odd behavior today.

The way she acted like she was trying to push him into a corner.

Is she the kind of person who would normally do such a thing?

Such a vulgar thing.

When he thinks about it, he gets really angry.

A simple sense of justice.

In addition, perhaps there is the desire that he didn’t want Tsuyu to do that.

…But.

Today, by hearing Himawari’s inner thoughts and family situation, he has come to think.

「Just like Himawari… I wonder if Tsuyu is also having worries and anxieties…」

I wonder if she might also have her own circumstances, he began to take that into consideration.
