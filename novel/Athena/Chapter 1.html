Chapter 1: Future Bride

When I arrived in front of the house, I was a little surprised.

A familiar moped was parked in front of our house.

This license plate bike is…

“Son of a motorcycle shop, you’re back now?”

From the front entrance of my home, a middle-aged man in a chef’s outfit came out.

He’s the head of Sushimaru, the sushi restaurant located in the shopping district.

“…You’re doing maintenance on your bike at my home?”

Though I thought it might be different, those were the words that came out.

“No, it’s a sushi delivery. Or rather, Hakuba, did something good happen? For the Ouji family to order the highest-grade sushi…as far as I know, this is the first time!”

He’s a very rude old man, but he’s right to be surprised.

If my memory is correct, I don’t recall ordering anything higher than the regular grade, let alone the highest grade.

“Who would have thought that I’d get three ‘Yukichi’ notes from the Ouji house? Could tomorrow be the day when octopuses rain down from the sky?”

(TN: ‘Yukichi’ refers to the ¥10,000 bill in Japan, which features the portrait of Yukichi Fukuzawa. )

I know he’s being disrespectful, but I can understand that sentiment.

Octopuses will likely fall from the sky tomorrow. That’s just how unimaginable this is for our family.

“Oh well, no time to dilly-dally. The next delivery is waiting. Alright then, take care, Hakuba!”

The head chef waved his hand lightly at me and headed towards the next delivery location.

“Could it be that Old woman’s prediction is coming true…?”

I’m about to have the highest-grade sushi.

Normally, I’d be ecstatic and call Kanako to brag, but right now, I can’t genuinely enjoy it.

For now, let’s go inside the house. I opened the front door and entered.

“Hmm? Is someone else here?”

Why did I come to that conclusion? The answer is simple,

“Ah~~ I’m so lucky to have such a cute girl pouring me drinks. I’m really happy right now!”

“Come on, don’t be shy, eat as much as you want.”

The cheerful voices of my parents echoed from the living room on the first floor.

It seems that someone, a guest perhaps, has come to the house. As evidence, there was a pair of shoes I’d never seen before.

Indeed, shoes that didn’t quite match the entrance of our house were neatly placed there.

Even though I thought it was impolite, I couldn’t help but be drawn to these shoes, lifting them up to my eye level.

“…What’s this? They’re incredibly flashy.”

Rather than flashy, the term ‘fancy’ would be more fitting.

It’s like the white heels a Western princess from a fairy tale would wear.

I wonder Where did I meet the owner of such shoes?

At least, they’re not in my memory. My curiosity was thoroughly piqued.

With that in mind, I scattered my loafers, headed straight for the living room, turned the doorknob, and stepped into the place where cheerful conversations were flying around.

——To my surprise, there was a girl I knew very well in the living room.

That girl greeted me with a smile and said, ‘Welcome back.’

I first doubted my own eyes. Then I doubted my own head.

I rubbed my own eyes and smacked my own head.

That’s how unimaginable the scene was: an unbelievable person sitting on a chair in the living room.

And then, I looked at her again——

“Welcome back.”

She waved her hand cutely at me and greeted me again with another smile.

I don’t know what’s going on, but she’s really cute…

Well, whatever, my eyes and head seem fine.

If my body is okay, then this situation is unmistakably real..it’s an unbelievable reality.

In the living room, a scruffy-looking middle-aged man and a baby-faced woman are devouring the top-grade sushi placed in the middle of the table.

Well, these two silly people are my parents, so there’s no issue whatsoever, and it doesn’t matter right now.

The problem is the woman sitting across from them.

Why is she here in my house? She just rejected me today!

She’s staring at me intently now, giving me a cheerful smile.

What’s happening? Why is Touka Himegi in my house???

No, the problem isn’t just that she’s in my house.

What’s with her outfit? Why is Himegi-san dressed in a wedding gown?

Yes, her appearance unmistakably resembles that of a bride. Pure white, a sparkling snow-white dress.

As far as I know, a wedding gown is not the kind of casual wear you put on at home.

…No, a wedding gown is meant to be worn for a wedding ceremony.

I see, the owner of the heels at the entrance must be Himegi-san’s…

Overwhelmed by this situation, I blinked my eyes and stood dumbfounded.

I tried to stay composed, but I couldn’t help it—this scene seemed like a dream.

“Ah, uhh?”

Like a puppet with its strings cut, I plopped down onto the floor with a thud.

I had what one would call a ‘my legs gave out’ moment.

“——Huh? H-Ha-kun, a-are you okay?”

Touka Himegi, dressed in a wedding gown, looked surprised as she saw me collapse.

Then she stood up from her chair and rushed over to where I was.

With trembling hands, I raised my finger and pointed at her face. And then——

“Hawawawa——” then I let out a silly-sounding voice.

Beep- beep- beep-

At the same time, an alarm rang out from my pants pocket.

It was the alarm I had set an hour and five minutes ago. It seemed to me as if that sound were representing my current situation.

Either way, that prediction had undoubtedly hit the mark.

Oddly enough, just as that Old woman had said, an hour and eight minutes later, I’ll momentarily speechless and let out a foolish voice.

I regretted it very much. I should have asked the Old woman to tell me the future developments.

To lose my balance and fall on my back in front of someone I like…I am truly a pathetic guy.

…Ahh, if only I could go back, I wish I could return to five minutes ago——

 

“Ha-kun, have you calmed down a little?”

“….”

“Heyy Ha-kun, can you hear me?”

I managed to sit down on an empty chair under my own power after regaining my composure.

I appreciated her concern, but unfortunately, there was no way I could calm down.

I tried my best to sort out the situation, but I ended up even more confused.

Sitting next to me, Touka Himegi peered at me with a worried expression on her face.

“I understand that you’re confused, but I think you’ll be somewhat convinced if you listen to what I have to say.”

“…Convinced?”

There are a mountain of things I’m curious about. Will she explain all of them from now on?

I repeatedly confirmed her appearance from head to toe. Just as I thought, it’s a wedding dress, right?

To me, it unmistakably looks like a wedding dress.

Since her shoulders and back are exposed, I can clearly see her beautiful collarbone and graceful neck.

Also, her chest is very large, as I thought. Even when she was wearing a school uniform, she couldn’t hide it.

Seeing her ample cleavage, I reconfirmed that she belongs to the category of girls known as busty.

By the way, I’m not looking at her with any improper intentions.

There’s a reason I can’t help but focus on her chest.

There’s a number on it. The number 『60』 is engraved on her right breast.

Is that some kind of tattoo? Even if it were, what could that number mean?

Is it perhaps her breast size? Does 『60』 indicate her bust measurement?

No matter how I look at it, I think Himegi-san’s bust size is well over 90 centimeters.

Maybe it’s not centimeters but inches?

I forced my gaze away from her chest, really struggled to look away, and then raised my gaze upward, focusing on her finely sculpted face.

“Hmm? Is there something on my face?”

Touka Himegi directed a carefree smile towards me.

Ahh…it’s my favorite expression.

Although she’s usually have a cold expression, this pure smile she occasionally shows is what captivated my heart.

Yes, I wanted to monopolize this smile all to myself.

However, as I looked at her face, I felt a slight sense of discomfort.

Could it be my imagination…?

She’s oddly sensual, or perhaps a bit seductive… Is it because she’s in a wedding dress? Or is it due to a different makeup or hairstyle than usual?

I thought her aura seemed more mature than ever before.

Though she already looks more mature than her peers, now she seems like a mature woman.

“Just to confirm one thing.”

“What is it?”

“…This isn’t some kind of prank or surprise, is it?”

I considered various possibilities, but this question seemed to be the most appropriate.

My parents, who were enjoying their sushi, Kanako, along with that fortune-telling Old woman and Himegi-san, all conspired together to surprise me with an elaborate prank.

If that’s the purpose, then they’ve certainly achieved it. But why?

I’m getting impatient for them to reveal the joke.

“Well, I’m sure you’re surprised, but it’s not a prank or anything like that.”

“Then why are you in my house? Why the wedding dress? Also you two, don’t just calmly eat sushi like nothing’s happening! This is an emergency, okay? There’s a woman in a wedding dress sitting in our house, sipping green tea with a serene face! Aren’t you supposed to react like an adult? Also, that salmon roe, sea urchin, and fatty tuna are mine!”

How can my parents eat sushi so leisurely? Unbelievable.

“Calm down, Ha-kun. I’ll explain everything step by step.”

“Step by step?”

“First of all, I am——a time traveler.”

“——Yes?”

“You see, I am not the Touka Himegi you know, but rather, I am Touka Himegi from six years in the future. I’m a future person.”

What is this person saying? Touka Himegi from six years in the future?

I don’t understand…I can’t grasp anything at all.

I hope I misheard.

“I admit, you do appear more mature than the Himegi-san I know, but to call yourself a future person… It’s just too absurd.”

However, there’s one thing I am certain of. She is undoubtedly Touka Himegi herself.

I can’t explain it well, but I’ve been watching Himegi-san for a long time, so I can tell.

The woman in front of me isn’t a look-alike or anything like that; she is the genuine Touka Himegi.

“Hmph, I knew you wouldn’t believe me! …well, it’s to be expected. Everything is as it should be.”

She tries to take something out of her designer bag.

“Surely you’re not going to tell me that bag is evidence from the future, right?”

“Rest assured. I’m going to show you something more convincing right now.”

And with that, Himegi-san proudly takes out a red smartphone from her bag.

“If you look at this, I think you’ll be convinced that I came from the future. In fact, this smartphone is evidence that I’m from the future. After all, this device is a new model of smartphone that doesn’t exist in the present time.”

She handed me the red smartphone, and I accepted it.

This is a smartphone from the future? It’s just a regular smartphone…

“I see…so this is a smartphone being sold in the future…”

“Ah! That expression clearly shows you still don’t believe it. Then I’ll show you the video inside. If you see that, even someone as stubborn as you should believe me!”

She takes the smartphone from my hand and starts doing something with it.

“See, you should be convinced after watching this.”

Then she holds the smartphone up to my face.

I focus on the screen, and the video seems to be taken in a luxury hotel somewhere.

“Hmm? Mom and Dad and Himegi-san?”

The smartphone screen displayed my parents, a boy and a girl who appeared to be around five years old, and Himegi-san in a wedding dress of the same design as the current one, all in what looked like a luxurious hotel.

I glance at my father and mother, who are eating sushi and nikujaga for a moment.

“Mom and Dad are oddly dressed up in fashionable tailcoats and black kimonos…”

The two in the screen appeared a little older.

Yeah, Mom had gained a little weight, and Dad’s forehead had gotten wider.

“Wait, who are the children waving while saying ‘Big brother, do your best!’? Did we have such young cousins or something?”

“—Those children are your twin sister and brother.”

“What? My twin sister and brother?”

What is this person talking about? I’ve been an only child since I was born.

There’s no such thing as a younger sister or brother in this world.

Unable to grasp the situation, I gaze at my silent parents and ask for an explanation.

Then, my father places his chopsticks down and speaks for the first time.

“Well, you see… Hakuba, you’re going to be a big brother soon.”

“Huh?”

“Ha-chan, Mommy’s tummy has two babies inside.”

My mother lovingly touches her belly with a gentle expression.

I’ve only noticed it now, but my mother’s belly has grown larger.

So, that’s what it is. Then, I’ll become a big brother soon…

“I see… Well, um, congratulations to both of you.”

” “Thank you!” “

And with that, I stand up.

” ” “——Ahahaha! ! !” ” “

Everyone bursts into a good laugh. Oh man, this is such a joyous occasion.

I never thought I’d become a big brother at sixteen.

Well, it’s probably a lie though. I bet they just put something in her belly…

“Sigh… If my future looks like that video, I’m sure I’ll be really happy.”

I plop down on the seat and gaze at the smartphone screen with a complicated expression.

From what I gather from the video, it seems that we were invited to Himegi’s wedding.

Even in the setting of a video, it’s genuinely heartwarming to know that I’ll still be connected with her six years from now.

Honestly, I had given up today after she rejected me.

After graduation, we’d each walk different paths and never cross each other’s paths again.

It’s sad, but that’s what I had imagined.

Yet my family and I were invited to Himegi-san’s wedding in the video. Even if it’s a fabricated video, it makes me happy.

If I’m in a position to celebrate Himegi-san becoming a bride, it’s genuinely an honor.

If such a future exists, I hope it comes true.

 

“…We’re still friends in the future, huh? At least, in the context of this video…”

Even though I’m happy for her, my heart still aches painfully. Yes, conflicting emotions have sprouted in my heart.

This girl with such an innocent smile on her face will belong to someone else…

Even if it’s just a setup for a video, I still feel complicated about it.

I should have understood that I had to give up on her when she rejected me today… but a part of me doesn’t want to.

And even though I knew it was just my own ego, I didn’t want to lie to myself.

“…Friends….according to the video… you still don’t believe it, huh?”

“Anyway, using my parents as well, what’s the purpose behind this grand scheme?”

It feels like they’re going overboard if all they’re doing is scaring me.

Who on earth came up with this idea?

“Sigh… I expected that kind of response, but it’s still a bit frustrating when you don’t believe me.”

“By the way, who’s the one who filmed this smartphone video?”

“My younger sister. But more importantly, have you understood that this video is my wedding?”

“Yes. It’s at some luxury hotel, huh?”

“Exactly. On April 14th, six years from now, we rented out a well-known hotel and had a grand wedding.”

“…Congratulations…”

“Well, Ha-kun isn’t exactly the side to offer congratulations…”

“Huh? I’m not the side to offer congratulations?”

What does that mean?

“Well, never mind. Let’s move on with the conversation. So, on this date six years from now, I’m getting married. And since it’s a wedding, it’s only natural that I have a partner. Are you with me so far?”

“Are you talking about the groom?”

“Exactly. By the way, what kind of person do you think my groom is?”

“I don’t know. Maybe a super wealthy heir or a handsome actor?”

“My husband isn’t an heir, and he’s not particularly handsome either. If anything, he’s a cute guy who falls into the ‘pitiful’ category.”

“Huh? ‘pitiful’ category?”

“Anyway, I’ll rewind this video a bit. And then, I want you to take a look at my husband.”

Himegi-san operates the red smartphone with a serious expression.

“Let’s start from around here… Ha-kun, try not to get flustered again.”

“You’re pretty demanding. Well, I won’t be surprised anymore. I mean, there’s nothing left to be surprised about.”

A magician thrives on surprising their audience. And yet, I’ve been surprised one after another today.

Here, as a magician, I’ll maintain my composure and remain unfazed, no matter what happens!

“You’re setting up various flags, aren’t you? Well, never mind. Stay calm and watch this footage.”

Himegi-san hands me the smartphone.

I accept the smartphone and gaze at the screen intently.

The scene transitions, and it changes to what seems like a chapel.

A white aisle with a large cross.

There, a foreign man dressed as a pastor with glasses, not Japanese, holding a thick book in his hand, standing with a very composed expression.

From the chapel, the soothing sound of a pipe organ resonates, and the familiar ‘Bridal Chorus’ by Wagner, heard by everyone at least once, is playing.

Then, the large wooden doors open, and from there, an unfamiliar handsome middle-aged man and Himegi-san in a wedding dress appear.

This is it, right? The situation of walking down the aisle with a parent.

I was slightly taken aback. I never expected to be shown such an elaborate fake video…

“It’s actually looks like a real wedding…”

“That’s because it is real…”

Pressing her temple, Himegi-san makes a frustrated expression. And once again, I focus on the smartphone.

On the screen, a pure white Himegi-san in a wedding dress stands next to a man in an equally white tuxedo.

Unfortunately, the screen only shows the back of the groom. So, I can’t see the groom’s face clearly.

But his height doesn’t seem to be particularly tall.

Still, the unfolding scenario is easily imaginable.

Probably, the groom and bride will exchange vows of eternal love before the pastor next. And as I imagined, the groom and bride, Himegi-san, exchange vows of eternal love before the pastor.

“This way, I can’t figure out who the groom is.”

I don’t know, but his back and head look familiar to me.

The voice, too, seems somewhat familiar… Where have I heard it?

Then, the exchange of vows concludes.

Even though I’ve never been to a wedding, I know what happens next.

Ahh, the ring exchange and the vow-sealed kiss.

The groom and bride would kiss in front of everyone, and everyone would applaud to bless the couple. That’s how it should go.

The pastor, uttering some profound words, would encourage the ring exchange between the two.

“——Hmm?”

Wait? This profile of the groom… I’ve seen this face somewhere. Huh, where did I see it?

I try hard to trace back through my memories.

Oh, that delicate build, chestnut-brown hair, and blue eyes.

An androgynous youthful face, with a slightly unreliable vibe…

“Wait, is it me? No way, could it be…is this me!?”

“What? Just now? You just found out now?”

To my disbelief, the groom in the screen was me!

In the video, I nervously placed a ring onto her slim and beautiful left ring finger.

I have no memory of experiencing an event like this. I often forget things, but surely if I had experienced something like this, I wouldn’t forget it even if I wanted to.

In other words, this is an event I don’t remember.

It seems that the unbelievably strange story she was telling wasn’t just a tall tale but the truth.

I gazed at her before me. She was looking at me with a triumphant expression.

Ah, this expression is unfamiliar to me. It seems to be a natural face that she only shows to someone special.

It may sound strange coming from me, but it looked quite picturesque.

It’s strange…I’ve never even touched the hand of someone of the opposite sex, let alone kissed them. And yet, there I am, placing my hand on her face and kissing her in a practiced manner.

It felt like a scene from a movie.

Ah, but I can’t put into words the feelings that are welling up.

Is this a dream? If it is, I wish I would wake up soon… It’s too embarrassing to continue watching.

With that thought, I stopped the video.

” ——Wait! The best part is coming!”

“Uh, no, I’m done now. My head will start hurting if I continue…”

No, I can’t get that kissing scene out of my head…

“With this, have you understood who my husband is?”

No words came out.

I was trying to speak, but my throat was trembling and no words emerged.

I see, if she’s my bride, then it’s not strange for her to be in this house.

Then she fluttered her wedding dress, did a little twirl, and showed me the sparkling ring to me.

“I am your destined victorious bride!”
