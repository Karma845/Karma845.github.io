Electronic Version Short Story: Yuki's Birthday Gift

During our lunch break on the rooftop after practicing our lines, we sat down for our usual meal.

"I wanted to talk to you about something, Rena."

"What is it?"

"I'm curious about what kind of birthday present women would appreciate."

As soon as I said that, Rena dropped the chopsticks she was holding.

Although they rolled onto the floor, she didn't bother picking them up and leaned in closer to me.

"W-what do you mean? Birthday present? For a woman?"

"Um, what's wrong, Rena?"

"Hmm... I see. Well, I guess it's normal for someone like Kaito-kun to have some close female friends."

"Female friends? Oh, no, it's not like that."

It seemed like I had unintentionally caused a misunderstanding. I hurriedly clarified.

"Sorry, I might have phrased it poorly. Actually, Yuki-san's birthday is next Monday, and I'm struggling to decide what to get her."

"Huh? Yuki-san?"

Rena suddenly toned down, exhaling as if she had been relieved.

"I see. Kaito-kun, you're taking care of your manager like that."

"I really appreciate everything Yuki-san has done for me. Last year, I didn't have much income, so I could only afford cheap things like a pen case. But this year, thanks to her, I've earned some decent money. I want to show my gratitude by getting her something nice."

I kept slipping in auditions and faced numerous setbacks, but Yuki-san never abandoned me; she always encouraged me. It's been hard for me to express my gratitude directly, so this seemed like the perfect opportunity.

"But, Kaito-kun, considering your long history with Yuki-san, shouldn't you already know her preferences and what she desires?"

"The thing is, Yuki-san doesn't reveal much about her private life. Even though we've been friends for over three years, when it comes to birthday gifts, I'm completely clueless about what would make her happy."

"I see. That's a challenge..."

Rena rested her chin on her hand, pondering the situation.

"Well, what about you, Rena? What would make you happy as a gift?"

"I'd be happy with anything Kaito-kun gives me."

As I asked, Rena responded promptly. Her straightforward words made my cheeks warm, but she continued.

"Though if I had to choose... something that lasts, rather than consumables. It feels a bit sad if a present disappears once you've used it, right?"

"Something that lasts, huh?"

Certainly, I wouldn't want consumables if they were a gift from Rena. They'd feel too precious to use.

But what about Yuki-san? We weren't as close as Rena and I, not childhood friends. Despite being quite open with each other, our relationship was more like actor and manager.

As I began to ponder again, Rena clapped her hands as if she had a great idea.

"That's right; you have a much better person to consult with than me."

"Huh? Who?"

"My manager, Karin-san. There are sisters, so she should know what Yuki-san wants, right?"

"Oh, you're right! But I don't have Karin-san's contact information..."

"I'll take care of that for you. Just wait a moment. I'll ask if she's available."

Rena took out her phone and confidently started typing a message. After a short exchange, she smiled at me.

"Yeah, it's fine to call her now. Should I connect the call?"

"Thank you, please!"

As she dialed the number, Rena switched her phone to speaker mode and placed it between us. Karin-san answered after just one ring.

"Hello? Is something the matter, Mizusawa-san?"

"Oh, Karin-san? Actually, Kaito-kun has something he wants to consult with you about. He's right next to me."

"Amano-san? What is it?"

"Sorry, Karin-san. There's something I'd like to ask... um, well, you know, Yuki-san's birthday is next week."

"Yes, that's correct. You're aware of it."

"So, I'm planning to get her a nice present this year, but I'm not sure what would make her happy. Any suggestions?"

When I casually posed the question, there was a brief pause on the other end of the line. It seemed like Karin-san was taking a moment to think.

Eventually, she spoke up thoughtfully.

"Well... honestly, I think just the sentiment alone would make my sister happy. But I understand you want to get her something she'll truly appreciate."

"Yes, that's right."

"Since my sister isn't particularly interested in fashion, she might not be too thrilled with accessories or cosmetics. She tends to appreciate practical gifts like boxes of nutritional drinks, but as a present, it might seem a bit dull..."

Having said that, Karin-san paused for a moment, then seemed to recall something.

"Come to think of it, she mentioned something she's been undecided about buying recently."

"Oh, really?"

"Yes. She loves collecting character goods, and there's a new one that came out recently."

Character goods?

I was a bit surprised. I've never seen Yuki-san with something like that, but if Karin-san says so, it must be true. 

"I'll send you the link to the product. She mentioned just recently that she'd have to give up buying it this month due to a tight budget, so there shouldn't be any overlap."

"Thank you so much, Karin-san! I appreciate it!"

"Not a problem. Well then, let's meet again on set sometime."

After the call ended, Rena shared the link with me. As we both opened the link, we couldn't help but exchange glances at the image of the product.

"Hmm, is this cute?"

"Oh, so you also think it's a bit strange?"

"Rather than cute, it's more eerie... it has a weird face."

It was a character with fish and legs – something quite peculiar that we had never seen before. Its large eyes and slightly unsettling expression made it stand out. The product was a mega-sized plush toy that seemed just big enough to be cradled in both arms.

"I wonder if Yuki-san really collects these..."

"But Karin-san doesn't joke about these things. I don't think she'd lie."

"That's true... but it's hard to imagine."

I tried to picture Yuki-san happily hugging this plush toy, but I couldn't form a clear image.

Still, Karin-san provided the information, and there was no reason not to take advantage of it.

"I'll go ahead and buy this."

"Yeah, I think it's a good choice. Personally, I might have a mixed reaction, though..."

Despite feeling a hint of uncertainty in Rena's words, I went ahead and purchased the relatively expensive plush toy as a birthday present.

*

And so, the day of Yuki-san birthday arrived.

Yuki went about her day as usual, efficiently handling work. After the shoot concluded, she came to call us from the dressing room.

"Great job, both of you. Mizusawa-san, are you ready to head home too?"

"Oh, wait, Yuki-san!"

"What's up, Amano-kun?"

"Here, please take this."

I handed her a prepared bag. Yuki-san blinked in surprise as she accepted it.

"It's a birthday present. I'm not sure if you'll like it, but please take it."

"Oh, wow! Thanks, Amano-kun! Can I open it right here?"

"Yes, go ahead."

"Okay, let's see... Wow!"

As Yuki-san pulled out the contents, she let out a loud cheer, unlike anything I had heard before.

Excitedly holding the plush toy, she turned towards me.

"W-What's going on, Amano-kun? This is Hopper's newly released merchandise for this month!"

"Well... when I consulted with Karin-san, she mentioned that you wanted this."

"Really? Oh, I really wanted this! But I've been spending quite a bit this month, and if I bought this too, I would have been in quite a tight spot... Thank you, Amano-kun!"

Her reaction exceeded my expectations. I was completely taken aback by how well it was received.

"But, Yuki-san, you don't usually wear this kind of merchandise, right?"

"Oh, yeah. Karin-san keeps pestering me about it. She says it's tacky and insists I shouldn't put it on as a strap or use it as my wallpaper. She nags me about it so much it's like having a broken record. It's really annoying, you know? Even though it's so cute. Right, Amano-kun? Mizusawa-san?"

"Uh, yeah..."

After giving vague responses, Rena and I exchanged glances.

Yuki-san, seemingly unbothered by our reactions, held the plush toy tightly, wearing a blissful expression.

"Wow, I'm so happy..."

It was a side of Yuki-san that I had never seen before—a moment that made me unconsciously loosen my lips into a smile.
