Epilogue: Stolen Heart

In the end ── carrying Tsuyu on his back, by the time he walked her home, the sun had completely set.

But anyway, he made it to her house.

Tsuyu’s house, and also Himawari’s house ── the Shishido residence, where he had been several times before.

「With your permission.」

It seems that no other family members have returned home, and the lights are off.

After having Tsuyu unlock the door, Kamome enters the house, and carries her all the way to her room on the second floor.

「Fuu… Okay, we’ve arrived.」

「Thank you.」

He seats Tsuyu on the bed in her room, and Kamome takes a breather.

「You must be tired too, Kamome. Why don’t you sit down?」

With that, Tsuyu pats the space next to her on the bed.

「Ah, sure.」

Kamome sat down next to her, looking a little puzzled.

「…I brought you all the way here without stopping, but I really wonder if this was the right thing to do.」

Without confirming Tsuyu’s intentions, he had taken an action in a way that would have a significant impact on her life.

The realization of that fact makes Kamome feel uneasy.

「It’s fine. Don’t worry about it.」

Tsuyu says to Kamome.

While Kamone’s expression is clouded, her face ── since their time by the riverbank, had been nothing but bright.

「Kamome, you saved me. And also…」

There.

Tsuyu took the hand of Kamome who was sitting next to her.

「When I ran hand in hand with Kamome… for the first time in a long time, I was able to run with all my might. Somehow, it felt good. Thank you, truly.」

「Tsuyu…」

Hearing Tsuyu’s words, Kamome felt reassured.

That his judgment was not wrong.

「Tsuyu. I’m sure there’s a possibility that you’ll be harassed or harmed by that person again in the future. If he does anything to you again, I want you to consult me anytime. I’ll protect you, Tsuyu.」

「…Yeah.」

…The two of them fall silent.

Inside the house filled with silence ── Tsuyu’s room filled with silence.

In an almost crushing silence, the two look at each other.

All they can hear is each other’s breath.

Kamome feels Tsuyu’s gaze, almost hotly.

Tsuyu places her hand on Kamome’s chest.

Just like that, she brings her face closer.

「…Tsuyu.」

Taking the hand that Tsuyu placed on his chest, Kamome says.

「…I saved Tsuyu because I wanted to. And I want to protect you from now on. I think of Tsuyu as someone important to me…」

His feelings of love for Tsuyu have not disappeared.

He understood that well.

But right now, he’s not qualified to clearly voice that.

「But we can’t go any further.」

Hearing those words, the heat disappeared from Tsuyu’s expression.

Her face becomes one of shock, as if she’d been pushed off a cliff.

「…Kamome, I…」

Tsuyu’s intent comes through.

Even Kamome, as insensitive as he can be, understands that from the heat emanating from her body, her expression, all of it, she is wanting Kamome.

「…Just for now is fine.」

Tsuyu pleads, as if clinging to Kamome.

「Please, just kiss me one more time.」

「…Sorry. I can’t… betray Himawari… I can’t betray her anymore.」

「……」

In the dim light, Tsuyu looked down.

He can’t see what kind of expression she has on her face now.

It was at that time.

「I’m home.」

The sound of the front door opening.

And then, a voice like ringing bells resounded in the house.

「Huh? No one’s here?」

──It’s Himawari.

She has come home.

That’s right, he has to explain to Himawari.

Why he’s at home with Tsuyu.

Kamome stands up.

In an instant ── Tsuyu pulled Kamome’s arm as he was about to head towards Himawari.

She lies on Kamome, who falls on her back on her bed──

A kiss.

Her tongue slips in, invading his mouth.

The heat, the touch, the scent, the heartbeat, the emotions, everything about her is transmitted.

Their lips do not part.

Their saliva mixes together.

He is forced to swallow the sweet poison that has accumulated in his mouth.

「Nee… Kamome.」

Tsuyu uttered with her lips still pressed to his.

Conversing while kissing.

Her voice, her breath, delivers a shock as if striking directly at his brain.

「If you tell me I haven’t changed since then, don’t you still love me?」

A sweet numbness shakes his brainstem.

「I love Kamome. I can’t hold back anymore.」

「Tsu, yu…」

「I love Kamome. I love Kamome. I love Kamome… Even though I should support your relationship with Himawari, I can’t do that anymore. What should I do? Kamome.」

Skin adheres to skin.

Body clings to body.

Breath melds together.

A voice echoes and resounds within his body.

「I don’t know what I should do. It’s absolutely wrong to steal you from Himawari… but I want to. Kamome, do you hate me like this?」

A painful voice.

Tears spilling from the corners of her eyes dampen Kamome’s cheeks as well.

With a love so heavy it hurts, Tsuyu asks.

「Do I have the right to be liked by Kamome, as I am now?」

『There’s no need for Tsuyu to force herself to change for the sake of those who torment you.』

『You just need to stick to what you have resolved to be.』

……

Ahh, that’s right.

I remember.

I certainly told her that.

To the man who made her bear a desire she shouldn’t wish for, who tormented her.

Tsuyu is trying to follow through with what she has determined without bending herself.

Yes, this is all.

A curse I’ve placed on her.

From now on, Ooshima Kamome will come to realize.

What it means to “steal a heart”, and how much responsibility it entails.
