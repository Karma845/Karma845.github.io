Chapter 9 - Mea-san, Let's go back home together!

“Here it is, ku-kun…”

Renjiyo-san guided me to a corner of a residential area, opposite of the railroad tracks from my house and the room I live in, and turning left on the way to the junior high school.

It was a very large Japanese-style mansion.

“I-it’s a big house… It’s so different from ours.”

“Me-chan is a princess after all… She asked me to keep it a secret though…”

Hahaha, She laughed with a wry face.

….Yes, indeed, it’s a ‘secret’ that can’t be kept secret for long. That’s how it is.

That’s why I have to bring Mea-san back from here first.

“Fu-chan and Sumitaro didn’t follow us, did they?”

“Yeah …Sumitaro, that’s how he is.”

He himself told me ‘show her the man’, that’s why he wouldn’t bother to follow me.

On the other hand, I had a feeling that Fuuga would be more concerned about how things would turn out and would be willing to follow me…

“Fuuga… I wonder if she’s embarrassed, she noticed herself pretending to be a gal.”

That jealousy was probably what that was about. I know it wasn’t romantic feelings though.

“Hmm, that’s a… woman’s instinct, isn’t it?”

“Uh-huh.”

“…”

“Renjiyou-san?”

“What can I say, It’s a secret♪”

She laughed involuntarily.

Well, it’s not something I should think about now. I have more important things to deal with.

I straightened my back again and glared at the nameplate “Chitose”

“…Mea-san, she should be home by now.”

“Be a little courageous.”

“Yeah… Well then, I’m going in.”

“Yeah… I don’t think I deserve to say this, but, bring her back! Ku-kun!”

“Yeah, thank you. Now then..”

I pressed the intercom.

“Is it Kuuya-kun?”

The reply was immediate. It was Mea-san’s father.

“Thanks for the other day. So, father, Mea-san is…”

“She says she wants to pretend she never had that conversation.”

“That’s…”

“I would like to ask you to leave her alone here. I will contact Touya later.”

….

“I was cut off!”

I was refused from the main gate. A-are you that hurt, Mea-san…!

No, or was the one hurt her dad? He’s the one who acted like he was against this, that he’d never let her daughter out of his hands again…

“…”

“Wh-what are you gonna do now, Ku-kun?”

“…I won’t back down until I talk to her!”

I have to play hardball! I opened the front gate and stepped into the premises.

As soon as I did that,

clank clank clank!

“…What?”

Something rang throughout the mansion. It was not only that.

Tsuuuuuu!

“Owaa!?”

I narrowly dodged a flying shuriken.

But I can’t see the person who threw it at me. In other words, this is a trap for intruders…?

I had forgotten that Mea-san is the princess of a ninja family. So, if you think about it, her parents’ house is a ninja mansion!

“What, what, what?! What is this?! Shuriken!?”

“That part’s a secret! Anyway, if that’s the case…”

I tried my best to remember what my father had taught me.

It would be almost impossible to break in from the front.

Then we must go around from the back, where the security seems to be thin!

“See you later, Rejiyou-san! Pray for my safety.”

“O-okay…”

I left Renjiyou-san and went to the back of the building to get to the wall!

“Ow, that hurts!”

The fence was carefully covered with spiky protrusions.

Of course, if it were an entry route, they would have set traps even in the back of the house! This area is indeed a ninja mansion!

However, if you are a ninja, THEN! I am an aspiring intelligence officer. Infiltration is our specialty, so to speak.

I would have loved to say that I was a ninja, but unfortunately, that was not the case.

I’m the son of a secret agent, but I don’t have any special tools… and I’m not formally trained to be a secret agent. I’m just a kid who pretends to be a secret agent, I only took occasional lessons from my dad.

“…”

But that doesn’t mean I’m gonna give up!

“Mea-san!!”

I call out my wife’s name as I once again mount the fence.

The spikes were stinging my hands as hard as it could, but… so what! I don’t care!

I forcefully lifted myself up and climbed on top of the fence. Then I called out again.

“Mea-san! It’s me! I’m here to get you back!”

Then, I saw a figure quickly pass by one of the windows on the second floor.

Ah, it’s Mea-san, I instinctively guessed, and raised my voice even higher.

“It’s not true that you are unqualified to be my wife, Mea-san! I rather think you are so very cute and adorable when you are shy! Just like a few moments ago…!”

My face was hot. But I have to say it. If I want Mea-san back!

“That’s why Mea-san… Come back home together with me!!”

Urrruuuuuu

“…What?”

Boom!

“Ouwaaa!?”

Then a smoke ball flew out of the air and exploded under my feet.

I felt a slight burning pain, and I lost my balance and almost fell.

“Tch..!”

If I’m going to fall, I’m going to fall on the property!

Thud!

I twisted my body forcefully and fell over the wall into a Japanese garden-style yard.

“Ahhhhh!! It hurts!!…. But! I have to be patient! I’m a man!”

I grit my teeth and stood up.

Smoke was still billowing around me.

But then─

“Ehh…?”

Suddenly, one of the sliding doors facing the garden opened.

“────!”

I don’t know, but it was better than staying in the yard!

I thought, and jumped in before the smoke cleared.

But it was a secret closet-like place with no way out.

“Eh… what the h**l, this is…”

It’s not a normal house.

That means…!

I pushed the walls on three sides, except for the sliding door.

Then, one corner! As soon as I pushed it, it spun around and opened.

A hidden door…!

On the other side was a staircase.

I knew that since I have come this far, there was no time to get confused.

I pushed the hidden door and ran up the stairs.

Then…

“Whhhaa!!?”

Fiich!! Suddenly, the staircase transformed into a slope.

I lost my foothold and slid down.

“Uwaaaaaaaa!!!!”

What’s with the staircase! If it leads to the second floor, I’ll climb it!

I used my hands as well as my feet to hold my ground, and I pushed my way up the stairs.

There must be Mea-san waiting for me! If that’s the case… there’s no way I’m falling!

“MEAAAAAAA-SAAAANNNN!”

I climbed up!

At the end of it was a short corridor and a Japanese sliding door!

Without hesitation, I charged forward and opened the sliding door with great force.

“Mea-san!”

“~──”

I was right

Mea-san was there.

The room has a calm Japanese-style that was befitting of the mansion’s exterior.

As if leaning against a Japanese wardrobe placed there, Mea-san quickly turned her head away from me.

“Huhh! Huhh!! Mea-san… You can hear me right…?”

“…”

“By any chance, Mea-san… you were the one who opened the …door for me?

“…”

“Anyway… huh, I’m here for you, Mea-san”

“…..”

I was out of breath. I didn’t even pay attention to my aching limbs.

“Let’s go home!”

I told her clearly.

“I know you’re embarrassed, but I think you’re adorable!”

“!…”

“It’s true that you may not have been able to do what Renjiyou-san and Fuuga did today! But even so, my wife is still Mea-san!”

Mea-san did not answer. She kept her face turned away.

But… that doesn’t mean I can’t come all the way down here and be a bit of a jerk!

“I want to continue to live with you, Mea-san! I want to understand you more and more!”

“Ku… Kuuya-san…”

Finally, A whispered voice.

Just by getting even a small response, my whole body is filled with strength.

This is the power of a man in times of need.

I’m not the kind of guy who is usually pretentious. I’m not a cool guy at all…

But…! It doesn’t matter!

“Mea-san, turn around and look at me!”

“…!”

“Now, all you have to do is take a close look at me! It’s okay to do it little by little … Let’s go together, Mea-san !!”

“…~”

Fuushh~

Mea-san looked back at me as she stood up, her hair almost floating in the air.

Her face was already bright red.

But just as I urged her to do, she looked straight at me…

“I-It’s embashishing, Kuuya-san…”

I nodded my head, conscious of the heat in my face.

“Yeah, I’m embarrassed too!”

“….Fufufuu~~”

“Hahah…”

“…Kuuya-san!”

Hug!

“~~”

Mea-san leaped onto my chest.

I put all my strength into my legs and caught her as best as I could.

The sweet and sour smell. The soft touch. The warm temperature.

“I’m very sorry…!”

I took those words – and her – to heart properly.

“Splendid.”

And there was,

“…Heh?”

Before we know it, an odd-looking woman was standing right beside us.

She was surprisingly beautiful… a woman who looked a lot like Mea-san.

I saw her at the entrance ceremony… She’s Mea-san’s mother, right?

“My son-in-law has shown me such great determination. You must also let go of your daughter, my dear.”

“…”

The father, with a bitter look on his face, also entered the room.

“I knew it was you who threw the smoke ball. Thanks to that, we can no longer tear them apart.”

“You also made a bad move by dropping the staircase. But it didn’t seem to work on my son-in-law.”

I’m not sure… but… he’s saying that Mea-san’s mom was trying to thwart Mea-san’s dad who was trying to thwart my intrusion?

“Nice to meet you, Kuuya-san. I’m Mea’s mother.”

“Ahh, Y-yes, Nice to meet you too, I’m Nonomiya Kuuya…!”

“Once my daughter has made the decision to marry, I was not going to let her cross the threshold of this house… again, even if she cried. That’s what I should do as the mother of my daughter, the Ninja Princess.”

“But…”

“You must keep your mouth shut. You also made a promise to Touya-san, didn’t you? And now you try to change it because your daughter whined… Yet you still call yourself the head of the Chitose family, you are pathetic.”

“…”

“Now, Mea.”

“Y… yeah… Mom…”

“This is what it means to be married. You go hand in hand with the man you have decided to marry. No matter how embarrassed you are…”

The mother looked at me and smiled.

“…Without fail, Kuuya-san, he reached out to you no matter what… Even I understood how much he cares for you, don’t tell me you don’t understand that.”

“…Yes. Yes, Mother.”

And then Mea-san sobbed.

“…Kuuya-san.”

She looked up at me while she was still in my arms.

I was aware of it all over again. I realized it.

All this time, I had thought that Mea-san was more mature than I am…

I still thought that I was still, in fact, a kid…

Just a little. Even if it’s only a couple of centimeters.

I’m taller than Mea-san…

I felt my whole body being filled with a warm feeling again.

“Yeah… Mea-san.”

“Mea is really sorry… Mea is still like this, but…”

“Yeah… yeah…”

“Can Mea be your wife…?”

“Of course!”

I replied immediately.

Not only that – I hugged her tightly with all the strength I had in my body.

From me. For the first time.

“Ah…”

Mea-san let out a warm exhale and gentle breath…

Then she gently intertwined her fingers with my hand.

No…

We intertwined our fingers with each other’s, and then she took hold of my hand, our hand on top of each other.

It was how lovers hold hands.

“I… I did it…”

“Mea-san…”

“Fufufu~”

“…Hahhahaha”

“My dear Husband!”
