Heracles 7


 


Haise, Geist, and Apollon approach Sasha and Hijiri, who stand facing each other.


"Both sides seem ready."


"Of course!!"


"Yes, Geist-san."


"Mmm-hmm. Such lively girls♪ So cute~ I remember when I was like that too~"


Apollon sways her hips seductively.


Haise, keeping a slight distance, glances between Sasha and Hijiri.


Then, Hijiri points her fist at Haise.


"You're next."


"…Don’t get too cocky. Keep your eyes forward."


Sasha’s eyes grow cold and deep.


Hijiri notices and turns back to Sasha.


Geist clears his throat and addresses them.


"Now, the duel begins. The witnesses are myself, Haise, and Apollon. If either of you admits defeat, if two of us deem the fight untenable, or if one of you dies before we can stop it, the remaining one will be the victor. And rest assured, we won’t allow any interference… Fight with everything you’ve got."


"Ufufun~ If anyone tries to interfere, I’ll give them a nice, warm hug, so don’t worry!!"


With a creaking sound, Apollon’s right arm bulges with muscle, tearing her sleeve and revealing her tightly coiled forearm.


Apollon hides her arm, exclaiming, "Eek, how embarrassing!!" but somehow her sleeve instantly repairs itself. If that arm were to grab you, escape would be impossible.


Sasha and Hijiri nod and take their positions, distancing themselves from each other.


Geist speaks to Haise.


"Haise, give the signal to start."


"…Me?"


"Yes. You can handle that much, can’t you?"


"…Fine, understood."


Geist and the others step back, leaving Haise standing between Sasha and Hijiri.


"Both of you, ready?"


Haise raises his right hand, holding a single-shot starter pistol.


Hijiri positions her right hand forward and her left hand near her chest.


Sasha draws her sword, bringing it to the side of her face, her hand resting on the hilt.


"Then───… begin."


Haise pulls the trigger, and the pistol fires with a sharp crack.


◇◇◇◇◇


As soon as the pistol fires, Sasha and Hijiri dash forward.


Sasha is cloaked in golden energy, while Hijiri remains as she is.


But Haise notices something.


"No way… Sasha’s on par with her!?"


Reynold shouts from a distance.


It’s not just Reynold. Piasora, Robin, and Tycoon are equally stunned.


Geist mutters, "Hoh."


"Draa!!"


A short uppercut from the right.


But Sasha tilts her head slightly and dodges. She steps back just a bit and slashes horizontally───… but Hijiri gently touches the blade and pushes it upward, altering its trajectory.


"!?"


"Dorarararararaaaa!!"


"───Tch!"


A rapid barrage of punches from Hijiri’s right fist alone. At such close range, Sasha can’t dodge.


But Sasha’s golden energy concentrates on her abdomen, blocking Hijiri’s fist.


Then, they both create distance.


"Ouch… What’s with that sparkly stuff?"


"Impressive. You broke through three layers of my [Aura Wall]…"


"Your sword’s fast too. I was worried I couldn’t deflect it with my [Blade Flow]."


"Hmph… Then, here I come!!"


Sasha focuses her golden energy on her legs and propels herself forward.


Even Haise is surprised.


"…That golden light… She can do that now too?"


Sasha’s new technique, [Aura Flow].


Sasha typically cloaks her entire body in golden aura to enhance her physical abilities or unleash sword-based attacks. But… this consumes too much energy, and even at full power, she can only fight for about ten minutes.


So, Sasha trained to focus her aura on specific parts of her body instead.


She’s experimented with partial enhancement before. Realizing that partial enhancement causes less fatigue than full-body enhancement, she honed this skill.


Now, Sasha concentrates 80% of her aura on her abdomen and distributes the remaining 20% across her body.


"Golden Sword, [Light Chain Blade]!!"


"!!"


For a brief moment, she cloaks her entire body in 100% aura and unleashes a flurry of slashes.


Hijiri backsteps to dodge the blade, slapping it down with both hands───… but she fails to fully evade it, and a single slash cuts her cheek, drawing blood.


"Not yet!!"


"Kuh…!?"


Sasha enhances her legs with 70%, her body with 20%, and her arms with 10%.


In an instant, she closes the distance and swings her sword.


Too fast───Piasora, watching this, breaks into a delighted smile.


"Amazing!! Sasha’s truly invincibleeeeeee!!"


"…………What a girl."


"…Cress, you noticed it too, didn’t you?"


"Fufufu, you guys finally see how incredible Sasha is───"


""Wrong.""


Reynold and Cress speak simultaneously.


Piasora pouts.


Tycoon adjusts his glasses, while Robin and Mueane tilt their heads in confusion.


"Can’t you see it?"


"W-What are you talking about? S-Stop acting like you know everything."


"…Good grief."


Tycoon is utterly exasperated.


Even as they converse, Sasha’s relentless assault continues.


◇◇◇◇◇


"…Impressive."


"Sure is~"


Geist and Apollon had already noticed.


Geist speaks with admiration.


"Sasha’s attacks are indeed fearsome. Her mastery of [Aura], one of the abilities of a [Sword Master], has improved significantly. Partial enhancement… Crossfald didn’t reach that level until his late thirties. It’s not just talent; she must’ve put in tremendous effort."


"But but~… It’s Hijiri-chan, right?"


"Indeed."


Geist smiles, deeply impressed.


◇◇◇◇◇


"Incredible…"


Haise mutters to himself, standing apart from Geist, Apollon, and the others.


"What’s incredible?"


"Can’t you see? ───Wait, you!?"


Somehow, Presea is standing next to Haise.


She must’ve snuck up on him. She shows no remorse.


Haise sighs.


"Whatever… You’re not misusing that disappearing ability of yours, are you?"


"Of course not. So… what’s incredible?"


"Can’t you see it?"


"…?"


Hijiri blocks, dodges, and deflects Sasha’s relentless attacks.


Gradually, Sasha’s complexion begins to pale.


Haise explains.


"Sasha, with her full-body enhancement, is evenly matched with Hijiri. Got it? Hijiri is keeping up with Sasha, who’s cloaked in golden aura, using nothing but her raw physical abilities. And… look at her eyes. She’s tracking Sasha’s attacks, dodging them, and looking for openings to counter."


Presea notices it too.


Hijiri, dodging the blade by a hair’s breadth, grins.


"───I can see it now."


"Kuh…"


Then, Hijiri deflects Sasha’s downward slash with her fist and gently places her hand on Sasha’s exposed abdomen.


"───Huh?"


"This is gonna hurt."


The moment Hijiri’s hand leaves Sasha’s abdomen, a tremendous shockwave rips through Sasha’s body.


What’s visible is the ground cracking beneath Hijiri’s foot as she steps in.


The attack is a palm strike. The impact tears through Sasha’s back, sending her flying.


"Guh───…"


She rolls across the ground, quickly regaining her footing, but… a violent wave of nausea overwhelms her, and she vomits.


Not food. What comes out is bright red blood.


Her stomach is damaged, and she’s bleeding internally.


Tears well up from the agony, but Sasha endures.


Hijiri remains in her palm-strike stance.


"Sasha!!"


"Calm down, Piasora. If you get close to Sasha now, it’s a foul!!"


"B-But…"


Reynold grabs Piasora’s shoulder.


He looks pained too, but as he watches Sasha, he says,


"Look. Sasha───she hasn’t given up."


"…Sasha."


Sasha stands up, wiping the blood from her mouth.
