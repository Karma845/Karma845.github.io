To the Holy Cross Adramelech Theocracy


 


"Now, are you ready?"


""Yes""


"Yep"


"Ooh!!"


"Haaaii!!"


Responding to Geist's confirmation were Haise, Sasha, Presea, Hijiri, and Mina.


Their destination is the 'Holy Cross Adramelech Theocracy' to the north. They are heading there as temporary instructors for the adventurer training academy, Adramelech Adventurer School.


However, Haise seems somewhat reluctant as he speaks.


"Um, Geist-san?"


"What is it?"


"I get Sasha and Hijiri, but... why are Presea and Mina coming?"


Yes, for some reason, the two of them were also joining.


Then, Mina grabs Haise's arm and says:


"Ufufu. That's because~..."


"I was hired as an assistant for Sasha and Hijiri. It’s tough to handle the classes alone, you know."


"Mina is my assistant. I have lectures on the adventurer guild too, so having a guild staff member like Mina will be helpful."


"Ahhh!? I was gonna say that!"


Haise pulls his arm away from Mina and replies with a sigh.


"...How long will it take to reach the Holy Cross Adramelech Theocracy?"


"About fifteen days by carriage. We’ll pass through several towns along the way."


"...Um"


"No."


Haise was about to ask if he could go ahead alone. But Geist smiles, placing a hand on Haise's shoulder with a light pat... For some reason, it felt like a weight of several dozen kilograms had been placed on him.


"Alright, let’s head out in the guild carriage. Everyone, get in."


Boarding the adventurer guild’s carriage, Haise and the others set off toward the north.


◇◇◇◇◇


"Kuhaa~"


"Hey, don’t lean on me."


The carriage is a large, covered one, pulled by two horses, making the interior spacious.


However, Hijiri, who fell asleep shortly after departure, began using Haise's shoulder as a pillow, sleeping soundly. Haise pushes Hijiri away and lays her down on a cushion to sleep.


Sitting across from each other, Sasha is seated in front.


When their eyes meet, Sasha speaks.


"Um, want to come over here?"


"No, I’m fine."


It had been years since Haise last rode in the same carriage as Sasha.


Presea is floating several water droplets on her fingertip, shaping them into small fish and birds, amusing herself by making them float around. Mina watches in awe.


"Wow, that’s amazing!"


"Just killing time."


"Hey, hey, Presea, can you make other things too?"


"Sure. Any requests?"


"Then make a goblin!!"


"...Well, alright."


Presea and Mina are getting along well.


Haise steps out of the carriage and sits next to Geist, who is holding the reins.


"Phew..."


"What’s wrong, tired already?"


"No... just feeling a bit uncomfortable."


"Heh, endure it for fifteen days. Besides, we’ll be staying in towns along the way, not just camping."


"Haah... Um, Geist-san. What exactly do we do as temporary instructors? I’d like to know properly."


"You and Sasha will handle mock combat training for adventurers and lectures on the Forbidden Six Labyrinths."


"Mock combat?"


"Yeah. Fighting using abilities. Don’t let your guard down, though. There have been cases where even S-rank adventurers were defeated. Even though they’re still trainees, they’re at least as strong as B-rank adventurers."


"Haah... Well, I’ll make sure not to kill them."


"...I’m a bit worried, though."


"Don’t worry. For example..."


Haise pulls out an M60 Revolver and fires at a nearby tree.


Bang! A light sound echoes, and a black lump hits the tree, falling off.


"These are rubber bullets for riot control. They hurt but won’t kill. For mock battles, I’ll use non-lethal ammunition like this, so don’t worry."


"I see. That’s quite convenient."


"I think so too."


Haise spins the revolver on his finger.


◇◇◇◇◇


That day, they decided to camp.


The camping gear is prepared in each of their item boxes, so they set up the tents in a friendly manner... or so it should have been.


Except for Mina, the adventurers quickly set up their own tents. Haise, in particular, has the largest capacity item box in the royal capital.


Haise speaks to Geist loud enough for everyone to hear.


"What about meals? Should we prepare something...?"


"Hmm, well, the guild’s item box has some ingredients prepared, but..."


Geist glances at the female members.


"Um, cooking is, uh..."


"...I prefer eating out."


"Meat, let’s grill meat!!"


"Ah, I’m not good at cooking."


Geist sighs and turns to Haise.


"Haise, can I ask you to handle it? Of course, I’ll pay."


"Sure. I kinda expected this."


Haise takes out a cooking table and utensils from his item box.


He also takes out stones for a stove, firewood, and asks Geist to prepare the rest.


He boils water, cuts ingredients, seasons them, grills the meat, lightly toasts bread, and sandwiches cheese and vegetables.


The result is sandwiches and soup.


"Done. Geist-san, is this okay?"


"Perfect. Haha, you’ve improved, Haise. You’re quite skilled now."


"Yeah, I kinda like cooking... Hey, what’s with you guys?"


Sasha, Presea, Hijiri, and Mina are all staring at Haise.


Mina nods enthusiastically and grabs Haise’s arm.


"A man who can cook is so cool!! Ufufu, maybe I’ll become your wife, Haise-san~?"


"If you’re not eating, I’ll take it."


"I-I’m eating!! Muuu, Haise-san, you’re so uninterested in me!"


They start eating immediately.


Sasha takes a bite of the sandwich and smiles.


"Mmm, delicious... as expected of Haise."


"Thanks."


"...You’re really good at cooking."


"Yeah, yeah."


"Seconds!! Mmm~ I might want to eat Haise’s cooking every day."


"Here, have some soup too."


"Haise, I want seconds too!"


"Here, have some of mine."


"...Haha, you’re such a caretaker, Haise."


"G-Geist-san... give me a break."


As Geist said, Haise is indeed a caretaker.


◇◇◇◇◇


On the first day, they decided to take turns keeping watch.


By drawing lots, the first shift went to Haise and Hijiri, followed by Sasha and Presea, and finally Geist. Mina, being a non-combatant, was excluded.


Grabbing a soot-covered pot warming by the fire, Haise pours some into a cup and hands it to Hijiri.


"Here."


"Oh, thanks."


Sitting across from Hijiri, she suddenly moves next to him.


"Hey, Haise. What do we do as temporary instructors?"


"Same question as me... You’ll probably just have to fight the students at AdAd."


"That sounds awesome!! Oh, but they’re kids, right?"


"They’re kids, but... they’re our age. Plus, they’ve been using their abilities since they were twelve and have plenty of combat experience, so don’t treat them like ordinary adventurers, Geist said."


"Really? Well, whatever. After that, it’s the Graveyard of Ruin, right?"


"...You’re going too?"


"Of course. Where there’s strong people, I’ll be there!!"


"Hey, keep it down."


Hijiri giggles while drinking her tea.


"Phew~... Hey, Haise. When I get stronger, I’ll fight Sasha and win completely this time... then next, I’ll fight you... okay?"


"Sure. If you win."


"Mmm..."


Thud. Hijiri rests her head on Haise’s shoulder.


Even after sleeping so much in the carriage, she’s still sleepy... Haise is amazed.


But Hijiri seems comfortable.


"...Tch."


Reluctantly, Haise decides to let Hijiri sleep for just five minutes, staying still and silent.
