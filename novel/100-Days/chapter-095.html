The Loser Ability Users


 


For now, I brought the two I saved and entered the bar.


Mina asks, "Huh? What happened?" but it's Haise who wants to hear the explanation.


We could have just gone home, but Sasha ended up bringing the two inside.


School uniforms, and upon further questioning, fifteen years old... They can't stay for too long.


After entering the shop and placing some casual orders, Sasha speaks up.


"So... why were you two being harassed in a place like that?"


"Uh, well..."


The bespectacled boy, Sein, glances at the ponytailed girl, Miko.


Miko turns away and mumbles.


"Well, we got hungry and went out to buy some late-night snacks. Since it was past curfew, Sein said it was a bad idea... but, but I really wanted to eat the eclairs from Hakonedou, so we sneaked out of the academy, and then those guys started picking on us..."


"...Those guys were saying something about your grades. First off, what's your relationship?"


"Me and Miko are childhood friends..."


Sein and Miko both look down. Mina lightly pokes Haise.


"W-we... are at the bottom of our class at the academy. Our abilities aren't great, and both our practical and theoretical skills are low, so we get made fun of..."


"Ah, my practical skills are decent though. I have the ability of a [Paladin]..."


"But you can't wield a greatsword, can you?"


"Ugh..."


"...I see."


Sasha quickly understood.


Miko's ability is [Paladin]... Generally speaking, it's considered an advanced version of the [Swordsman] ability. However, unlike [Swordsman], advanced abilities often have fixed traits.


For example, a [Swordsman] can use a variety of weapons, from standard swords to greatswords, dual blades, and rapiers, but the advanced abilities like [Dual Swordsman] specialize in dual blades, and [Great Swordsman] specializes in greatswords.


The 'Holy Knight' ability is an advanced version of [Great Swordsman]. The sword they can use is fixed to a greatsword.


Miko is about the same height and build as Mina. Someone as short as Sasha wouldn't be able to swing a greatsword around.


Sometimes, when an ability awakens, the body undergoes changes to accommodate the special ability... but Miko likely gained the 'Holy Knight' ability without any significant physical changes.


It happens occasionally. Someone blessed with a powerful ability, but their body isn't suited for it.


"A Loser Ability Holder, huh..."


"...Ugh."


"Haise, that name is insulting."


"Ah... my bad."


Haise apologized straightforwardly.


Miko waves her hand as if to say she doesn't mind.


"Uh, well... it's not like I can't use it at all, you know? If I boost my physical abilities with the [Enchanter] skill, I can swing it around."


"Hmm, I see... What about him?"


"I, I have the [Swap Change] ability..."


"Huh? [Swap Change]... I've never heard of that."


"Ahaha... It's a minor ability, only mentioned in the corner of the ability encyclopedia."


Mina tilts her head in confusion.


Sein touches the glass of water he just finished and grabs a nut from the snack plate. In an instant, the nut and the glass switch places.


"...That's all I can do. I can swap the positions of 'something I'm touching' and 'something I've touched'. But the maximum size is about the size of a glass, and it only works on things that fit in the palm of my hand... It's a useless ability."


Sein forces a bitter smile.


Indeed, this ability isn't suited for combat.


Haise, sipping his wine, speaks up.


"Then why are you at AdAd? Surely you realize this ability isn't suited for adventurers, right?"


"...Well, my family has been a line of adventurers for generations. My father is an S-rank adventurer, and he runs a large clan... All my siblings are attending AdAd too. I couldn't be the only one not going."


"I see. But trying to become an adventurer with that ability is practically suicide."


"...If I drop out, my parents said they'd disown me. I can't survive on my own, so... I figured I'd at least graduate from the academy and become independent, no matter how much I get made fun of."


"...Wow, that's heavy."


It was genuinely pitiful.


Perhaps feeling the same, Sasha asks Sein,


"Is there anything else you can do?"


"...Well, I'm good at calculations and stuff."


"You do seem like the studious type."


"That's rude."


"Muu, Haise, you were just—"


"Hold on. Don't start fighting... Huh? It's getting pretty late. You two should head back soon. We'll walk you."


"Y-yes..."


Then, Miko suddenly gets up close to Haise.


"Onii-san!!"


"W-what?"


"Onii-san, you're so strong, it was amazing! I want to be your apprentice after I graduate!!"


"Denied."


"Instant rejection!? Ugh, I won't give up!!"


Meanwhile, Sein approaches Sasha.


"Onee-san, I mean, Sasha-san!!"


"W-what?"


"My ability might not be much, but... I want to become strong like you!! You seem like a famous adventurer. I'll carry your bags or do anything, so please take me as your apprentice when I graduate!!"


"S-sorry, I don't take apprentices."


"Fufufu. You two don't seem to realize just how amazing these two are."


Mina puffs out her chest, about to say something, but Haise lightly smacks her head to shut her up.


After paying the bill, Haise, Sasha, and Mina walk the two back to the academy.


Once they confirm the two have gone inside, Mina speaks up.


"Haise... why do you hide the fact that you're S-rank?"


"If they knew, they'd be even more annoying."


With just that, Haise starts walking back to the inn.


Sasha glances back at the academy once.


"Childhood friends, huh..."


For some reason, she recalls her own past with Haise, and though they're nothing alike, she can't help but see some parallels in their situations.


◇◇◇◇◇


Haise and Sasha were walking side by side, chatting—a rare sight.


A few steps ahead, Mina is busy looking around at the town.


"Loser Ability Users, huh."


"...Haise."


"I know. Well, I used to think the same about myself."


"............"


"Hey, that glasses kid said he's good at calculations, right? Why not hire him as an accountant for your clan?"


"...That depends on his will. His eyes are the eyes of someone who dreams of being an adventurer and won't give up."


"Hmm... Also, even though it's a different type, that girl has a sword-related ability too, right? You should train her."


"...If she can't wield a weapon, it's pointless. Ah."


Sasha suddenly remembers something.


"Seven-Star Light. If we forge a greatsword from that ore, she might be able to wield it."


"Seven-Star Light... Ah, that gem from the Forbidden Six Labyrinth?"


"Yeah. Swords made from it are light yet durable... but Seven-Star Light is classified as a national treasure. There's no way we can give that to a rookie adventurer."


"Here."


"...Huh?"


Haise pulls out a Seven-Star Light from his item box.


A translucent gemstone glowing with a rainbow hue rests in Sasha's hand.


"T-this is..."


"I picked it up in the deepest part of the Forbidden Six Labyrinth. It's useless to me, so you can have it."


"W-wait, hold on!! I just said it's a precious material!! It's not something you can just give to a rookie adventurer... It'll cause envy and misunderstandings."


"Is that so... Well, I'm giving it to you, so do whatever you want with it."


"~~~... You're really something else."


Sasha sighs in exasperation as she stores the Seven-Star Light in her item box.
