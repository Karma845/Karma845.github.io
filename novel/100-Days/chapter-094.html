A Chance Encounter




Three days had passed since Haise and the others entered the Holy Cross Adramelech Divine Kingdom


While Sasha and the others seemed busy with sightseeing and shopping in the town, Haise was relaxing in his room.


Then, there was a knock on the door, and Mina came in.


"Haise-saaan, let's go out to eat together for once!"


"...You, for the past three days, you've been eating out every single time. For once, eat the food at the inn."


"Ehh? But we're in a foreign country, a foreign country! It'd be a waste not to try the local food!"


"Don't Sasha and the others say anything about you shopping every day?"


"Everyone's having fun! Sasha-san's been buying lots of souvenirs for everyone in the clan. Presea-san's been getting books, and Hijiri-san's been getting sweets and meat and stuff."


"..."


"Hey, hey, let's go! It's almost evening."


"...Sigh."


Haise stood up and put on his coat.


As they left the room, he asked Mina,


"So, where are we going? And is it just you and me?"


"Ah, Sasha-san will be joining us too. Hijiri-san took Presea-san to a yakiniku place on the outskirts of town."


"Yakiniku... I'd rather go there."


"No way! We're going to a pancake place!"


"Pa-pancakes? Isn't that usually for breakfast?"


"Come on, it's fine! Ah, Sasha-saaan!"


As they left the inn, they found Sasha, who had changed into casual clothes.


A simple white shirt, a dark blue skirt, long boots, and a thin belt with a sword hanging from it. Her long hair was left down, and she wore a headband with a feather decoration.


"You came, Haise."


"Yeah. Hey, Sasha, apparently dinner is pancakes..."


"U-uh. Well, I think it's fine?"


"...Yakiniku."


"!!"


When Haise said that, Sasha twitched slightly.


Despite her appearance, Sasha was a meat lover. To be honest, she could eat more than Haise.


"Come on, let's go! Pancakes~♪"


Mina started walking.


Haise glanced at Sasha and said,


"Don't push yourself too much. If there's something you want to eat, just say it."


"S-shut up. It's just that, for once, I want to... you know, eat something girly like pancakes."


"Sure, sure."


"Ah, wait! You're really something..."


Not far from the inn was the pancake shop.


A pure white brick building with a wreath of white flowers on the door.


When Mina opened the door, the inside was bright and had a nice atmosphere.


"Welcome. Please take any seat you like."


They sat at a four-person table by the window, and the waitress handed them menus.


Haise looked at the menu and muttered, "Oh."


"This is a pancake place, right? They have Black Diamond Oak steak here."


"!!"


"Ah, you're right. It seems the menu changes for dinner time."


"Then I'll have the steak. What about you two? It's a pancake place, so are you getting pancakes?"


Haise was being a bit mischievous. Sasha looked at Mina.


"Fufufu. As a delicate maiden, it's only proper to eat pancakes elegantly at a place like this. Right, Sasha-san?"


"Eh, ah, uh... yeah, not meat, uh..."


"───Pfft."


Haise almost burst out laughing. Sasha, being a meat lover, probably wanted the steak.


It wasn't like she was hiding her love for meat, but the atmosphere made it hard for her to order.


In the end, Sasha ordered a cream-topped fruit pancake (extra large), and Mina ordered a regular-sized one.


What arrived was a beautiful pancake.


And then, a sizzling, jet-black oak steak. Moreover, it was the highest-grade cut, the "Diamond" cut.


The price was fitting, but it wasn't a problem for Haise at the moment.


"...Gulp."


"Wow, the meat smells amazing!"


"Yeah. Looks delicious."


Then, two plates of steak were placed in front of Haise.


"Huh? Haise-san, did you order two servings?"


"Ah... I must've ordered by mistake. I can't eat two servings... Hey, Sasha, want to swap half your pancake for my steak?"


"───!!"


"Looking at that pancake made me want something sweet. How about it?"


"...Haise."


"Really, Haise-san, you're impossible. And there's no way Sasha-san can eat all that, right?"


"True. Well, as long as you can eat it, it's fine. What do you say?"


"...Fine, I'll eat it."


"Wow, Sasha-san, you're so kind!"


And so, Haise and the others enjoyed their meal.


By the way, according to Sasha, the oak steak was exquisite.


◇◇◇◇◇


They left the shop.


Then, Haise covered his mouth, looking pale, and said,


"Ah, sweet... It's like the pancake was made with hellish sugar."


"Really? I thought it was super delicious!"


"Yeah, it was exquisite. Fufufu... meat."


Somehow, Sasha seemed to enjoy the pancake too. Though she probably enjoyed the meat the most.


"Ugh... my stomach feels sweet. Maybe I'll grab a light drink somewhere nearby."


"Ah, I'll come too!"


"Then I'll join as well."


"...Fine, whatever."


And so, the three of them started walking to look for a bar.


Thanks to their few days of staying, they had gotten a grasp of the town's layout. As they walked toward the bustling district, they could hear the voices of many adventurers enjoying themselves at the eateries.


"Speaking of which, we haven't been to the guild in this town... Hey, Sasha, one of the Five Great Clans is here, right? Have you been?"


"No, I sent a request for a meeting on the day we arrived. I plan to go greet them tomorrow."


"...Even for you, it takes time to meet the clan master, huh?"


"Fufu, just so you know, even if they're called one of the Five Great Clans, I don't consider myself their equal."


"I know. You're that kind of person. Then I guess I'll visit the guild tomorrow."


"Hmm, are you taking on a request?"


"No, I won't take any in this country. But... I might as well greet the guild master. Geist probably already did, though."


Geist had hardly been at the inn these past three days.


It seemed he had acquaintances at the adventurer's guild and had been busy greeting them.


Then, Mina, who was walking a few steps ahead, said,


"Ah, there's a bar over there. Come on, let's go... Huh? Sasha-san, Haise-san... what's that?"


Mina pointed to an alley next to the bar.


There, a man and a woman were surrounded by five rough-looking boys and girls.


"A fight?"


"Wait. Those uniforms... they're from Adad. Could they be students from the academy?"


"What should we do?"


"...Mina, Haise, go ahead and secure a seat at the bar. I'll check things out and join you later."


"Got it."


"Eh, eh..."


"Sasha will be fine. Unless they're SSS-rank magical beasts or something."


With that, Haise went into the bar. Mina also looked worriedly at Sasha, but when Sasha smiled, she hesitantly followed Haise inside.


◇◇◇◇◇


"Hey. Hand over your money."


"You two, the bottom of the class, huh?"


"Just give us what you've got, and we'll let you go."


It was clearly extortion.


The ones being extorted were a bespectacled boy and a spirited-looking girl with a ponytail.


The boy was unarmed, but the girl had a sword at her waist.


Sasha called out to them.


"Hey, what are you doing?"


"Huh? Who the hell are you... Don't you know who we are?"


"Don't know. Well, I can tell from your uniforms that you're students from Adad, but you just look like criminals extorting your fellow students."


"...You're an adventurer, huh? Heh, there's a difference in experience between an elite like me and a bottom-feeder like you. If you don't want to get beaten up, get lost. Little girl. Gyahahaha!"


There were five extortionists. Three boys and two girls.


The leader mocked Sasha, and the rest laughed.


Just as Reynold had said, elite adventurer trainees from the academy tended to look down on those who started from the bottom.


Sasha sighed. What should she do in this situation?


"Huh? Hey, hey, you're a real beauty. How about ditching these guys and hanging out with us instead? Hehe, and hand over your wallet too."


"...Hmm, what to do."


"Hey, don't ignore us!!"


The boy swung his fist at Sasha.


Dodging was easy. But adventurers were forbidden by the "Adventurer Law" from attacking civilians without just cause. She was about to dodge, but a hand was placed on her shoulder, pulling her back.


"Wha—"


"Ugh..."


It was Haise.


Haise pulled Sasha back and took the punch to his face.


The boy must have held back, as Haise only got a small cut on his lip.


Haise spat out some blood.


"The Adventurer Law states that adventurers cannot harm civilians without just cause... but that's only if there's no 'just cause.'"


"Huh? What the hell are you talking about?"


"You know what? 'Self-defense' allows you to attack civilians if they strike first. You threw the first punch, and I'm bleeding because of it... which means I can neutralize you to protect myself."


"Haah? You're full of crap."


Bam!! Haise's Desert Eagle fired, and a rubber bullet hit the boy square in the forehead.


"Ah, it hurts!? Wha-what the hell!?"


"Non-lethal rounds are like this. Well, they're useful for mock battles."


"You little brat!!"


The boy lunged at Haise, but Haise fired his Desert Eagles in both hands repeatedly.


The boy crouched down, covering his face in pain from the rubber bullets.


"It hurts, it hurts, it hurts!! Stop, stop, stop!!"


Haise ignored him.


When he ran out of bullets, he ejected the magazine, and another boy lunged at him───but Sasha stepped forward, grabbed the boy's arm, and threw him to the ground with a single motion.


Meanwhile, Haise reloaded.


"Ah, it hurts... damn you."


Bam!! A live round embedded itself in the ground next to the crouching boy, leaving a hole in the brick pavement.


"Next time, it won't just hurt... What'll it be?"


"Haise, that's enough."


"Right, got it."


"Eek!!"


The boys ran away.


Haise holstered his gun and rubbed his cheek. Then, Sasha gently pressed a handkerchief to his cheek.


"Don't be reckless... You could've claimed self-defense without taking a hit."


"...It's fine."


"True. Fufu, thanks, Haise."


"...Let's go get a drink."


And then───they finally remembered.


"Ah, um!! Th-thank you so much!!"


"That was so cool... Really, thank you so much!!"


"Eh, ah, yeah. Are you two hurt?"


"Yes!! Um, I'm... I'm Sain!!"


"Ah, yeah. I'm Sasha."


"Onii-chan, thank you so much!! I'm Miko."
