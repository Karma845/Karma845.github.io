Heracles 6


 


The guild carriage carrying Sasha was heading towards the Overlook Wilderness.


Inside the carriage, Sasha was alone. Arms crossed, eyes closed, focusing.


Soon, the battle would begin.


Not as an adventurer fighting magical beasts. This time, her opponents would be humans. And formidable ones at that.


"…………"


Sasha remained silent, swaying with the carriage.


She calmly reflected.


S-rank adventurer Heracles Hijiri. At first glance, Sasha recognized Hijiri as a "powerful foe". Likely, an equal match for Sasha.


Throughout her adventurer career, Sasha had fought and even killed humans.


Her first opponents were bandits and thieves.


This was back when Haise was still with Sacred. They happened upon a village under attack by bandits while on a quest, and Sasha fought to protect herself.


Her first human combat. She still remembers her hands trembling, her teeth chattering.


But───seeing a little girl's father cut down before her eyes, Sasha snapped.


She couldn't remain calm like she does now.


She unleashed her full power as a [Sword Master].


Blood splattered, flesh and screams flew. When she realized the screams were from the bandits... it was all over.


After taking a human life for the first time, Sasha couldn't eat properly for a week.


"...Nostalgic."


It was because Haise was by her side that she managed to recover.


Back then, Haise had always been there for Sasha.


But Haise is no longer here.


Likely, the fight with Hijiri will be a battle to the death.


"Huh..."


But Sasha was smiling.


Some part of her was looking forward to it.


"This is also me..."


And then───...the carriage stopped.


The door opened, and Sasha stepped out.


About ten meters ahead, Hijiri emerged from another carriage.


"You came, Sasha."


"Yeah."


What she felt was fighting spirit.


Her expression was different. The childish air was gone, replaced by the eyes of a beast hungry for battle.


Sasha was the same.


"You are one of the walls I must overcome to become the strongest. Next is Haise."


"Sorry, but... I'm not a wall you can easily climb over."


Sasha and Hijiri both wore the smiles of hungry beasts.


◇◇◇◇◇


About an hour before Sasha and Hijiri arrived.


Haise, the members of Team Sacred, the Heiberg royals Cress and Myuane, and their four knight guards had already arrived at the Overlook Wilderness.


Haise, the last to arrive, greeted Geist, who was already there.


"Good morning, Geist. You're early."


"Yeah. I got here yesterday and took care of the magical beasts in the area. I gave them a good scare, so beasts below S-rank won't come near."


"R-really... You're as amazing as ever."


A little further away, Reynold and the others were gathered.


Beyond them was a tent bearing the Crest of the Heiberg royal family.


Just now, Haise noticed Cress and Myuane laughing together with Reynold and the others.


Not wanting to bother with greetings, Haise avoided eye contact.


Then───it... arrived.


"Ohhhh~!! You must be Haise-chan!! Mmm, what a fine man~!!"


"Wha!?"


Haise almost instinctively drew Desert Eagle.


Then, Geist approached.


"Apollon, don't startle him."


"Gei-chan!! Nfufufufu~, isn't it fine? A fine man is vitality!! Energy, you know!!"


"…………Uh, um..."


"Yeah, let me introduce you. This is Apollon. S-rank adventurer Panacea Apollon... Ah, despite appearances, she's the top healer among S-rank adventurers."


"Nfufu~n, nice to meet you~n!! Mwah!!"


"Eek!"


Haise leaped back with all his might as Apollon tried to kiss him.


S-rank adventurer Panacea Apollon.


She wore a pure white nun's habit adorned with golden embroidery and held a staff glittering with embedded gems.


But... for some reason. Apollon was unmistakably male. His body was a mass of muscles, the nun's habit straining to contain him, on the verge of tearing. His face was rugged, and he had no hair, but his scalp was tattooed with a flame pattern. His ears were pierced with numerous earrings, his face adorned with flashy makeup, and his bright red lipstick was impossible to miss.


Haise was genuinely puzzled. He even wondered if Apollon was a new species of humanoid magical beast.


"Ah... I get that you're confused, but think of him as a 'man with the heart of a woman'."


"No!! Not a woman, but a maiden!! A maiden's heart in a girl's body!!"


Apollon's deep voice contrasted with her girlish demeanor as she swayed her hips and approached Geist.


She was nothing short of suspicious.


Geist let out a heavy sigh.


"Haise. Despite appearances, Apollon is a cardinal of the 'Church' and holds the power of the [Saint King], who commands all [Saint]. Look, Piasora's turning pale."


"Ah, really."


Piasora was hiding behind Reynold's shadow.


Pale, avoiding eye contact.


"The Church, right? I only know that Piasora was part of it... So the top of the Church is an adventurer, and... quite the character. Why an adventurer?"


"Because I'm 'free', you see~. Ufufu."


"...I see."


Not understanding, Haise decided to let it go.


All Haise knew was that when a healer's abilities were identified, they were obligated to report to and join the Church.


Apollon was the head of the Church.


Not understanding and not wanting to, Haise decided not to dwell on it.


"Anyway, you don't need to worry about injuries. With Apollon here, even if someone dies, they can be revived within a day."


"R-really? That's amazing..."


"It's not amazing."


Then───Apollon suddenly turned serious, gently touching Haise's right eye... the eyepatch.


"I'm sorry..."


"Huh?"


"Apollon."


"It's fine. Gei-chan..."


Geist sighed again.


Apollon spoke apologetically to Haise.


"When you lost your right eye, Gei-chan contacted me. He asked me to heal your wounds... but I couldn't make it in time."


"…………"


"Within a day, even if you lose a limb, or your body below the neck, or are just a head, or even a single finger, I can revive you. But... after a day, it's impossible. I... couldn't make it in time to heal you. I was outside the capital, and by the time I got the message, more than a day had passed..."


"I see..."


"I owe Gei-chan a lot. I would do anything for him. But... I couldn't do anything."


"Apollon, that's enough."


Geist patted Apollon's shoulder.


"I didn't call you here to make you feel regret. If Sasha and Hijiri get injured in today's duel, heal them without a scratch."


"Okay!! I'll do my best!! I love you, Gei-chan!!"


"Stop that. Haise, sorry... Let's leave it at that for now."


"Uh, I don't mind. Besides, I've gotten used to not having my right eye."


"...Yeah."


They didn't discuss it further.


Then, two carriages arrived.


Stepping out were Sasha and Hijiri.


"Alright, let's go, Haise, Apollon. We have a job as witnesses."


"Yess~!!"


"Understood."


The duel was about to begin.
