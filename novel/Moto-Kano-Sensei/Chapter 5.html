As if not willing to miss even the slightest lie, he looked straight into my eyes for confirmation.

The sharp glint in his eyes made me want to look away immediately, but I held back my impulses and nodded while staring right back at the principal’s eyes.

“It’s true that I intervened. Of course, I had only intervened because I wanted to, and it’s not like I was pushed by the three girls to do so. So, if you’re going to punish someone, please let it be me and me alone.”

Hearing his judgment of self-defense, I could see the light of hope at the end of the tunnel for a moment. However――

“However…”

The principal gave me a stern look and my faint hope that I might be forgiven was forever crushed.

“Whether it was in self-defense or not, the fact remains that you got into a fight outside of school. And then, acknowledging the precedent, you should definitely be expelled from our academy.”

“… Was there a student who went through the same thing as me in the past?”

“It’s not exactly the same. To tell the truth, the two men you were engaged in a quarrel with were students who were enrolled at our school four years ago.”

“Eh? They were past students of this very same academy?”

“Yes. The two of them caused a violent incident during a fellowship excursion. I expelled them myself from our school.”

I did the exact same thing they did — I got into a fight in Cosmoland on a fellowship excursion trip.

But even then —-

“Is that so…”

What leaked out of my mouth was such a careless line.

My punishment has been decided, but Akari, Kohaku, and Mashiro-san wouldn’t be punished.

Are you telling me, I won’t be punished at all?!

And on top of that, I was getting thanked by the principal at that…?!

My heart was filled with joy as he told me this with a smile on his face.
