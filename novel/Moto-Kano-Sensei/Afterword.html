Afterword

Nice to meet you. I am Nekomata-Oko here.

For the book,『Ex-girlfriend wants to re-nurture love with me starting with a slightly naughty home visit.』 thank you very much for having this volume 1 here.

I think some people will read starting from the afterword, so I would like to introduce the contents of this work in such a way so as not to spoil the actual storyline in any way.

As the title suggests, this work is about a heroine who is a teacher.

When I debuted, I don’t think I’ve seen many stories where the teacher was the main heroine, but as time went on, I think it was not such a rare storyline anymore, so I decided to make the teacher the ex-girlfriend.

Shirasawa Kohaku and Akamine Akari.

There are two ex-girlfriends.

And they both love their ex-boyfriend, Touma Nijino.

What kind of days will Touma Nijino, a high school boy who reunites with his ex-girlfriends at school, be like —- I hope this excites you and makes you enjoy the story to the fullest.

With this, I will go to the Acknowledgments.

Many people have contributed to the publication of this work.

Everyone in the HJ Bunko editorial department, including the person in charge.

The illustrator Kat-sensei, who worked to create such a wonderful illustration.

Proofreaders, designers, and other people involved in this work.

My heartiest thanks to all of you there.

And above all, I would like to express my deepest and best gratitude to all the readers who purchased this work.

I would be more than happy if everyone enjoyed reading this, even if it is only by a little.

I hope to see you again in the next volume.

In 2021, still our normal every day lives continue.
