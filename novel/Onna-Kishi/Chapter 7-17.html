Chapter 7 – Introduction to the World

○ History

The history of human beings has only been in existence for about 1500 years. Before that time, humans existed, but they were not accustomed to keeping records of their existence, and their area of living was very limited, being found only along the east and west coasts and in parts of the central plains of the continent where the protagonists are now. There are three major continents that have been discovered, and while the eastern continent has been found to have a human race with a different culture, there is little evidence of a human race living in the southern continent.

Fifteen hundred years ago, the Archfiends held the supremacy of the continent, and the struggle for supremacy was mainly between them. It is said that there were several hundred Archfiends in existence during that time. In a broader sense, an Archfiend is a fiend that unifies fiends across races, but in a narrowed sense, an Archfiend can also be a being who follows many fiends, or a being who had extraordinary power, even though they are alone.

Humans, elves, fairies, and some giants, who were in the minority at that time, cooperated with each other, and it is said that the papermaking and recording techniques were introduced to humans at that time. It is also said that the human race was superior ti other races in that they were greedy for survival and learning, and took in the advantages of other races one after another. The elves taught magic and languages, the faeries taught herbalism and natural science, the dwarves taught iron making and construction, and the giants taught martial arts and combat. In addition, humans are highly diverse, with a wide range of attributes and characteristics depending on the individual, and their resistance to environmental changes is a major advantage. For example, spirits with the attribute of water would have easily become extinct if they were forced to leave their watery habitats, but humans were able to survive by digging wells and drawing water.

It is said that the first time humans started to fight back was about 1200 years ago. A young man named Dayadan, who was a close of the giant race, was the first to succeed in conquering the Archfiend. This fact was quickly communicated to the entire continent through the fairy information network, making people realize that the Archfiends were not immortal, but an existence that humans could defeat. At this stage, the Archfiends were still underestimating the humans, and there was no sense of cooperation among the Archfiends themselves, so looking back on history, the Archfiends were behind the curve.

In addition, about 1,000 years ago, the concept of a nation was established among humans, and the nations that fought against the Archfiends in groups began to form. The Archfiends realized that the situation was bad at this point, but only half of them tried to make countermeasures and tbe other half were engaged in a struggle among the Archfiends. The humans took advantage of this gap, and with the help of Sigmund, the king of elves, and Fard, the king giants, they defeated the Archfiends who seemed to be aware of the threats posed by the humans. In this era many heroes appeared one after another, including the legendary hero king Graham, and the sword emperor Titania and the brave Zebeire.

In the past 700 years, humans have expanded their territory to cover half of the continent. In addition, organization such as Church of Arnelia and the Sorcerous Society have appeared, actively taking the roles as mediators between nations. The number of Archfiends was reduced to about 100, but yhe remaining ones were the mostly powerful, and the six especially Archfiends were called『Great Archfiends.』

This battle ended in total victory for the humans who annihilated the Great Archfiends, but the spirits and giants were exhausted by the successive battles and gradually disappeared from the stage of history. Many of the Archfiends who survived came to live in hiding so that they would not be discovered by humans, and the power structure between humans, fiends, and Archfiends was completely reversed. The only difference between the Archfiends and the humans is that the Archfiends do not know how to cooperate even when they have other species in their ranks, so the war naturally becomes a『us versus them』situation, and it is no longer possible to reverse the position.

However, following the defeat of the last Great Archfiend, which marked the end of the Great War Period, conflicts broke out among human nations. In this era, cooperation between nations and groups faded, and the Church of Arnelia and the Church of Magic remained neutral. In the midst of this, the Church of Olympus was established in the west, and from then on, it was with the Church of Arnelia in the east. In addition, various branches of the Church of Magic were established, and oversight became insufficient.

In this era, the hundreds of small nations that had been stered throughout the world were unified into the dozens of nations we know today, and this is known as the『Dawn Period.』It is said that this period ended about 20 years ago, which is a very recent development. The reason for the end of the Dawn Period was the emergence of an Archfiend among the humans, which led to a series of wars involving multiple nations. These include the war of the usurper king Bramsel, the forbidden war of the sorcerer kind Herhold, the rebellion of the bandit Yapu, and the rebellion of the slave gladiator Zasamund. It was also during this period that the beastmen began to claim their own state. In any case, these wars exhausted the nations, and a feeling of war weariness grew. The Church of Arnelia and the Church of Magic acted as go-betweens, leading to the conclusion of a peace agreement between the countries.

After this process, we are now in what is called the 『Peace Period,』where each country sends a representative to hold a peace conference once a year. However, no one knows how long this era will last, and many point out that it may be as fragile as walking on thin ice. Even historians are calling the past two decades『too quiet.』

○ Species

(1) Spirits

Spirits, as the name of the magic type implies, are said to be beings that control the elements. Based on the basic elemental thinking of this world, they are distinguished as fire, wind, earth, water, gold, light, gold and unclassifiable.

Each of them is said to have a ruling spirit, like with the Salamander of fire and the Undine of water being representative, but they are said to be visible only on rare occasions. Some of them are treated as「gods,」but their names vary from place to place, and their chanting may differ even in the same magic. Perhaps because of that, there are two types of magic systems: spirit magic, which seeks to negotiate with the spirits and borrow their power; and physical magic, which directly manipulates the elements. The former is often used by those who are deeply religious, while the latter is used by those who pursue magic as a form.

(2) Fairies

They are said to be messengers of spirits, but they are one of the species that make up the ecosystem. Some of them are actually spirits. While some of the fairies are relatively friendly to humans, nymphs are generally exclusive fairies. In a broader sense, goblins and elves are considered to be a kind of fairy, but in this world, fairies tend to be palm-sized species, and goblins and elves are often recognized as demihumans.

(3) Demihumans

They are represented by elves, a species that is similar in appearance to human, but is definitely different from humans. Elves for example, have pointed ears and live about three times as long as humans. They also have considerably more magical power than humans on average, but their physical strength is somewhat inferior. They are a race that has sided with humans at times, but they remain neutral and are considered to be a race with little emotion. There is also a race of brown-skinned elves that humans named Dark Elves, but this is a nuisance to them, and there are many races among them.

In addition, there is a race called Beastmen, which often have an appearance somewhere between dogs, s, and horses, which humans have long treated as livestocks, and humans.

On the other hand, there are other races such as goblins, orcs and some of the giant tribes, many of which act hostile towards human. They are often cooperative with the Archfiend, who will be discussed later, and they also like to attack other races. Their intelligence is even lower than that of a beastmen, and they are very faithful to their three major appetites: desire to sleep, hunger, and sexual desire.

(4) Phantoms • Magical Beasts

Appearance varies, but some species, such as the Forest Wolf are similar in appearance to normal wolves, differing only in size, while others, such as the nue and griffin, exhibit characteristics of multiple animals. Some species that are more familiar to humans are the amma and the dragon. Unlike wyverns, there are also dragons that understand human languages, but it is generally believed that only a few of them still exists.

(5) Fiends

It generally refers to species that are hostile to everything but themselves. In a broad sense, goblins and orcs are also classified here, but unlike goblins and orcs, fiends are generally highly intelligent, and it is not uncommon for them to use magic. Among them, those who show outstanding abilities and have the ability to unite other races are called 「Archfiends」and「Great Archfiends,」and are the object of fear not only for humans but for all races. Some fiends have an appearance similar to that of humans.
