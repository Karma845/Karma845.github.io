Prologue

"Nice to meet you, I'm Tsubakiya Hinata."

Shivering feeling.

With just that simple greeting, the whole atmosphere changed.

In the second year of class A, in junior high school - Mominoki Private Academy.

This is spring weather. The opening ceremony ends at 11.00. In a new class with new friends, the first homeroom period is held. Of course, there is no shortage of time for students to introduce themselves.

“I was in class C in my freshman year. I didn't join any club. I don't have any particular interests, but I quite like reading."

The female student's attitude seemed to emit light.

Just standing still, it was as if rays of bright light were emanating from her body.

Spring is also the season of new encounters. From here, you can see the cherry blossom trees through the open windows. The flower petals dance to the rhythm of the wind.

“There were a few who were in the same class as me last year, but there may be many more who will become new classmates. So in the coming year, I look forward to your help.”

Like that, she ended her speech.

He didn't forget to make a very precise stooping posture.

The atmosphere in the class changed. The sound of silence, even the sound of holding her breath. The voices just blended together, spreading in the hearts of every student.

[…The next student who introduces himself will be under a lot of pressure.]

The homeroom teacher Onodera Tatsuya sighed in frustration. Students must introduce themselves next, no matter what they do, they can't compare.

The girl is extraordinary, for better or for worse, both are extraordinary.

Shiny black hair, eyes that emit light that fascinates one, slim figure, flawless skin.

Above all is height. She is taller than other students her age, the numbers prove it. 172cm is a number that exceeds the average height of a boy.

From the outside, she looked like an adult. However, her facial features still had a hint of immaturity left...

That magical balance made one implicitly see that her appearance was completely unsuitable for her age.

Yes, everyone knows it, everyone feels it.

That this girl is not normal.

That she was clearly just a child like the others, but something was wrong.

That in the not too distant future, she would become a certain character that no one else could reach... No, perhaps she had completely become a "character" there by now.

Then, as the child grows up, the child will feel tortured and uncomfortable whenever she tries to explain her way of thinking in the past every time she looks back at the past.
Second year of junior high school.
