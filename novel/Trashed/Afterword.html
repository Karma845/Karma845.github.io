Afterword

Hello everyone, I'm Yamamoto Takeshi making my debut with this book.

Have you ever seen a condom vending machine?

There are many condom vending machines near where I live. The vending machines always shine brightly on winter nights, so I thought of making this story.

Do you feel embarrassed to be seen by others when you buy something from a vending machine?

What if the protagonist is a high school student?

If you are still seen by the heroine who has a good impression of the protagonist, what will everyone think?

I fantasized about such a scene in my mind, and gradually became curious about the further development, so I started to write down these thoughts.

It took only three weeks to start writing from the impromptu intention to the end of the competition. But I finally finished writing it, and the rankings rose smoothly.

In the 6th Web Contest, I won a special award in the romantic comedy category and the ComicWalker manga award. My work has also been revised and remade a lot.

I wonder if everyone enjoyed the story that unfolded differently from this web version?

If you find it interesting, you'll want to keep reading. It will be my pleasure.
