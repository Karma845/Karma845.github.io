Epilogue

Since the incident at the sports equipment warehouse, the secretly photographed photos of Touyama and Takai have not turned into rumors, and Kurashima and Ishiyama have done nothing to Touyama and the others.

After school, Touyama was in charge of the library committee members' work in the library as usual, and Takai read quietly beside him as usual.

「Touyama! I came here to play！」

The library door slammed open, and Uehara rushed in.

「Uehara-san, please stay in the library. And this isn't the place to play.」

「Hey hey, sorry. But, isn't there anyone here right now? It's okay to be a little noisy.」

Uehara also appeared in the library as usual, behaving the same as usual.

Touyama pointed to the table where Takai was, and looked at Uehara.

「So Takai-san is here? Sorry, I wasn't paying attention to you.」

Uehara, who saw Takai's figure, ran to his side and talked to him as usual.

「Well, it's okay, I don't care.」

Takai looked at Uehara who was standing beside the table.

Uehara and Takai looked at each other with their eyes facing each other.

He's so cute.

Uehara seemed to be attracted by Takai's eyes that couldn't read emotions.

The mysterious atmosphere that Takai exudes, as well as the ghosts that break out when you touch it, fuel its charm.

Am I going to salt the enemy?

Helping Takai change his image and maximize his charm made Uehara regret a little.

Uehara remembered what happened in front of the sports equipment warehouse.

Uehara remembered the conversation between Touyama, Takai, and Kazuto. Kazuto said that he saw Touyama entering Takai's house, but Takai didn't deny it.

Later at that time, Takai used Yuki to call Toyama. That was the name the two of them used when they were together.

Uehara was very confident.

Could it be that he had not entered the space between the two of them?

Thinking of this, Uehara felt a pain in his chest.

But Uehara put that idea behind him.

Even so, the courage Touyama showed at the class meeting that day did make Uehara to fall in love with him.

I don't want to give up!

Uehara, who had such a strong desire, brought his face close to Takai's ear and whispered.

「I won't give up Yumi.」

Hearing this, Takai looked at Uehara again.

Takai, who was always expressionless, widened his eyes in momentary surprise.

「Me too!」

After Takai left those words, his eyes returned to the book.

As usual... it was a lie of self-satisfaction with his status. After Uehara declared her rival love for Takai, the gears that started spinning could not be stopped.

「Uehara-san, please don't disturb Takai-san's reading.」

Touyama, who was worried that Takai would be disturbed, came to talk to Uehara.

「Takai-san, that's not it, is it?」

「 Uehara-san, you've interrupted my reading, please don't talk to me if you're okay.」

「Eh!? Takai-san, this isn't what you should say, you should say『don't interrupt my reading anymore』！？」」

Seeing their conversation, Touyama couldn't help but laugh.

Then, holding his stomach and squinting at Takai, Touyama still noticed that he had an expression that Touyama had never seen before.

Yes, Takai was smiling at that moment.
