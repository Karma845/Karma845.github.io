Interlude 2: Her change.

『Good night, Nanami-san.』

Even now, I was still ruminating about Youshin’s words from earlier, and I could still hear them ringing in my ears. This wasn’t the first time I spoke to a boy, and I knew that I wasn’t very good at it, but despite that, talking with him kept me on the edge of my seat.

It felt as if Youshin was right next to my ear, and I thought what a great invention the telephone was, thanking the person who invented the telephone, whose face and name I didn’t know.

….Now that I think about it, isn’t this the first time I’ve talked to a boy alone? Moreover, in a room at night like this.

“What’s this, Yabai! T-This is too much!!”
[Tl notes: Yabai:]

I was lying face down on my bed, flapping my legs violently. It didn’t make any sense, but for some reason, there was a part of me that couldn’t relax unless I was moving in some way.

I-It’s no good…. I can’t seem to control my feelings somehow. I feel really restless. My head feels light, like the time when I had a fever.

“I guess I can’t argue with Hatsumi and Ayumi after all…”

Today was filled with a series of unbelievable events.

When I arrived at the meeting place too early in the morning and was trying to figure out how to kill time, he came right up to me.

Despite his embarrassment, he noticed the changes in my hairstyle and said I looked cute. Because of that, I thought Youshin looked even cuter than before.

As soon as he told me I was cute, I instinctively suggested holding hands. Inwardly, I panicked, wondering why I was saying such a thing out of the blue, but it didn’t matter anymore when his hand touched mine.

While feeling the comforting warmth of Youshin’s hand in mine, I was very nervous as this was my first experience.

I didn’t have sweaty hands or anything strange, did I?

I was really nervous when I invited Youshin out for lunch. Because of what Hatsumi told me, I was in a panic about what I would do if Youshin brought his lunch with him, but I was relieved to find out that he didn’t have his lunch with him.

Is there anything you don’t like?　

Would you be okay with something homemade?　

I thought of all the things I should have asked him afterwards, inviting him was a good idea, but I felt very nervous.

I also got poked on the cheek, ate lunch together, and somehow ended up doing the “Ahhh” thing.

Why did I do the “Ahhh” thing!?

But once I did it, I couldn’t withdraw.

Please eat it, I beg you! Come on, eat up! The stares of the people around me hurt, and I’m embarrassed to be doing this to myself!! I was in a hurry.

I was so relieved when he ate it that I continued with my lunch…….

On second thought, isn’t that an indirect kiss?

Uwa, I just noticed this…and now I’m starting to feel embarrassed. I curl up on the bed, holding both my hot cheeks.

I had an…indirect kiss with Youshin…

No, I can’t, that kind of thing; if someone said that to me, I’d probably die of embarrassment. Youshin would never say that kind of thing to me, too, I think.

In the first place, was Youshin even aware of this? That this was an indirect kiss?

At the end of the day, after school, we even went out for a shopping date.

Wait, is it a shopping date or a first date?

Not exactly, is it?

All of this was a first for me.

Well, it comes as no surprise since this was the first time I’ve had a boyfriend…….

Although he was the ‘boyfriend’ who I confessed to in a punishment game…I had a lot of fun whenever I was with Youshin.

…I-I wanted to spend more time with him.

But I can’t help but feel guilty about that in the end.

Even so…I glanced at Youshin’s lunchbox that I kept secretly in the room.

He bought it for me… it’s my prized possession.

No, I don’t think it’s quite the same as saying he bought it for me. Cause if you think about it, it’s emotionally understandable that he would buy it for himself…..I was just a little floaty and ecstatic that I didn’t realize it until I said it myself.

But even so, I still feel like I’ve gotten a gift from Youshin, and I still keep it in my room secretly instead of hiding it in the kitchen.

Every day, from now on, I will fill this lunch box with my home cooking and give it to him.

The mere thought of that just makes me so happy.

『Is that a loving wife’s bento?』

Immediately, the smiling faces of the grinning Hatsumi and Ayumi come to mind.

“I’m not a loving wife yet!”

I got up from the bed with a jolt and excused myself to the two people who weren’t there.

Ugh….those two are so weird…..

Cooking is all about love, and it’s certainly true that you pour your love into it. Love is put into it, but… it’s like a mother’s love…. That kind of love…it must be…..

The more I thought about it, the hotter my cheeks got, and once again, I writhed on the bed.

“Eh, what’s going on? Nanami said that she was a little scared of boys, but now she was holding hands with one on the way to school…”

“I’m so jealous～. I wish I could hold hands with my boyfriend on the way to school…but I can’t….”

That’s what Hatsumi and Ayumi said to me this morning when they interrogated me about my situation.……but that’s not what I want to hear from you, Hatsumi.

Ayumi seemed to be envious of the fact that Youshin and I held hands on the way to school.

It was definitely just going to school, but….it was a lot of fun.

No, going to school with friends was fun, but this was a different kind of fun. Ayumi couldn’t do it herself, whereas I could…so she was jealous of me.

At any rate, I explained everything that had happened yesterday, including the fact that Youshin had helped me altogether.

They listened to me in silence.

“Really…so Youshin did that, huh….and then you fell in love with him right then and there, after Youshin saved you, am I right, Nanami? Umu….I see. Then our worries were truly right on the money, weren’t they?”

“Nanami is so simple, really, really. But I guess I made the right choice by choosing Misumai. He’s unexpectedly dependable in times of need, and he’s a good match for Nanami.”

You’re being pretty rude, calling me simple.

Despite this, I was so delighted when they praised Youshin that I continued to speak of him afterwards to the two of them.

Even though we’ve only been dating for two days…..

When I came to after talking too much, the two of them had a grin on their faces…..but there was a look of relief on their faces as they watched over me.

“T-This is just a normal love talk…. isn’t it? It was the first time the three of us had a girls talk together. It was fun.”

The three of us were chatting even before I contacted Youshin.

The three of us talked about love….I may have talked too much…however, they still listened to my words diligently.

And then, without hesitation, they gave me irresponsible advice about how I could go even further. Because it was impossible for me to go even further than I could right now!

Towards the end, I was told,

“Enough bragging about your sweetheart, go and contact him already”,

but was I really bragging that much?

I’m a little embarrassed.

But actually, it took me a while to get in touch with Youshin.

For a long time, I stared at the phone, unable to muster the courage.
Should I…really contact Youshin?

Would it bother him?

Is he already asleep?

Thoughts like that ran through my head.

But I wanted to hear his voice…I wondered what I should do…In the end, I decided to leave Youshin with a message.

I think I’ve lost my mind.

Youshin called me himself, which made me happy.

Then I started talking to Youshin…and he asked me out on a date.

Although he was always quiet at school, I get overwhelmed when he comes on strongly….but I don’t particularly hate that. Unfortunately, I had a prior engagement on Saturday.

His panicked voice was cute, so I pretended to be relaxed on the surface, but secretly I was nervous and disappointed that we couldn’t have a date on Saturday.

However, he didn’t stop there. For some reason, he suddenly became respectful and asked me out again on Sunday. My answer was obvious.

But the truth was, I thought of asking him out on Sunday myself…but he beat me to it. That’s a little frustrating.

Even so, Youshin asked me out on a date.

And that fact made me very happy.

I’m so happy.

Why am I so happy?

Date….

If I excluded today’s after school shopping ‘date’, then Sunday would be my very first date…Sunday is my very first date…I can’t stop myself from feeling restless.

“I’ll have to put in a lot of effort for tomorrow’s bento. Oh, but I still have to make sure my mom doesn’t find out about it……”

At any rate, Youshin is sincere. So I don’t have to worry about him thanking me for the bento.

And I like it when Youshin praises me….

Like?

Like who?

No, I just like to cook, and this should simply be nothing more than practice…..at least, that’s what was supposed to be.

…..Even if I say so myself, I realized it was an unreasonable excuse.

In any case, I thought about tomorrow’s bento in order to discipline my irresistibly festive mind.

Youshin said that hamburgers would be good. I’ll cook him a huge one. Will it fit in his lunch box? Do you think he’d like an omelette? Come to think of it. I wonder which he likes better, sweet or sour…….Maybe I should have asked him what he liked today.

Should I make him some onigiri’s or….some heart-shaped Sakura denbu…or something?
[Tl notes: Sakura denbu refers to mashed, seasoned fish that is colored light pink with red food coloring. The pink color evokes the blossoms of Japanese cherry trees, called “sakura,” in Japanese. ]

……Yeah, I’m a little too embarrassed to make a heart shape, and I don’t know what they’ll say if they find out, so I’ll just make…an onigiri.

He said he could eat anything with the exception of herbaceous foods, but I didn’t have enough time to ask him in detail about his preferences because of all the various things that were going on……

Tomorrow I want to talk about many more things. I want to know a lot more about Youshin, and I want him to know a lot more about me.

But when that time comes…

“One month…is short…”

Before I knew it, I was muttering to myself.

“You don’t have to keep going out with him, but if you really like him, I guess you could keep going out with him, you know?”

That’s what they said to me when they proposed this punishment game. In the beginning, I felt depressed about it, believing that I had to go out with him for a month.

It was bad enough for the him to begin with…because I wasn’t clear on what to do in the meantime.

But now it’s different. One month felt way too short. I’m surprised at how much my feelings can change in just one day.

I wanted to try Tapioca and drink it with Youshin. I’m sure he’s never had it before, so I’d like to teach him a few things.

I wanted him to eat more of my food. Not only my bento boxes but also my freshly cooked hot food. I wanted him to say,

“It’s delicious.”

….In that case…will I be going…to his house?

I get so nervous just thinking about it.

There are a lot of events in a relationship.

I think it would be fun to go to festivals together, and I want to go to the beach during summer vacation. Along with Halloween, Christmas and Valentines day…

What I want to do, what I want to do for him, what I want him to do for me…makes me feel that one month is truly short.

“Are you guys going to kiss tomorrow?”

“I won’t do it yet! I can’t!”

I argue with Hatsumi and Ayumi as they come up in my mind once again.

Like that, I was screaming my lungs out while half-destroying my bed when Mom scolded me.

Calm down..calm down….

Youshin was calm, unlike me……..I thought he was quiet at school, but as a matter of fact, he seemed mature even.

He probably had no idea how nervous I was and how my heart was pounding wildly inside during the call.

Come to think of it, why was he being so respectful when he asked me out again? If he was truly as nervous as I was…wouldn’t he feel as happy as me?

One month later…what should I do?

I can’t imagine what would happen if Youshin found out about this and left me….just thinking about it makes me want to cry.

“…Am I really that easy?”

Questions that have no one to answer are directly digested into my mind.

I’m sure that’s not true… I’m not that easy…even now, I’m thinking only about Youshin, and I’m well aware that I couldn’t argue with what they both said.

So I decided to do one thing. I decided to follow their advice and……go for it.

“I’ll make Youshin love me!! I’ll capture his stomach! I’ll play with him as much as possible! …. I’m still too embarrassed to kiss him, though! That way, he won’t leave me!”

I know I’m the worst.

Even if this started out as a punishment confession, I hope Youshin will somehow forgive me…so before that, I’ll make sure to grasp his heart completely.

I’ll make Youshin fall in love with me all over again. ……This is the best I can do, as I still haven’t worked up the courage to confide in him.

“Alright, it’s decided! I’ll make a bento for him tomorrow! Yeah, I’m going to put in all my effort into it!”

I rose up from bed and made a racket again, and my mum got mad at me.
Now that I have a plan, there’s no more hesitation on my part!

And then, I slipped into my bed and fell asleep.

Good night, Youshin…I hope you have a good dream.

No, wait. I hope you have a good dream? Maybe I’m just that easy after all…
