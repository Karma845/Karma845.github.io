Interlude - A Result of her Boldness

Did I overdo it a little too much yesterday? 

Alone, I ask myself on the bed.

Remembering what happened, my face blushed a little, but I felt that I was too mean to Shibetsu Senpai for interrupting a valuable moment between us.

Eating lunch together with Youshin, I felt happy, although…I didn’t approve of Youshin’s actions yesterday, in the end, I did.

I remember the first thing Senpai said to me, and it made me feel somewhat disgusted. After all, Youshin still calls me by『San』, whereas Senpai still addresses me as 『Nanami』…

Well, I know that Senpai wasn’t a bad person. I understood that in my head very well. However, I wasn’t very good at dealing with that.

That’s why I flaunted my actions heedlessly.   

I felt ashamed of myself.

I picked up the grain of rice on Youshin’s cheek, ate it, and went out of my way to snuggle close to him. No, it may have been a little too much eating the rice on Youshin’s cheeks.

Well, half of my intention was to show off to Senpai, but I thought I should have been aggressive to some extent with Youshin, who seems to be quiet and aggressive to a certain extent. I have no regrets.

In the end, Senpai said that Youshin and I make a good couple, which made me feel subconsciously happy. I was happy to hear him say that, although it’s only natural.

“He said that we were a good match….but, are we really a good match?”

In the empty room, alone, I stared at the ceiling and muttered. Naturally, there was no answer. It was precisely because there was no answer that I was able to speak these words.

I wonder if Youshin would’ve been happy to hear that we’re a good match? It bothered me, but I couldn’t just ask him about it.

And while I am very happy to hear that we look good together, it was a little painful. At this moment, did I seem truly happy? I do feel happy, but for the me who lies, I no longer know my own feelings.

I have now lied not only to Youshin but to Senpai as well.

The Senpai who said that we were a good match, what would he think if he found out that our relationship was a punishment game? I-I…I’m the worst.

“Hah….”

I let out a small sigh and unbuttoned one of the buttons of my pyjamas. I thought my breathing felt a little heavy, but doing so didn’t change anything.

“That’s right, Youshin. He didn’t give me any reaction concerning the kiss on his cheeks, did he?”

After winning a basketball game, I unintentionally sent such a thing to Youshin in a fit of momentum and over-excitement, but his response was exceedingly cool.

『I’m happy. But, I’m a little embarrassed doing it at such a place.』

It was the only message I received after moments after he had read it. My mother even got angry with me because I was being too finicky, although I was awfully composed.

Perhaps, today would be the day where he brought up something about a reward, but that didn’t happen either. I was also wondering whether today would be the day when he would kiss me on the cheeks.

Maybe it was annoying? Or he doesn’t really like doing those kinds of things? Senpai also rushed in.

No, I don’t think so.

He said such cool things on the rooftop, which made me squeamish, and I’m not convinced that he doesn’t like to flirt with me in public just because he’s embarrassed.

Ahhhhh, my face is getting all hot and bothered just remembering it. Really, why does every single thing he says make me so happy?

I was so happy when I instinctively snuggled up to Youshin. I don’t exactly remember what we talked about, but I do remember the warmth of his body.

Someone who was warm, kind, and cared about me.

I was happy to have a connection with such a person, but at the same time, I felt guilty every day when I was alone. Because of this guilt and loneliness, I exchange messages with Youshin every night.

The things we talked about were silly. We would talk about school, among other things, like what we’d do tomorrow……Tomorrow, yes, I wouldn’t be able to see Youshin tomorrow.

I’m going to see a movie I’ve been looking forward to for a while now with Hatsumi and Ayumi, but as soon as I’m not able to meet him, I feel lonely.

I’ll send lots of messages at least.

Nevertheless, I was only able to enjoy romance through watching movies and such, but I never thought I would come to fall in love with a real-life boy.

“Will tomorrow’s movie be helpful in advancing my relationship with Youshin?”

I was excited that I would be able to enjoy the movie in a slightly different way than usual. Though I can’t see him tomorrow, I need to learn a lot for him.

“Ah, a message from Youshin.”

I was talking to him a short while ago. And then a formal message comes from him. Didn’t he say he was going to play games until late today?

I lifted the edge of my mouth a little as I checked the message.

“Nanami-san, tomorrow’s movie. Have fun. I’ll miss seeing you, but I’m looking forward to Sunday.”

It was slightly stiff; however, the message seemed to have been written with me in mind. Just by looking at the message, I felt as if all the bad feelings inside me were melting away.

“Thank you. I’m looking forward to Sunday. I’ll be sending a lot of messages tomorrow too.”

That’s all I sent, I crawled into bed and closed my eyes in preparation for tomorrow. 

I hope the movie…..doesn’t have too many H-scenes. With that thought in mind……I went to sleep with a slightly lighter feeling in my heart.
