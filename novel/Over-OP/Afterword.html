Afterword

Thank you for picking up this work.

I am the author, Miku.

This is the 10th volume of the series.

I would like to thank all the readers who have read this far.

In this memorable 10th volume, the sage, which can be said to be the beginning of everything for Yuuya, has finally appeared.

Originally, I had hoped to introduce the sage at some point, but I did not expect him to make an appearance at this time.

I had some vague idea of the setting for the sage itself, but when I started writing it, I had a lot of fun writing it.

However, the unplanned part was no different this time, and I found myself surprised at the amazing turn of events that occurred in the story.

However, I am delighted that I was able to enjoy writing the story of the sage so far.

Also, on the Earth side, Merl has finally transferred to Ousei Academy.

As I wrote the story, I found myself thinking, I wish I could be a part of such an athletic festival.

At any rate, I think I enjoyed writing this 10th volume more than any other volume I have written so far.

However, as usual, I have no particular plan for the next development, so I do not know what will happen next, but I hope you can look forward to it.
