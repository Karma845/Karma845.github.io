The next morning, we decided to take a look around the Elf village, which was in the midst of its once-in-a-decade festival. Yesterday it was too dark to make out much, but the houses in the village were colourfully decorated with many flowers and ornaments, and the atmosphere was very festive.

Hey! I didn’t know there was an event like that?

If that’s the case, I will also!

“No, me too, that, I’m sorry.”

“No, umm, well, sorry.”

Then, out of the corner of my eye, there was the figure of the pervert with a grin and a nasty smile on his face. And for some reason, the other spirits are gathering one after another.
