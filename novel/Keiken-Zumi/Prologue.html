Prologue

The dazzling summer passed in the blink of an eye like a flash of light, and engraved many memories in my heart.

The first summer I spent with my first girlfriend taught me a lot about feelings.

How to trust.

How to accept the past.

What it means to love someone. .......

There were many precious “first experiences” that my experienced girlfriend gave to my inexperienced self, and it made me happy.

I want to cherish her for the rest of my life.

I want to be with you forever.

The more I wished for it, the more impatient I became.

I want to become a decent man for her as soon as possible.

I wanted everyone to acknowledge that I was the person she deserved.

As if driven by this frustrating feeling, I started walking towards my own seat.
