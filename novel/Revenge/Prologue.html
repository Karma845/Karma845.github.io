Her dress was dyed red, and having been dragged by me, her clothes are a complete mess.

Hmm. Should I make it a little more painful to recreate “that day”? But unlike me, she is a weak princess.

It wouldn’t make sense for her to die just for overdoing it a bit.

[Uhh… .. Uhh… ..?]

Victoria moaned as if wondering.

[Hm~? Why am I doing something like this? Hey, hey, did you forget?]

That time when you crucified me. You didn’t even give me the right to die, I only received suffering.

Victoria spent five days with me slowly tormenting me because couldn’t die so easily due to the divine protection.

[I thought about letting you taste that suffering]

I casted magic on her and applied the “Curse not to die” to Victoria again.

This way, it doesn’t matter if the coyotes devour your flesh or the crows gouge out your eyes, you won’t die.

[Guhhhh]

[Hm~? Don’t worry you are not alone. Look closely]

At the point where I pointed, there were the bodies of Dr. Rine Beneke and Sandra.

Their corpses were mummified so they would not rot, and like Victoria, they were crucified.

[Hey? Would be good if there are other people crucified alive? Also do not worry. I’ll come see you. If I feel like it]

[Uhhh… ..]

[I won’t let you die unlike what you did to me though]

[Hmm… ..hiiiiiii… ..!]

It’s the first time Victoria has struggled to resist since I brought her here. It’s impossible to escape you know

[You must be happy, Victoria. I’ll give you a special place on my collection shelf]

I stuffed General Ernst’s remains in a glass jar, and set it to decorate the shelf, so I moved it slightly to make Victoria the center of attention.

It wasn’t just the general. There were also his subordinates, children, wife and servants.

Items recovered from their bodies are also on display.

I put up a powerful barrier to prevent theft.

[I’m planning to build a museum of revenge here just for me. I can’t wait to complete it. You also think the same right?]

This hill still has enough space.

The demons that are the citizens of the of Horrorbach, received horrible treatment from the kingdom after losing the war. Torture, abuse, slave trade.

Today also in the town near the western border, demon hunting is carried out to obtain goods called slaves.

The captured demons are taken to the slave market, and there they are traded.

[Nooooo… ..! Let go…..!]

A demon girl was forcibly dragged from a house. Such a scene has become common since the defeat.

This is the third time this small town has encountered demon hunting. As the result of the men and women being taken away, even the children’s fall into the dirty hands of slave traders.

[Stop…..! Please don’t take my granddaughter!]

[Let me go! Grandmother! Grandma, help me!]

[Shut up!]

[Ah!]

[Hey, don’t overdo it. There are customers who get noisy because they found injuries and stuff, so treat her with care]

[Hero-sama!]

That young man smiled as he waved his jet black cloak.

The man they called a hero looked around with a haunting smile.

[How many have been caught?]

[Yes. There are 6 women and 4 men]

[Hmm. I guess it’s fine for a desert town. Only old men and women remain. So we’re done here, we’re going]

At that moment, the old vampire whose granddaughter had been taken blocked their way.

[Hero… ..sama… ..Hero-sama, please!]

[Hey, old lady! Don’t go near Raul-sama!]

The girl’s grandmother clung to Raul’s leg in a desperate way, without listening to the slave trader.

[I don’t care what happens to me… ..! Please don’t take my granddaughter! She’s only six years old! She being turn into a slave is something… ..]

[You said it doesn’t matter what happens to you right?]

The hero stared at the old woman.

[Hahaha! Do not make me laugh! Instead of making money, it will only make me lose it!]

[Gyahahaha! It’s just like Raul-sama says!]

[You are too arrogant to think you could achieve that! You are not a woman, but a monster!]

The hero who laughed out loud, when he looked at her, he kicked the old woman’s face.

[Auhh… ..]

[Grandmother!]

The attack did not end with that blow. She was kicked several times, and blood overflowed from the old woman’s nose and mouth.

[Aguhh! Ugah!]

The old woman quickly became covered in blood and couldn’t stand up from the ground.

The girl seeing that, tried to stop him while she was very pale.

[Grandmother…..! Stop, I’ll go… ..! Please don’t punish my grandmother… ..!]

[Haa? Why is a demon giving me orders !?]

The hero laughed with an even worse face and ordered the slave traders.

[Hey, make her understand a bit. Don’t kill her]

[Hahaha! Understood!]

[N-Noooooo… ..!]

[Fuhahahahaha! It’s what you get for opposing humans!]

This time the girl was a victim of violence.

The old woman cried like a beast while on the ground.

Every time the slave traders heard a sound of despair, they laughed happier.

[Yes. His majesty allowed it]

The Hero, Meeting the King

—– An hour after the execution.

I went to the king to convey my impressions of today’s show.

The king is currently staying in a corner in a separate field on the south side of Auerbach Castle.

He was always there, and he didn’t move.

When I arrived to the king’s room, the guards guarding the door became very nervous.

They don’t have to be so tense, I don’t intend to eat them. For now.

[Raul-sama… ..]

The guards bowed their heads with frightened faces.

[The king has called me]

[Yes! We are informed of that!]

The guards opened the double door and announced my visit.

[The hero Raul Evans-sama has arrived]

There is a huge bed in the center of the spacious room.

Putting my hands in my pockets, I casually walked in.

The first thing you notice is the large magic tool that surrounds the bed.

A light blue liquid flows into the clear glass container, and several tubes from there are connected to the king’s body.

What is sent into his body is a potion of the highest grade created by the court magicians.

Even if he is someone they call the king of kings, he is still a human.

The disease that is devouring his body has already spread all over, so if the potion does not continue to be administered, he will not last five minutes.

The innumerable snake-like tubes that extend from the magic tool are the lifelines for the king.

[What did you think, hero? Did you enjoy the show I made for you?]

The bedridden king asked me.

The helpers who were close by for anything, read the atmosphere and backed away.

I stood in the space open to me and looked at the king.

Despite being ill for years, the king’s eyes were still full ambitions.

He doesn’t even to try to hide his greedy nature.

Although he is a wrinkled old man, he still believes that the world is his and that everything moves as he expects.

I looked at the poor old man, and opened my mouth.

[I’ll say it wasn’t too bad.]

[Hoo. Those trash that I thought wasn’t useful anymore manage to entertain you?]

[At first it was rather boring. But when they followed my instructions, it became something than could entertain me]

It was like a dance of death.

The executioners who were hesitant at first became more enthusiastic by the smell of blood and the cheerings of the spectators.

In the end, let’s say I was able to enjoy that bloody sight.

The assembled spectators were happy with what they saw, and I think they were screaming with joy.

[To enjoy the execution of someone they don’t even hold a grudge against, the people of this kingdom are amazing]

[Fuhahaha! If you say so. But not as much as you!]

When I smiled, the king spoke after a little thought.

[The evil to commit unimaginable cruelties. You think you are the only one right?]

The king muttered in a serious tone looking at the ceiling.

[What do you mean?]

[—–Information came that a person calling himself a hero is hunting demons near the border]

[Hee~ I am having fun every day in the capital. Do you think I did it?]

Taking an arrogant attitude, the king fell silent to think in less than a second.

[The man who is hunting demons has been investigated as an imposter. But, it seems that he looks very similar to you]

[Hmm. That’s a very interesting story]

Yes of course. I knew that a long time ago.

But I pretended it’s the first time I’ve heard of it.

Still, does he really look similar to me? Although the information I received from a clone that I sent for monitoring said it was like a very degraded version.

At that moment, one of the ministers who is always with the king, said something unnecessary.

[If I may, Your Majesty… ..I think it is too early to determine if that demon hunter is an imposter… ..]

[Minister Alcott. Who the hell asked for your opinion?]

The king was pressuring the minister for what he had done. Wait wait. I don’t want that boring development. So–

[Ohh]
