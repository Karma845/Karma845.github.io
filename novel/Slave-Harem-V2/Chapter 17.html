「Why are there no monsters or undead in this layer?」

「Kruse-sama!」

　Everyone is so full of wounds that it is obvious at a glance.

「How shameless of you to talk like my loyal subject, yet follow other men around! So you can't wipe out your lowly blood after all?!」

　Kruse is furious, his veins almost torn out.

　He did not seem to be able to see Mars.

　It seemed as if all of his fatigue and frustration had erupted from Kruse.

　In short, he was taking it out on Julis.

「K-Kruse-sama......」

「Are you okay ......?」

　Julis' nose dripping blood as she reaches for Kruse's back as he leaves.

　Mars feels foolish, wondering why she should be with such a man.

　Even though he knows that people's thoughts are not motivated by rationality, she should have a better chance of living a happy life in her own place.

「It's the City of the Dead. ......」

「Th-This is probably the ancient Tomb Guard's village. ......! The altar over there, it's broken now, but I'm sure if it was done right, it will surely look like that. ......」

「Nyaa ...... Nemu doesn't know what that means-nya」

「What the heck ...... Everyone stays close to me. It's a strange place, but I think this is the lowest layer, so you never know what might happen.」

「I thought there would be real dragons or some legendary monsters, but ...... it's eerily silent」

「If that's the case, I think it's kind of cute. ......?」

　The tension is subtly diminished.

「?」

「Nyaa?」

「Is that ......!?」

　-――This is bad, this is bad, this is bad. This is a deadly place.

　This is three times as many as when he defeated Hydra, a realm Mars has never used before.

　And it is also a realm that mankind has never reached.
