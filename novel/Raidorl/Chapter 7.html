Arslanian Empire.

After the catastrophe caused by the 『Six Witches of Doom』 three hundred years ago, this great empire quickly recovered, annexed the neighbouring countries and became the central continental champion.

It was only a few years ago that this country began to harbour the endless ambition of continental domination.

Despite being a major power, the empire before that time had a rather conservative system and worked to avoid losing the wealth it already had, rather than invading neighbouring countries by force of arms.

Even so, having become a major power, it had created many enemies in its surroundings.

There are no small number of forces threatening the Empire, including the subhuman countries in the south of the continent and the guild allies in the east.

Although the Empire is allied with the Divine Empire in the north, it is not certain whether it can be trusted, as alliances between nations can be broken at any time.

If they were to start a war unwisely, they could create enemies on all sides, giving them the opportunity to take advantage of the situation.

The impetus that drove the Arslanian Empire to conquer the continent was the three holy swords owned by the Empire.

The Holy Sword of Fire 『Durandal』

　The Holy Sword of Ice 『Gjallarhorn』

　Holy sword of thunder 『Claíomh Solas』

The holders of these three holy swords appeared at the same time.

Holders of the Holy Sword may or may not appear once every few decades.

However, it was the first time since the founding of the Empire that the holders of the three sacred swords were chosen at the same time.

The emperor of the current time, Zacharias Van Arslanian, saw this as an opportunity to conquer the continent and launched a large-scale invasion of neighbouring countries.

On Zacharia’s orders, the Empire Army launched a simultaneous invasion of the Guild Confederacy in the east, the subhuman countries in the south and the Kingdom of Zain in the west.

In the eastern part of the Kingdom of Zain, at the Imperial Army position.

With the help of a powerful weapon, the Holy Sword, the invasion of the Kingdom of Zain was progressing smoothly.

It’s a situation that makes me want to cry. I don’t think the aristocrats on that side…..are entirely to blame, though.”

Betrayal is an abhorrent act. But Raidorl had no intention of blaming him for it.

In the first place, a monarchy allows the king the right to rule over the people with privileges because he protects his lands and people.

When he can no longer protect the country, he is no longer worthy of being a respected king.

The same applies to the nobles. What is important to them is their own home and territory, and they have no obligation to risk their lives to serve a royal family that cannot guarantee their safety.

“If the plundering continues, the Kingdom of Zain will be destroyed, even if the fortress is defended. To avoid this, we have no choice but to win this battle in the short term.”

“Hmm, I see. I understand. So what should I do?”

As an adventurer, Raidorl is one of the best swordsman in the kingdom. This is also guaranteed by his sword master, Zafis Bartolomeo.

However, in warfare, he was no different from a new soldier on his first battle. Raidorl never felt like he could command and move an army.

“Your Highness will stand by until the time comes. And when the time comes, you can wield your sword to your heart’s content.”

“That’s easy to understand…….By the way, you said the Imperial Holy Sword:Excalibur Holder uses the Holy Sword of Thunder, didn’t you?”

“Ha…..The holy sword: Excalibur Claíomh Solas, which governs thunder. Its wielder is the Imperial Princess Celia Von Arslanian.”

“An Imperial Princess….the enemy Holy Sword:Excalibur Holder, a woman?”

Raidorl asks back with surprise at the information he has not heard before.

Raidorl took the liberty of imagining a strong warrior like Bazel wielding a holy sword, but it seems he was mistaken.

“Be on your guard, even if she is a woman. It is because of that girl that Fort Balmes has fallen and the Royal Army has been pushed to this point.”

Bazel’s rocky face moves slightly. His lips twist in frustration and regret.

“No matter how many tricks we put up, no matter how careful we are, she defeats us as easily as if she was brushing away a spider’s web with a branch. …..As abominable as that sounds, that’s what a hero is.”

“Hee,so she’s the quintessential Holy Sword:Excalibur holder,eh? That’s interesting…..It makes my blood boil.”

Raidorl’s mouth hangs open in amusement.

In the frontier cities, he has encountered powerful demons many times, and has even faced life-threatening encounters on many occasions. The other day, he even fought a basilisk, a sub-dragon.

Even if he were to place such past powerful enemies on the same scale, the imperial Holy Sword:Excalibur holder that he will fight in the future would be superior,not inferior.

“But…..here I am. I’m going to show the Empire that there is more than one hero.”

There was no trace of fear or nervousness in Raidorl’s voice.

No matter how strong an enemy stood in his way, there was no way he could ever be defeated by the man he was now. He was not careless or proud, but clearly convinced of this.

‘There was no way I could lose. I have this guy now.’

Raidorl gripped the hilt of his Dáinsleif tightly. The holy sword at his waist lightly pulsated as if to say, [Leave it to me].
