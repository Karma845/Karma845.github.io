Prologue

A small country in the western part of the continent – the Kingdom of Zain.

Sandwiched between the great Arslanian Empire to the east and a group of several smaller states across the mountain range to the west, the country is a monarchy with a 200-year history since its foundation.

A boy – Raidorl – was born into the world as the second prince of the Kingdom of Zain.

Despite the misfortune of losing his mother in an accident when he was a child, Raidorl’s life has generally been a smooth sailing. He was able to live until the age of 13 without serious injury or illness.

His character is clever and honest. His curiosity is quite vigorous, and he sometimes escapes from the royal palace to go on 『adventures』 in the castle town, often to the annoyance of his caretakers. However, his silver hair and red eyes, similar to those of her mother from the north of the continent, were very eye-catching, and he was soon brought back and constantly scolded.

Although his academic performance in politics, economics and other subjects has been somewhat subpar, his swordsmanship is outstanding that even his instructor is astonished. He has already won a mock battle against a grown-up adult knight.

As the second prince, he could not take the throne as long as his elder brother prince was still alive and well, but Raidorl adored his elder brother, who was ten years older than him, and had no complaints.

If nothing had happened, Raidorl’s life would have gone on peacefully from then on.

“What kind of adult do you want to be in the future, Raidorl-sama?”

“Something wrong, Tina? Suddenly asking me weird questions”

The question was posed by his childhood sweetheart, Mertina Marcell, to which Raidorl raised his eyebrows suspiciously.

The two of them are in the Zain Royal Palace, the heart of the capital. They are in a garden built in the courtyard of the palace. Raidorl and Mertina were sitting on a bench in the middle of the garden.

The spring garden was full of colourful flowers and the sweet scent tickled their noses on the breeze. A handkerchief was spread out on Meltina’s lap, and a few flowers picked from the flowerbeds were laid out.

Raidorl is the son of the current king. Mertina is the daughter of the prime minister, a close associate of the king. Both are the same age of thirteen, and both were well dressed as children due to their status as a prince and a noblewoman.

The two, who are also close in status, have spent a lot of time together as childhood friends since they were born, and it is planned that they will be formally engaged once they come of age. It was what is known as a 『political marriage』, but the two showed no signs of dissatisfaction. Raidorl’s eyes show a fondness for Mertina, and Meltina seems to accept the affection shown to her by the prince.

Today, as usual, Mertina has been taken to the royal palace by her father, and Raidorl is in the middle of a conversation with her.

“It’s not urgent. Raidorl-sama will come of age next month, so I was just wondering what your plans are after that.”

Mertina shakes her purple hair and looks at Raidorl’s face.

The young lady, Mertina Marcell, is a lovely-looking girl to look at. Her hair and eyes are the shade of purple crystal. Skin as white as freshly cut marble. Although still a child, she is not stunted, and her slender body has feminine roundness in all the right places.

Her ruffled peach-coloured dress suited the lovely young lady very well, and the way she sat in the garden was like a bewitching spirit of flowers.

“T-that’s right, yeah,…..I haven’t thought about it much, but surely it’s time to start thinking about it properly.”

Gazed at up close by a childhood friend who is acquiring a more mature charm with each passing year, Raidorl flinches slightly as he ponders.

Next month, Raidorlr will turn thirteen, which means he will reach the age of adulthood in this country.

Raidorl will not be able to play around as children as he has done in the past. He will have to take on some kind of job as an adult in the not too distant future, though probably not immediately as an adult.

“Probably kinda obvious, but would I want a job where I can support my brother, who will eventually become king.”

“Ara?　That’s Surprising. I thought, 『I want to be an adventurer and travel the world!』 I thought you were going to say something like that.”

“Who do you think I am!　I’m a prince born into the Zain royal family too. I will not irresponsibly abandon my royal duties!”

Mertina is astonished and, with Raidorl’s arms crossed, shares his vision of the future.

“Becoming your brother’s vassal? Isn’t the prime minister and minister….a bit too much for you?”

“I’m not very good at studying, am I?”

“…..Yes, how about a job where you can swing a sword and fight, like to be a knight or a military officer, defending this country against enemy invasion. That would be perfect for you, Raidorl!　I’m sure you’ll make a wonderful knight!”

“’Uu—unn! I know Tina would agree!”

Slightly embarrassed by Mertina, who clasped her hands together and shouted floridly, Raidorl said, [Right!] and rose from the bench with his chest puffed out.

“If I’m going to be a hero, I’ll be a hero who can protect my country from all dangers!　I will use my sword to slay demons, armies of other nations, and all enemies that threaten the Kingdom of Zain, and become a hero who will go down in history!”

“Fuu….!”

Mertina is involuntarily blown away by Raidorl, who utters boyishly grandiose words.

“Ah! Did you just laugh?”

“No, I didn’t laugh! It’s a very nice dream to have…… fufu!”

“I knew it!　That’s terrible Tina, to laugh at people’s dreams!”

“Because, I can’t believe you’re a hero……!”

“Mu……”

Raidorl sulked and turned away from Mertina, who was finally laughing in earnest with her back arched.

The prince’s face turns embarrassed to look at her and a large palm is placed on his head.

“You’re telling an interesting story, Rai.”

“Uwaa……Ni–san!”

Before he knew it, standing behind him was Raidorl’s older brother, Granard Zain.

The first prince, who was already over 20 years old, had brown hair and eyes, and at first glance it would be hard to tell that he was a brother to the silver-haired, red-eyed Raidorl.

This is not because of any dramatic circumstance such as 『they are not actually brothers』, but simply because Granard resembles his father and Raidorl resembles his mother.

“It’s a nice dream, isn’t it? Becoming a hero. ……It’s a great goal, worthy of a royal boy. After all, a man should be able to talk about dreams as big as that.”

“Mwu……”

Granard laughs and pats his brother on the head. Despite his frustration at being treated like a child by a brother ten years older than him, Raidorl seems to enjoy the comfort.

“You are right, my dear child. Your Highness Raidorl’s dreams is wonderful.”

“F-father……”

Behind Granard is the Prime Minister, Lockwood Marcell.

When Mertina’s father saw her laughing hysterically, which is not typical of a daughter, Mertina looked somewhat uncomfortable.

The prime minister’s expression was calm, but there was a reproachful tinge in his eyes as he looked at Mertina.

Mertina placed the flowers in her lap on the bench, stood up, fingered the edge of her skirt and bowed her head.

“I have been rude. Your Highness Raidorl.”

“No, that’s fine……But more importantly, Ni–san, are you free now? If you have time, would you like to go on a long run with me?”

Raidorl invited his older brother to play, but Granard lowered his eyebrows in hesitance.

“Sorry …… I have some business I need to clear up with Lockwood now. Apparently, Father hasn’t been feeling well lately.”

“I see, …it’s okay if you can’t help it!”

At the apologetic look on his older brother’s face, Raidorl shook his head hurriedly.

Their father, the current King Bernard, had been suffering from lung problems for several months and was unable to get out of bed. As a result, Granard has been conducting political affairs as acting king.

“Rai……I’m sorry I haven’t been able to keep you company lately. I know you must be feeling lonely after what happened to father.”

“It’s not a problem!　Ni–san has a job to do as acting king, I don’t blame you!　I have Tina and my other subjects are good to me. I won’t be lonely at all!”

“Well, …… I’d appreciate you saying so. ……”

Granard relaxes his face at his little brother’s concern for him, but…..suddenly a shadow appears on his face.

“Hero,is it……well, you’re about to have your coming of age ceremony. In that place, the 『Holy Sword Room』……”

“Ni–san?Is there something wrong?”

Raidorl tilted his head at his brother, who murmured with a somewhat thoughtful expression on his face.

Granard immediately returns to his usual gentle face and pats Raidorl on the head again.

“No, it’s nothing. Rai, it’s not easy being a hero. No one, including me, has ever been chosen by the 『Holy Sword』 of this country. But if it were you, you would be a great hero even without the Holy Sword.”

“Ni–san….”

“Be the strongest hero and protect this country. You will support me, won’t you?”

“Yes! Of course!”

Raidorl nodded his head vigorously. He tapped his chest with a thump with Mertina giggling next to him.

“I will be a great hero. I will become one!　I will become so strong that no one can beat me, and I will be able to protect my Ni–san, Tina and even the Kingdom of Zain!”

Granard nodded back at Raidorl with a calm expression on his face.

It is a scene from a time when the two princes were still on friendly terms.

Raidorl did not yet know reality. It is a memory from before he experienced the pitch-black betrayal and the deep red bloodstained battlefield.

Later, Raidorl Zain’s dreams would come true.

The boy who could only talk about his dreams would become a hero with wings as powerful as those of the Phoenix King, and would fly into the sky of the battlefield.

But – as yet – Raidorl did not know.

Heroes who shine like stars in history. None of them have been able to become heroes without being sacrificed.

Every person who is called a hero has experienced many tragedies.

They kill their enemies, bathe in their blood, get themselves covered in wounds and bleed. After betraying and being betrayed, they become known as heroes. Sometimes they even kill their parents and brothers and lose their homeland in the fires of war.

And the moment when Raidorl will become a hero in exchange for many sacrifices is approaching step by step like a demon lurking in the darkness.
