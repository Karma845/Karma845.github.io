“Regardless of what you think about yourself. I am grateful to you from the bottom of my heart. Really.”

The protagonist of the story; a second-year high school student.

Tachibana Rika

“I’m your friend right now. Even if I may leave, I’m still your friend who loves you the way you are.”

She is the female protagonist of the story. She is a second-year high school student.

She is a beautiful girl who is famous within the school and is good at studying. She is a composed person with a dignified atmosphere despite her delicate build. Her personality and actions are sharp and decisive, and she is not easily influenced by her surroundings.

Like Ren, she is a logical person, and cannot tolerate things that she does not agree with. Because of this, she often makes enemies and is deemed as unfriendly. However, she is honest and innocent, and her friends love her very much.

Her hobbies and tastes are very similar to Ren’s, so they keep bumping into each other in various places even though they don’t have a fixed schedule. Through these meetings, she and Ren started to talk to each other and started to have a feeling of bonding with each other.

Currently, She and Ren are lovers and she is changing from a cool girl to a cool girl in love.

“Rika might be like that, but she’s a good girl. So, please get along with her, I don’t care how you do it.”
