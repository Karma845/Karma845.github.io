Chapter 4

4.1

“Uh…Ah…”

If I do eye contact, she might give a reaction.

Not as pretty as Tachibana, but beautiful.

What on earth…

“Alright then, go ahead.”

Apparently, it looks like Susami doesn’t care about her information.

It’s a trick that can’t be imitated by a mob-like me, as expected of a normie.

“Why are you so interested in me?”

“…We don’t get along that well.”

“OH! Which one?”

“Ah…”

DAMN…I was set up.

I was stupid to reply like that.

Susami giggles again.

But she seems rather happy than amused.

“Well…my question is, do you like Rika Tachibana?”

To be honest, I disliked that word.

I like to go at my own pace, not on the same one as a normie.

“Uhh…I…”

“That’s right”

I just want to go home as soon as possible, but I can’t as she is right in front of me.

Susami was staring at my face with her hand on her chin.

“Do you know why I said that you don’t have to answer?”

“…No”

“I was sure you would be more honest with your face than your words. I was right”

“…What do you want to say”

“Nothing really, just one thing though.”

Suddenly, her expression softened.

The uncomfortable feeling that I had was gone, instead, I felt a warm and kind gaze.

A strange face that didn’t suit a girl of the same age.

“Rika might be like that, but she’s a good girl. So, please get along with her, I don’t care how you do it.”

“…Even if you say that, I won’t be getting involved with her anymore.”

Susami just gave a small nod.

She’s a bit strange.

But she isn’t a bad person.

Somehow, I get a similar feeling to that of Kyoya.

“Chitose”

 

Suddenly I heard a familiar voice coming from behind Susami.

She’s a bit short, but the overwhelming presence and good looks are undeniable.

 

It was Tachibana.

 

“Oh, how did you find out?”

“If you think for a bit, it’s obvious you’d be here.”

 

“Fufu, sharp as expected.”

“Hey, stop laughing. You’re troubling Kusuba-san.”

“Excuse me, Kusuba-kun. Am I troubling you?”

Susami’s gaze asks for consent.

But I decided to remain silent, at least with the intention of revenge.

“Chitose, let’s go.”

“Alright”

“I’m really sorry, Kusuba-san”

 

“Next time, control her properly.”

“Yes.”

Tachibana bowed his head and walked out of the gate while pulling Susami’s hand, who held no remorse at all.

After they were far enough that I couldn’t see them, I took a deep breath.

“…Is this how normies talk?”

 

Let’s just go to the public bath when I reach home.

Whenever something like this happens to you, that’s the best place to relax.

Let’s just eat the dish Tachibana had prepared.

 

If this gets finished, this time, our relationship will definitely end.

4.3

“Oh no…”

As soon as I got home from the public bath, I opened the refrigerator and found out something outrageous.

“…No…”

The bacon and vegetable curry stir fry and Nagimayo Chicken that Tachibana had made, had completed vanished,”

Who did it? Who ate it?

 

Of course…It’s me…

“Did I eat too much of it yesterday?”

 

Sadly, it’s finished.

It was so delicious…Well…now what’s the matter?

 

The cup noodles had also run out.

It’s troublesome to go out right now because it started to rain heavily just before I reached home.

The day gets worse and worse. First, meet with a weird beauty, then the rain right after a bath, and now I’m out of food.

“Hmm…”

I only have seasoned seaweed and packed rice.

…Can I eat that?

Let’s just eat what’s there.

It’s unhealthy, but there are times you have to do these things if you live alone.

If you decide to do so.

 

As soon as I closed the refrigerator, there was a flash and a tremendous roar that seems to shake the earth.

Lighting.

Really big too.

Seems like it fell somewhere.

Looking out the window, the rain was getting faster and faster.

Even the thunder is getting louder.

Even if it’s not that loud, there isn’t any sign of the rain stopping.

So, I took out the food for today.

I looked again, but there was no other thing to eat.

Looks like it’s time for a man to start cooking.

But, suddenly, the doorbell rang.

Who is it in such weather?

I was hesitant to go and thought to ignore it, but for some reason, I had a feeling I should open it.

“Good evening”

“…yes?”

Rika Tachibana was standing wearing her pajamas I had seen the other day.

The hem of her pajamas and lower legs were slightly wet.

“Ahh…I…”

Tachibana’s easy-to-understand speech became stagnant.

Being a straightforward person, it’s usual for Tachibana to act that way.

She was fidgeting as she thought, and her cheeks were slightly red.

“That’s right, I forgot my apron when I was cooking here!”

“Apron? I don’t have it.”

“Uhh…I’m still doubtful, I’ll confirm.”

Tachibana hurriedly said so and tried to slip by my side.

Even if she said that her apron isn’t here.

“No, it’s not there. I remember you taking it home. Did you check properly?”

“…Anyway-“

BOOM!

“Hyaaa!”

Tachibana suddenly screamed and clung to me.

Her petite body fit perfectly in my chest.

“Eh! Hey!”

I tried to tear away from her, but the trembling body grabbed me hard and wouldn’t let go.

My head comes to a conclusion for the situation.

In other words, the reason Tachibana came here is because she is afraid of lightning.

“…Maybe, you may have forgotten the apron”

“Eh?”

“Yeah, maybe I can’t remember properly. It still might be there, so you can look for it.

As I said that, Tachibana took a small peek with her face still buried in my chest.

Her complexion looks like that of a discarded cat.

I decided not to ask if she was afraid of lighting.

There are some things that a person wants to keep to themselves.

“…yes”

“Alright then.”

Tachibana then calmed down and released me as she was now relieved.

However, she grabbed my hem and slipped into my house.

Today is really strange.

…and would you please stop holding my hem?
