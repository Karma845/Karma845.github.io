Chapter 21 – Morning

Next morning.

Looking at Helvi’s face so closely, and being kissed so lustfully…

Having his thumb in his mouth, made him remember having Helvi’s tongue inside it.

(Helvi’s tongue was softer and warmer… No, stop! Get a hold of yourself…!)

“G-good morning Helvi. Hum, your wings and horns are showing.”

Seeing her demon-like features, Theo was once again fascinated by her beauty.

“…Do you like horns and wings?”

“No, I have no problem with that. So, what do you think?”

“Hum, it’s not like I like horns and wings, it’s just that you look so beautiful… I couldn’t look away…!”

Both faces were red, and both looked downwards a bit, but the corners of their mouths pointed up, as they thought about what was next.

(I get to see this form when we’re alone… An Helvi only I know…)

(Starting the day like this… Too cute.)

“Eh!? H-Helvi…!?”

(W-what do I do…!? I feel bad about wiping it, but I can’t leave it like this…)
