SS 3: Neneka

After school. I was being bombarded with questions from both Nao-chan and Harumi-chan.

The place was at the back of the school building where there were few people, but Harumi-chan was asking loudly and it was very embarrassing…

“Neneka-chan didn’t tell you because she thought that Harumi-chan would make a fool of herself if she did.”

“…!?”

Harumi-chan was immensely surprised when Nao-chan told her.

“I’m sorry. We’ve actually just started dating, and the reason I didn’t tell you is because I was… embarrassed. I was planning on telling Nao-chan and Harumi-chan one day.”

“Yeah…?”

I hurried to follow up, but Harumi-chan was shunned.

The two of us had talked about many things in our lives, and we had even confided secrets to each other.

And yet, if I kept it a secret that I had a boyfriend, it would be more than a little shocking. I felt bad too.

–I didn’t think that they would find out that I had a boyfriend at a time like that…

My face got a little hot when I remembered the day of the festival, when Daiki came to my rescue with a different hairstyle than usual.

“Oh, you were just thinking about your boyfriend, weren’t you?”

“What are you talking about, Nao-chan? I’m not thinking about him!”

“Huh? Really?”

Nao-chan looked at me, grinned and… immediately turned serious.

Nao-chan, whom even I, a girl, thought was beautiful, moved her face closer and asked in a whisper.

–“So, was your summer vacation mission a success?”

“Huh?”

“If you were talking about Neneka-chan and not your friend, then it was Neneka-chan who was worried about not being able to do what she wanted as a girlfriend, wasn’t it? Was my advice adopted? How did things go after that?”

“Let’s see…”

Nao-chan stared at me intently.

When I told her about a friend of mine who was worried because she felt like she wasn’t doing the things that were typical of a girlfriend, Nao-chan suggested that if she wanted to do things that were typical of a girlfriend, she should make her boyfriend’s dreams come true.

She gave me a lot of advice about how to dress to please boys, and I decided to cook dinner for Daiki in an outfit that incorporated those elements.

… But that failed along the way, and in the end I couldn’t do anything like a girlfriend.

It was embarrassing and hard to talk about, but Nao-chan did her best to give me advice, so I thought that I should talk about it properly when it already got here…

“I dressed up and cooked for him as per Nao-chan’s advice… But I screwed up and almost got burned while cooking, and then we didn’t seem like lovers at all. I’m sorry I wasn’t good enough… for your advice.”

“… Did your boyfriend tell you that Neneka-chan wasn’t like a girlfriend?”

“No, he didn’t tell me!”

“Then why are you so depressed?”

“That’s…”

Because I was struggling with self-confidence.

Because I wasn’t sure if I was the right girlfriend for Daiki.

When I pontificated about my problems, Nao-chan and Harumi-chan quietly nodded and listened.

I almost cried because I was so happy that they were kind enough to listen to me, even though I had kept quiet about my boyfriend for so long and they only found out about him today.

“I was so anxious, so I asked him, “Are you glad that I’m your girlfriend?”  He said, “Yes, I do.” But he gave me an insecure look that said, “Did I look like I didn’t think so?” I embarrassed him because I asked a strange question… I was having a hard time being like a girlfriend no matter what I did…”

This was the first time I had been able to talk to someone about it, and my emotions were running high.

As I was crying and talking, Harumi-chan gently patted my back.

“I thought, “It’s nice to be with someone I like, but I had a lot on my mind.”… I was alone all this time, and it was painful…”

“Neneka-chan…”

Then Nao-chan started groaning, “Hmm.”

She said, “Hmm… I see. I see… I guess my advice was misdirected.”

“… What?”

“Hey, Neneka-chan. Didn’t I tell you? “What is your goal?” Do you want to get closer to an ideal image of a girlfriend, or do you want to please your boyfriend more…? You said that you wanted to please your boyfriend more, but what you really wanted was to get closer to an ideal image of a girlfriend, didn’t you?”

I couldn’t quite understand what Nao-chan was saying, so I cracked my eyes open.

I wanted to make Daiki more happy… didn’t I?

“So let’s say, for example… that the most girlfriendly thing you could do was make your own bento. If you want to make your boyfriend happier, then it’s okay if he’s happy, right?”

“Yeah…”

“But for someone who wants to get closer to an ideal image of a girlfriend it isn’t just okay… if he’s happy. Of course it’s important if he’s happy, but they also cared if they made a good bento.”

Before I knew it, Harumi-chan was nodding along with me.

“These two are different goals, aren’t they? If your goal is to make your boyfriend happy, it doesn’t matter if your bento box is messy. It’s okay if your boyfriend smiles and says “Thank you.” No matter how happy your boyfriend is, if you think, “But I failed to make it half-way through, so I couldn’t make a proper bento… I’m a useless girlfriend.” Then the goal isn’t here”

“Oh…”

“I’ve heard Neneka-chan’s story, don’t you think that it’s not that your boyfriend wasn’t happy and you didn’t achieve your goal…? It just sounded like Neneka-chan was depressed that she couldn’t do what she wanted to do. She’s just worried because she’s not ideal, not because her boyfriend was disappointed with her, right? If you mix that up, you’ll never know what you really want.”

Hearing Nao-chan’s story made me think again about my summer vacation.

At the beginning of the summer vacation, I was mistaken for Daiki’s sister at the pool and became depressed because I was worried that he wouldn’t be able to see me as his girlfriend. Daiki said, “Is it something to worry about?” But I was worried that I was like his little sister.

When my parents went on a trip, I was determined to do something girlfriendly, but I couldn’t do what I wanted until the end, and I got depressed. Even though Daiki didn’t say that I wasn’t like a girlfriend… On the contrary, Daiki said, “… Neneka is my girlfriend, so I don’t think that it’s necessary to think about making you look like a girlfriend, right…?” But…

–All of it, I was the only one who cared.

When I thought about it calmly, I realized that I was only thinking about myself.

Daiki was always concerned about me, but I was only worried about myself.

“What am I going to do, Nao-chan… I’m the worst…”

“Wait.”

Kyan!

I was about to say, “I’m a lousy girlfriend,” when Nao-chan chopped me.

The top of my head hurt…

“So, Neneka-chan has too low self-esteem. No one said you were the worst. You just thought so yourself.”

“I-Is that so…?”

“Is that so…? No, that’s right! I don’t know much about Neneka-chan’s boyfriend, but from what Neneka-chan told me, I think that he loves Neneka-chan. I think you’re really loved, but because Neneka-chan always lacks confidence, your boyfriend thinks that he doesn’t love you enough and gets depressed, right? Imagine how you would feel if you were in the opposite side of this situation?”

“What if it was the other way around…?”

I guess it would be like he thought, “I love her,” but Daiki was unsure and wondered if it was okay for him to be my boyfriend.

Even if he tried his best to tell me, “No, no, don’t worry, I love you,” Daiki would always be worried, saying, “I can’t do this as I am.”

“… Wah, I might be depressed because I’m not sure how to convey my feelings…”

“Yes, yes, yes. You would be worried, wouldn’t you? Or “I’m telling you how much I love you, why can’t you understand?” You’re either angry. Well, from what I’ve heard, I don’t think Neneka-chan’s boyfriend is the kind of person who would be upset at such a time.”

“Hey, Nao-chan, how can you analyze relationships so well when you don’t have a boyfriend?”

Harumi-chan asked Nao-chan, to which Nao-chan replied, “Is it the result of reading and daily observation of people?”

But I felt that Nao-chan was the only one who could examine my love life so far just by reading and observing people on a daily basis…

“You’re really great… Nao-chan,” I didn’t even know myself at all. I was getting mixed up about whether I wanted to satisfy him or satisfy myself…”

“I think to some extent the two goals are intertwined, though, because sometimes you can’t be satisfied unless he is satisfied, and sometimes your satisfaction leads to his satisfaction. I just think that if the goal of the objective is different from the goal you are aiming for, like Neneka-chan just did, you will never be able to achieve your goal.”

“Yes…”

Maybe it was just a coincidence that Daiki’s son didn’t respond to me, not that he no longer saw me as his girlfriend. Or maybe Daiki was making an effort behind my back not to react. Whatever the case, I felt that I worried too much…

Otherwise… he wouldn’t have come to my rescue as my boyfriend on the day of the festival, would he…?

–That day, Daiki… was so cool…

On the day of the summer festival, I went to the yakisoba stall and was surprised.

With his bangs up, Daiki looked more mature than usual… I was so excited that I couldn’t even talk to him like I used to.

He followed me, admitted that he was my boyfriend in front of a classmate who said mean things about me, and told the classmate who was flirtatious, “I don’t need the contact information of girls other than hers.”

He protected me.

I was just so full of myself that I couldn’t see what was going on around me, and I was always taken care of by Daiki…

All in all, my self-esteem was too low.

Oh, I want to apologize for the trouble I caused to you, Daiki.

I wanted to say “I’m sorry” and “I love you” a lot.

Let’s stop worrying about whether I’m good enough to be his girlfriend. Let’s take Daiki’s words in stride.

Daiki was an honest person, so he didn’t say things that weren’t on his mind.

“Nao-chan and Harumi-chan, thank you for listening to me. I’m glad I could talk to you both about him.”

I laughed, and Nao-chan and Harumi-chan laughed too.

“I enjoyed our real-life love talk.”

“I only listened to Nao-chan’s story with you… Haha.”

“Harumi-chan was there for me, too. Harumi-chan, just being by my side cheers me up.”

“I know. Harumi-chan is always in good spirits.”

“In vain, it was superfluous!”

The three of us laughed a lot, and I went home feeling refreshed.

… On the way home, I received a message from Daiki saying, “I have something to talk about in my room at night.” I was about to reply with high tension.

Dangerous, dangerous… I needed to start by apologizing face to face and making up with each other properly.

Could I have lover’s time with… Daiki after that?

Once I got my own feelings in order, I suddenly missed Daiki.

I wanted sweet time with Daiki. I might be a little nervous… because we haven’t had much lover’s time lately.


–And then the night came…

Daiki’s face was more serious than ever.

“Well… actually… today, I got back the results of my competency test, and… it’s not looking good.”

“Huh?”

It was unexpected and surprising.

“So, sorry… I’m going to have to cut out lover’s time for a while and let myself concentrate on my studies. Of course, we’ll get along as brother and sister-in-law.”

“Yeah, no lover’s time is fine… But how long is a while…?”

“That’s right… two weeks or so.”

“Two weeks…”

“How about a new rule? “How about a two-week lover’s time ban… as a penalty for a significant drop in grades?”

Wha, it wasn’t long…

And two weeks might not be enough time to restore the grades that dropped in the first place.

Perhaps Daiki set it at two weeks so that I would not be lonely. In fact… he might want to take a longer period and concentrate on his studies without thinking about me…

“I will study hard in two weeks. I’ll go over everything I didn’t do well on the test. Can I ask for your cooperation?”

“Yeah… of course it’s fine. It’s a big time.”

“Thank you.”

Okay, that was all I could say…

I had only ever thought of myself and caused trouble. I had to properly put Daiki’s feelings first here…

“Then I’ll get right to studying.”

I was impatient because Daiki wanted to end the conversation immediately.

“… Umm, Daiki. You know…”

“Yeah?”

–Actually, I also have something I wanted to talk to you about, Daiki…

I tried to cut that off and couldn’t get the words out.

Was it okay to say now that I was sorry for being so selfish since summer vacation, or that Daiki did nothing wrong and I was just worried on my own?

No, talking about my problems would only get in the way of Daiki’s studies. I wanted to do something lover-like with Daiki after I apologized, but lover’s time was now forbidden…

“What happened?”

“Umm… nah, it’s nothing. I’ll study hard for the two weeks you’re studying hard. Let’s work hard for each other.”

“Oh!”

I’ll hold out for two weeks, too.

Let him concentrate on his studies and talk to him later.

I went back to my room, telling myself to relax, but I felt uncomfortable. I felt in agony because I couldn’t satisfy the feeling that I missed Daiki.

“I have to put up with… Daiki for two weeks…”

It was a strange human thing, when you felt like you had to put up with something, you wanted it more.

I hadn’t done lover’s time recently, but that didn’t make me feel lonely. And yet, I couldn’t believe I was going to be lonely at the same time that lover’s time was banned…

“Ah, I’m an idiot…”

Thus began my… two weeks of Daiki patience.
