Short Story – Neneka 2

I really wanted to take a bath again, but I was still getting used to my new home, so I didn’t feel comfortable taking baths multiple times.

I had no choice but to wipe my sweat off with a towel, then wipe my body with the sweat wipes I used at school, and then change into the clothes I wore for dinner.

The loungewear I got from Daiki’s mother was damp because I had put it on earlier when I was soaked with sweat.

“I’ll wash it tomorrow and wear it again.”

I hugged my beautiful floral loungewear tightly, then carefully folded it and placed it beside my pillow.

“Well… my response today was perfect, and I’m sure that Daiki had given me the benefit of the doubt.”

After getting under my futon, I decided to reflect on the incident earlier.

Tonight too, a change in status occurred in Daiki’s son.

Daiki looked very flustered, but this time I got dressed right away, so it should have been fine.

–As expected, when he asked me to end the summer, I asked him again because I didn’t know what he was talking about. That… was Daiki’s fault for using unique expressions as usual.

“Even so, I had been sweating a lot… What should I do… Maybe Daiki thought I was sweaty…?”

I hadn’t thought anything of it earlier, but the smell of my own sweat-soaked body on the futon bothered me. If he thought I smelled bad the moment I got out of the futon, I would be sad and unable to do anything about it.

Without a second thought, I smelled the loungewear I had just taken off.

It was probably because it had just been bought but it had a distinctive smell, like when it was still in the store. And mixed in with that smell was the smell of my own sweat. Maybe the smell was best described as a loungewear that had just been bought, or maybe because it was the rainy season, which made sweat easier to smell, but it was a very subtle… smell, at least not a bad smell.

I jiggled around on my bed for a while, then huffed and puffed and let out a long breath to calm down.

“Uh… it’s not necessary to ask a pervert on the internet… Daiki said, “My foolish son is in an early summer mood, and it’s having a lot of fun and getting carried away.” His son got carried away, so even if it smelled a little like sweat, it wasn’t an unpleasant smell, right…?”

Remembering Daiki’s words, I finally convinced myself.

It’s okay. Daiki wasn’t the kind of person who would say he hated me just because I sweated a little.

Moreover, here I noticed a terrible message from Daiki.

Let’s recall again what Daiki said.

He said this…

“… Sorry. My foolish son is in the mood for an early summer, and it got carried away again…”

What was noteworthy was the “My foolish son is in the mood for an early summer mood” part.

The “summer” here meant that Daiki and I would make love to the end. Therefore, it meant that his son was ready to make love, but he said that it was “a little early.”

–In other words, Daiki was telling me that we, as high school students, were not ready for that kind of thing… I was sure he meant that day would come, but for now we should hold back!

After all, Daiki was thinking about our future, my body, and many other things.

What a sweet boyfriend.

Daiki’s kindness made me happy and my heart ached.

“Daiki’s message… I received it properly. From now on, something might happen to his son again, but he would leave it alone, so I didn’t have to worry.”

Sometimes Daiki said some strange things, but I had gotten a little used to it.

I felt as if this was a sign that I had come to understand Daiki better, and I was very happy.
