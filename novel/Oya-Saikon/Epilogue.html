Epilogue – Neneka 3

He had lost his father when he was very young, and I thought that our circumstances were similar and that we could understand each other’s feelings better than anyone else. Because I had lost my mother in the past, I was sure that I could understand his heart to a certain extent.

So, it meant a lot to me to have met him. I guess it was a little too romantic to say that I met him… when I was meant to meet him.

Then, the third turning point came when my father remarried.

And when my boyfriend became my… Onii-chan… just two months after we started dating.

From living with my father to living with four people at once. Moreover, there was my boyfriend in the room next door.

Oh, I should have bought cuter loungewear.

I wondered if the air freshener in my room smelled okay.

I should have been more excited about the body cream, too.

The trouble was, I couldn’t relax even when I was at home. I never knew when or where we might bump into each other.

If it wasn’t my boyfriend living with me, I wouldn’t have bothered to prepare a wet towel before going to bed and wipe my face as soon as I woke up in the morning.

I didn’t want him to see my face when I woke up before I went to the… washroom to wash my face. And I didn’t want him to think even for a second that I had a dirty face!

Six in the morning. It was time for Daiki to wake up.

I had already washed my face and brushed my teeth.

I wondered whether I should change into my uniform first, but since I was wearing my favorite loungewear that my mother-in-law gave me, today I decided to go straight to the room next door.

As I slowly opened the door to his room, I could hear Daiki sleeping.

Thanks to the sunlight shining through the window, the room was dimly lit even with the curtains closed.

When I gently stepped into the room, I was wrapped in a slightly chilly air.

The air conditioner was left on.

Daiki was lying in bed, curled up in a blanket. He looked a little cold.

–I couldn’t help it anymore.

Sometimes, Daiki was a careless person.

He looked solid, but he easily lost track of the most important things.

But, that was what made him so cute.

Someone said it before. When… girls fell in true love, they didn’t look at their boyfriend and think that they were cool, they thought that they were cute.

I also noticed this when I started dating Daiki.

Every time I got to see him in a slightly bad way, I thought of Daiki as cute.

I read in a book that boys didn’t like to be told they were cute, so I refrained from mentioning it. But, I always shouted in my mind, “Daiki is cute.”

Gently and softly, I approached Daiki and crouched down by the bed and put my face close to his ear.

Then, I whispered softly and tenderly.

“Good morning. Onii-chan.”

“… Hmm?”

Daiki frowned and turned over.

Not only did he not get up, he rolled over with his back to me.

–Couldn’t wake up in one try?

I was going to give him a wake-up call with my sweet sister voice, but I failed.

It seemed that my gentle call didn’t pull him back from the world of sleep.

–In that case… there’s no other way.

“Yeah!”

Mugyuu.

“Huh!? What are you doing!?”

I hugged Daiki vigorously as I curled up on top of him, covered with a blanket.

… No, I think he woke up in pain from my weight. Yeah, I didn’t care either way.

“Beep, beep, beep. My sister clock is letting you know that it’s time to wake up, Onii-chan.”

While I was on top of Daiki, I told him by imitating a mechanical voice.

He had to get up, but he couldn’t because I was on top of him. It was so cute how he fluttered around in the blanket, making me want to hug him even tighter.

“Neneka…! What if our parents find you in my room this early in the morning and misunderstand something!?”

“It’s all right, it’s all right. I just came to wake up my sleepyhead Onii-chan. I’m a good sister, right?”

When I proudly said that, “Fufu,” Daiki laughed like he was troubled.

“Really… Neneka is really good at pretending to be my little sister.”

I felt sorry. I knew, I was being troublesome because I kept doing things that were going to make our parents find us out.

But, I… liked Daiki, who looked troubled like that, but in the end he forgave me.

I liked it so much that I wanted to… see that face again.

I felt sorry, I… was trying.

I apologized in my heart, but I couldn’t say it out loud.

I knew I was a little weird for wanting to embarrass him even though I liked him.

“I can pretend to be your sister so well because you are so much like an Onii-chan that I naturally become like a little sister.”

“Huh? Am I that much like a big brother? It makes me feel uneasy…”

Daiki looked anxious.

I didn’t mean to say anything bad, but what happened?

I got anxious too and asked Daiki.

“What are you worried about?”

“That’s… umm… Neneka will get used to my Onii-chan look, and eventually she would say things like “I can only see Daiki as my Onii-chan after all,” and our relationship will end. O-Or something…”

I couldn’t help but chuckle when I heard what Daiki considered to be a point of concern.

Cute.

“What are you talking about? Even if I call you “Onii-chan,” I will always think of you as my favorite boyfriend.”

When I expressed my feelings, Daiki’s face turned red with embarrassment.

In a muffled little voice, he said, “Thank you.”

 So cute. Daiki who worried like that was so cute.

–Oh my God, I couldn’t stop loving him more ever since we had been living together!

I wanted to kiss him, just on the cheek… just a little bit…

“Daiki… umm…”

Can I… kiss you?

I wanted to ask him that, but I was embarrassed and the words didn’t come out easily.

“Hmm? What?”

Daiki was looking at me.

Oh, I was sure he would notice that I was thinking about embarrassing things because of my red face.

If people knew that I wanted to kiss Daiki this early in the morning, they would probably think that I… was a naughty girl.

But, no.

It was something that I couldn’t stop thinking about.

–Chuuu.

I kissed… Daiki’s cheek.

Daiki kept blinking his eyes.

It sounded much, much better than I expected. I was practicing making a chu-chu sound… I guess he noticed.

“Neneka… umm.”

–Totto-totto-totto-totto…

Whose heartbeat was this?

No, no, it was footsteps!

Before the door opened with a bang, I withdrew from Daiki and moved away from the bed.

“Hey, Daiki! How long are you going to sleep…! Is that… Neneka-chan? Did you come to wake him up by any chance?”

The one who suddenly entered the room was, as expected, my mother-in-law.

My mother-in-law walked into Daiki’s room without knocking, so it was really bad for my heart.

“Ah, yes! As a little sister, I can’t overlook Onii-chan being late!! Look! Onii-chan! Your mother is here, so it’s about time you give up and wake up!”

“Oh… yeah…”

Daiki got up with an unnatural yawn. He was talking normally with me just a moment ago, but I guess he was faking the mood of having just woken up.

These parts were cute, too.

“You guys really get along really well. It seems like you were really brother and sister from the beginning. Then, you two come downstairs while I prepare breakfast.”

Mother-in-law smiled as she looked at us and left the room.

After checking for signs of my mother-in-law going down the stairs, I saw Daiki getting up from the bed and stand face-to-face with me.

And, almost at the same time, we bursted out.

“Safe!”

We both laughed.

Daiki laughed happily, which made me happy and I laughed more.

I’m sorry, Daiki.

I might pretend to be your sister again and play a prank on you, my boyfriend.

And today, we continue to use our position as brother and sister-in-law to secretly strengthen our bond as a couple…
