Hello everyone, I'm Jiou.

There is finally a book out.

I'm grateful for all the help I've received from everyone involved, and I've been sending waves of thanks all the time. I hope I'm not causing you to dream about me.

I think all published works are given a copy to the state.

As long as this country is still here, even if I am at the end of my life, my words and stories will live on. I feel a lot of emotion when I think about it.

It seems like ...... uses too many inflections.

That's right.

After writing for a long time, I seem to be prone to what I call "occupational disease". (I'm sorry for saying that.)

For example, I'd be very concerned about ending more than two lines in a row with "~oh" or "~ah", or having the same text too often, punctuation just right, etc.

Like this.

Tummy, hungry.

So hungry, to eat.

I feel like I'm on pins and needles when this happens ......

While I've often had the opportunity to write long essays before, like writing papers and organizing data for reports, I've never paid much attention to the arrangement of the words.

This new discovery is really interesting.

This is an interesting discovery.

Sorry for the inexplicable stuff in the afterword. Thanks again to those who bought the book.

This book got to readers thanks to everyone involved in helping to produce and sell it, and I can't thank you enough!
