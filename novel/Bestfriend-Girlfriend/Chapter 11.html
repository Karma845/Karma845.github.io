Chapter 11: Although I’m Happy, The Holiday Is Even More Fun

Finally, a holiday date with Yorka arrived.

I woke up even before my smartphone’s alarm went off, having prepared everything from clothes to essentials the day before. After changing, I checked the weather forecast on TV while having breakfast. It was a clear day today – the perfect weather for a date.

“Alright, the weather is perfect.”

“Hey, Kisumi-kun. Why are you up so early today?” asked Ei curiously as we ate breakfast together.

“I have a date with Yorka today.”

“Nice! I want to go too!”

“You have your extracurricular activity. Are you thinking of skipping them?”

“It’s not fair that only Yorka-chan and Kisumi-kun get to go on a date.”

“It’s not. Taking my little sister on a date during holiday is practically a punishment.”

“Yorka-chan would be happy.”

My sister asserted confidently, far too self-assured in her beloved character. Well, Yorka did like Ei, so it should be fine. However, at least on the first holiday date, I wanted to leave happy memories of the two of us alone.

After seeing Ei off to her extracurriculars and cleaning up after breakfast, the doorbell rang. It was Sayu.

“Good morning, Ki-senpai.”

“Morning, Sayu. What’s up?”

“I brought a little something as a thank-you for the souvenir you gave me last time. My mom baked cookies, so I came to deliver them.” Sayu handed me a paper bag. Inside, I found carefully wrapped homemade cookies.

“They look delicious as ever. Thanks.”

“No problem. The hot spring manju you gave me was delicious too. Thank you.”

After expressing her gratitude, Sayu scrutinized my outfit. “Are you going on a date with Yor-senpai? Looks like you’ve put a lot of effort into dressing up.”

“Well, yeah.”

“…Are you, by any chance, nervous?”

“Of course I am. Hey, do I look okay? Is there anything weird about my outfit?”

Having known each other for so long, Sayu saw through me effortlessly. I found myself instinctively seeking a fashion check from her.

“Even if it’s tacky, you can just let Yor-senpai coordinate and buy clothes for you, right?”

“How rude! Any advice that isn’t just snarky comments?”

“Hmph! You’ve at least cleared the minimum fashion standards. Anything more and my preferences would kick in!”

Surprisingly, she was genuinely considerate.

“Is that so?”

“It would be unpleasant if you show up on a date wearing an outfit coordinated by another girl’s taste. Especially if it’s Yor-senpai, she’d notice for sure.”

“My bad.”

“Don’t worry. Yor-senpai genuinely likes you.” Sayu reassured me, easing my tension with her straightforward approval. It was like the good old days.

“That’s true.”

“Aw, how romantic! Dates with a lover seem so fun!”

“Sayu.”

“What is it? Still want advice?”

“…Thanks.” Sayu lowered her eyes for a moment and then grinned. “Just make sure not to disappoint and get dumped right after the date starts.”

“I’ll be careful.”

“If the Sena club ever plans another gathering, be sure to invite me!”

“…Hey, can’t we drop that name? Can’t we at least go for Nanamura club?”

“Pfft! That wouldn’t make any sense. Your presence is what brings those people together, whether there are romantic feelings or not!”

When Sayu put it that way, I couldn’t really argue. Perhaps I was just imagining some unsettling undertones.

Naming relationships was actually quite a significant matter. Simply having a label created a stronger bond. I had been clear about my relationship with Yorka by announcing us a couple. Similarly, the gathering called “Sena Club” brought together Sayu and other students from different grades and strengthened their connections.

“Well then, please take good care of Yor-senpai!”

Cute, but somewhat cheeky, Sayu spoke with a smile as she left.

“Now, it’s time to go.” I walked alone to the local station. Riding the train, I reached the nearest station to our meeting place. I made my way towards our designated meeting spot while navigating through the crowded streets.

Within the bustling crowd, there was a strangely vacant space amidst the sea of people. Standing in the center was an exceptionally beautiful girl.

Arisaka Yorka was already waiting. Seeing Yorka outside of school gave me a refreshing feeling. It was the first time I had seen her in casual clothes, and they suited her exceptionally well. People passing by couldn’t help but be drawn to Yorka, regardless of gender.

However, Yorka stood there appeared somewhat lost. She was clearly not accustomed to being alone in such a busy area. I glanced at the wristwatch on my hand. There was still more than thirty minutes until our agreed meeting time.

“She couldn’t wait, huh?”

We were in the same boat.

Yorka suddenly looked up, and as soon as she spotted me in the crowd, a radiant smile bloomed on her impeccably crafted face.

I sprinted straight toward her. Today marked the long-awaited holiday date.
