“『Just four more AV shoots and I’ll be free.』”

However, she couldn’t endure it any longer.

Then, on the night she confided in Kanaya Chihiro that she was thinking of going to the police, she was abducted on the street and taken onto a ship bound for Southeast Asia.

My impression after hearing her story was──a terrible story. I couldn’t think of it any other way.

Well, I guess I’m just waiting for her sarcastic retort.

I glanced at Lili’s expression. She was floating around near the ceiling, seemingly oblivious to the conversation.

Honestly, I couldn’t quite grasp the intention. What did she want from me after telling me all of this?

“So…what is it that you’re asking from me?”

“Akira Mitsuki, President Kurashima, Hikami Kirito, and also Manager Yasunochi Kiyoka…I want you to bring them down to hell.”

“……”

Kanaya-san clenched her back teeth tightly.

“Hey, Lili, what do you think?”

While feigning disinterest, I deliberately turned the conversation to Lili.

In reality, I do feel sorry for her and would like to help her in some way but I can’t just go along with this devil’s intentions without understanding what she’s aiming for.

And it seems that stance was, indeed, the right one.

“Ahaha, it’s not that big of a deal, Devi. I just thought that using an entertainment agency…might be a convenient stepping stone for what lies ahead, that’s all, Devi.”
