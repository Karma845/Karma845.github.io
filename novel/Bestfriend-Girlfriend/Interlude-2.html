Interlude 2 - Something I'm able to Return

“I’m home.”

As soon as Yui entered the house, her father and mother came to greet her at the door.

“Welcome home, Yui. You’re back pretty late.”

The kind father looked relieved that his beloved daughter had returned safely.

“I-I’m sorry. Did I worry you?”

“No…Oh? Did you cut your hair?”

“Yes. My friend took me to a beauty parlour.”

When Yui said this, her mother smiled happily.

“Well, I’m glad that Yui has a friend like that.”

“Yeah. He’s my best friend. He’s very kind…and I love him♪.”

“Oh-oh. I can’t believe Yui said such a thing.”

“Anyway, you must be hungry. Would you like to eat?”

“Yes!”

And so, the three family members sat down at the table to eat their dinner together.

In it, Yui was happily recounting the events of the day.

“Then I went to get a haircut with my friend, oh yeah, we both did our makeup…and I also learned how to take care of my hair. Right away, today was the day I thought I’d give it a try.”

The father and mother were smiling and listening to their beloved daughter.

They were worried. At any rate, Yui had never been able to go to school very often, and she had a communicative disorder that made it difficult for her to talk to others.

They were afraid that she wouldn’t be able to make friends in high school or worse; she would get bullied.

However, she seemed to have made some good friends before entering high school. To be honest, they were a little worried when they were told that she was going to meet a friend on the internet, but they were relieved to hear that Yui was being taken care of.

Surely, it must’ve been a 〝Big sisterly girl〟, they imagined.

“But Yui. No matter how much fun it is to play with that friend, you shouldn’t stay up too late, okay? Especially with all the disturbances recently.”

“Hmm. But it’s okay. My friend always sends me home.”

“But then your friend would be alone until she got home, right? From what I’ve heard, she seems like a strong girl, but still, a girl walking alone at night is……”

“? The friend I’m talking about is a boy, though?”

The moment she said that, her father blinked in surprise.

“……Huh?”

“Eh? Didn’t I tell you? My friend, is a boy named Sugisaki Yuuma?”

“Oh, dear!”

At these words, her mother’s eyes sparkled as she covered her mouth, while the father’s mouth fell open for a moment, in complete contrast to mother’s actions.

After a few seconds, he composed himself and let out a loud cough.

“Wait, wait, wait. Um…in other words, what is it. So now you’re telling me that the boy you’ve been going out with to play every day is this Yuuma Sugisaki-kun?”‘

“Y-Yes, but…”

“You mean to tell me that this was the boy you’ve been hanging out with in a private room in an internet cafe, clinging your arms together as you walk back?”

“Y-Yes.”

“B-By the way, it didn’t go any further than that, did it? Like going to the other party’s house and so on…”

“Yes, I did, but…”

Finally, the dad froze.

The appearance of Yui was like saying, “What’s going on?”

“Ah, um…what was it. Yui, the boy, known as Sugisaki Yuuma, do you like him?”

“? Yeah, I like him.”

“I-I see. W-Well, Yui will soon be a high school student. But, um….going to the other party’s house…that wasn’t a mistake, was it?”

“A mistake?”

Yui tilted her head at her father, who was acting strange.

And then she thought about it for a moment…and started to flutter when she realized what her father was worried about.

“It’s not like that, okay? Yuuma is my friend, and when I say I like him, I mean as a friend, and I only went to his house because it was raining and I got wet, so I borrowed his shower…”

“You showered at a man’s house!?”

“T-That’s why Yuuma’s just my friend, not like how father thinks….!”

“…..Kohon. Yui.”

Yui’s father cleared his throat and started talking with a mysterious look on his face.

“Listen, Yui? Yui is a girl, and not long later, you’ll be a high school student. Thus, you’ll be a little defenceless, so you have to start being wary of men…”

“U-Uh, huh.”

“And this Yuuma seems to be very kind to Yui, but there has always been a saying that a man is a wolf. In most cases, men are usually kind to women when they have an ulterior motive, and even if they seem kind, sometimes…”

“Honey.”

Yui’s father stopped speaking when her mother, who had been silent up until now, spoke.

Yui had a sulky look on her face, and she was looking grumpier than ever.

“Yuuma is my best friend.”

“Y-Yui? Ah, no. By no means did I ever mean to speak ill about Yui’s friends, okay? Dad was just worried about Yui…”

“Yuuma would never do anything bad to me.”

Yui abruptly turned away with her cheeks puffed out.

Her father, who had never seen such a reaction before, suffered tremendous damage. He already felt devastated.

“Thank you for the food. I’m going to take a bath.”

While still in a sulky mood, Yui put the dishes away.

†

“Huff.”

Soaking in the bath, Yui looked up at the ceiling and exhaled pleasantly.

She liked baths. It was supreme bliss for her to soak around aimlessly in the bath, playing around with her smartphone (completely waterproof) and thinking about fun things.

However, she was feeling a little uneasy.

As far as it goes, she understood in her head why her dad was worried.

But more than that, Yuuma was her best friend, her special someone…and having her special someone badmouthed by someone, especially by her father, made her act cold towards him. She’ll have to apologize to him afterwards, she thought.

“…………”

Looking back on the days she’s spent with Yuuma so far.

She made friends with him through the game, and ever since she first met him, they would play together every day, and he’s treated her kindly…

Until now, she was never aware of love or anything like that. She enjoyed her days so much with Yuuma that she didn’t even have time to think about it.

But when her father said it like that, as one would expect, it made her think a little.

(Maybe Yuuma…really likes….me…..?)

Yuuma was always very kind to her. As far as it goes, they were boy and girl, and he frequently told her she was cute, so it wasn’t impossible.

If that’s the case, how would she feel?

──As for Yuuma directing such feelings at her, she didn’t really hate it.
But….

Yui also liked Yuuma. She loved him greatly. She wanted to be with him even more and get to know him better.

However, Yui liked him as a best friend.

Or rather, she didn’t even know what love was since she never had a first love in the first place.

They say that when you fall in love, you’re heart pounds, and it’s painful to be together with the person you love.

However, when she stayed with Yuuma, she felt relieved.

Being close to him gave her peace of mind, and it felt good having her head stroked by him. She wanted to be pampered and doted on a lot.

(If Yuuma was a girl, I would have felt comfortable staying close and sleeping together without reservation….)

She immersed her face half in water while giving off bubbles as she thought about such things.

But in reality, she was a girl, and Yuuma was a boy.

They were men and women, so it was possible that they would feel such feelings towards one another.

But….if that’s the case…..

(If Yuuma liked me…would he be happier if I went out with him…..?)

Suddenly, she thought of that.
