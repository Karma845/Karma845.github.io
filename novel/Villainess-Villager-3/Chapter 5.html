“Mother!? Why are you here?”

“Are you surprised? Well, it’s good to see Allen’s surprised face.”

The Duke said in a matter-of-fact manner.

“And today we’re having dinner together because Katerina-san is going to be family, too.”

The Duke wrinkles his brow when I say that.

This is how I decided to go see that pervert.
