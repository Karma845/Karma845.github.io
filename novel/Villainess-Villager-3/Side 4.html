Side – Anastasia (1)

I unleashed the most powerful anti-personnel magic available indoors and stabbed Leonardo to pieces.

“Allen! Allen! hold on!”

“Ugh, Anna, sorry.”

“It’s okay, hold on!”

“Anna, blood …… on your hands.”

Allen is concerned about others, even in a state like this.

“I don’t care about that. Don’t die! Hey! In a place like this! Hey! Allen!”

Even though Allen was able to ask for me with great trouble!

And, finally, we were supposed to tie the knot!

Something like this! Something like this!

Oh, God!

I can see the light slowly fading from Allen’s eyes.

No! Don’t die! Don’t leave me alone!

My face is probably a mess of tears.

“Allen! Don’t die! Don’t leave me! Allen! Allen!”

Then, Allen’s body suddenly lost power.

“Aaaaaaaaaaahhhhhhhhh!”

Anyone. Please! Heal Allen! I don’t care what happens to me! Please!

[ O you who seek guidance! I recognize your strong desire and grant you strength. ]

“Eh?”

And the next moment, a brilliant light enveloped my body.

“Ah, ah, this, this is …”

The moment that light subsided, I was suddenly convinced.

I pull out the piece of chair that is stuck in Allen’s body and cast a spell.

“Buried in the ice are the sprouting seeds. Melted water is a blessing that brings life. O Holy ice, I command you in the name of Anastasia Clynel von Ramslett ・ Heal my Allen’s wounds ・ Holy Ice Heal.”

I don’t know why I know this magic. Why ice magic can even cure is also something I don’t know.

But I am convinced that I can save Allen in this way.

Thus, my magic covered Allen’s body with ice, and soon Allen’s body was completely trapped in the ice.

“My Lady! Allen-dono!”

“Th-this is-“

“I’ll explain the situation later! Those guys are getting away. Get them now! I don’t care if you have to kill them.”

“Ha-hah!”

Then the soldiers chased after the fleeing Crown Prince and the others. Afterwards, with father and others, who came later, I returned to my room with Allen, who I had put on ice.

“Why did you arrange a meeting? And what were the guards doing!?”

“Well, about that. I don’t know why, but when that woman said it to me, I felt like that for some reason.”

My father is questioning the soldier who was on guard, but his answer is not making any sense.

“At first, I laughed when that woman referred to herself as a Saint, but then I began to feel that she was right….Also, His Highness the Crown Prince, ah, the royal family of the Centraren Kingdom, said that it’s natural to talk to the classmate from the same school….”

“Is that why you had them meet?”

“Y-Yes.”

Father held his head and let the soldier leave.

“What the hell is going on? He would not have been foolish enough to make that decision. That’s why I left it to him…..”

“Father, Allen called that woman a witch. From the looks of it, he was using a Blizzard Phoenix magic stone, so he was probably doing a character appraisal.”

“Witch …”

Then, after a pause, my father changed the subject.

“And Anna, what is this all about? Why did you put Allen on ice?”

“That is …… I heard a voice from the staff that woman was holding, and then I was surrounded by light. Well, I don’t know, but somehow I was convinced that if I did this, I could heal him.”

I look at the block of ice that enclosed Allen.

“Indeed, the wound seems to be healing, but……”

Yes, most of the wounds that were so severe have already closed up, and the pieces of the chair that may have remained in Allen’s body seem to have been expelled from his body.

“I have never heard of ice healing magic. And what is with that staff?”

“That woman said that the staff was the sign of a Saint and that she was recognized by this staff, and had become a Saint.”

“But in Allen-kun’s character appraisal, she was a witch.”

“Yes, maybe….”

Father thought for a while and then suggested to me.

“Anna, let’s check your status. Something might have changed.”

“Yes.”

Thus, I was to have my status appraised, and crystals for appraisal were brought to me. This is a very expensive magical tool, but every noble family has one of these in their house.

Even without the adventurer’s guild card, this crystal can be used to find out what blessings, skills, etc. one has.

Of course, this could be done by registering with the guild, but aristocrats do not want the guild to have their information, so they do this to check for themselves.

I held my hand over the crystal and there it appeared with surprising results.

“Ah, umm? Father? What is this?”

“What? What the hell is going on? Ice Saint? 【Holy Ice Magic】? Moreover〖Sky Knight〗!?”

“Ah.”

I suddenly understood what had happened.

“I am sure that this staff, this staff gave me the strength I needed to save Allen”

“This staff is… isn’t it a dangerous staff that creates a witch?”

“But I can only assume so.”

The next moment, the ice that sealed my Allen cracked. Then immediately the ice shattered with a sound.

“Allen!”

I move my body, which is still not well, as hard as I can to get to Allen.

His body was cold, but I could hear his heart beating, and as I held him tightly, I could feel the warmth slowly returning to his body.
