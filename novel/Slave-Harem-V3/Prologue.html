Prologue

　One cannot choose one's birth.

　You cannot choose your parents, your location, or your race.

　You cannot choose your looks, your talents, or even the world.

　But you can choose how you live.

　This story is just a story of living with a loved one and describing ordinary events until death.

　It is just one of the many acts in the lives of those who walk toward the end they desire.


　Life is too long to live alone, and the world is too big to explore alone.

　That is why people seek companionship. They want someone who understands them. That's all there is to it.
