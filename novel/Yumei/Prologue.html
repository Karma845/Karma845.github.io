Prologue

What happened to Misaki may be something I will never forget. The day she came to the library—

Honestly, at first, I was afraid of her.

Because she and I are diametrically opposed people.

She is cheerful, indifferent, free-spirited, a native of a bright world that is completely different from mine.

Her bright smile fascinates me, like burning eyes.

Well. In my heart, she is like the sun.

And this was the record of the alliance made with her like that, until it fell apart.
