Confinement - Day 11

As scheduled, I continue to draw my manga.

At first, like yesterday, I drew some non-fiction daily life manga. But drawing one story will always be boring. So I began to consider adding fantasy and SF or Science Fiction. But the idea is not that good together.

Because of this, I got myself get back to my work that I almost forgot the time for dinner.

The time has finally come. Where the girl will reveal what she cook this time.

It was served with the same tray as before, with a pottery plate and fork on top.

She usually stays outside the room while I eat.

But, today she sat in front of the cardboard box, staring at me with absorbed expectation.

I understand how she feels.

It must be the same feeling when the time I waited for her to comment on my first manga work.

I stared at her cooking and starts to inspect it.

(This is.... Um what should I say..... Is this japanese food or foreign food?)

How will I say this to her?

Overall, the main course is two baked pancakes.

However, it was so burnt that for a moment I thought it was baked rice.

A side dish that looks like something like scrambled eggs.

There is a reason why I don't know where did this food come from. Firstly.

The scrambled eggs are covered with a tomato sauce that could be mistaken for a murder scene.

I can't even tell what is the sauce in this course. Is it the tomato or the scrambled egg?

If it is normal to make a scrambled egg, it should not be like this.

I've tried to make omelettes rice before, but I failed and ended up spreading a bunch of ketchup to cover up my failures.

That's probably how she also try to cover up her failures.

Finally, there is the apple for dessert. And there is no doubt that it is an apple.

Normally apple's is shape like an rabbit but now it looks like piranha.

I guess it was intended to look like a rabbit, but the actual product was not like that.

Even if it's a rabbit, it's still a zombie rabbit.

The plates brand-name is pottery from England or probably somewhere, so the two look even worse together.

"Here it is."

"I'll eat now."

I reached for my fork and the young girl stopped me.

"Are you going to feed me? I'm not a child anymore......"

"It's my duty as the chef."

The young girl said, grabbing my fork.

The girl stabbed a piece of pancake in one fork and started to bought it in my mouth

Frankly, probably it's even better than pancake bought in store, but of course let's not expect.

"Well, then, I'm counting on you."

I opened my mouth.

The young lady grabbed my chin with her left hand and shoved the pancake into my mouth.

...... Is it sweet or salty?

No, it's a bit too salty.

However, if you treat it as a dessert, it is not recommended.

"---and here's the egg."

She said while pointing the scrambled eggs into my mouth.

In all likelihood, the name of the dish should be scrambled eggs, but it would be rude to her to say it's wrong. So I chose to say the 100% correct egg.

"It's good."

The young lady put the scrambled eggs (presumably) on the fork and shoved them into my mouth again.

If you want to ask whether there is a taste, there is, I think...

After all, the tomato sauce itself is a delicious seasoning, so the overall quality of the dish is barely palatable, regardless of how well the eggs are made undercooked.

Some eggshells were mixed in, but that's nothing compared to the pineapple thorns.

The rest were dessert apples, which she grabbed with her hands and shoved into my mouth.

There is nothing to say about the taste.

It's just plain apple flavor. Normally eaten by other people.

I finish eating safely without incident.

The overall rating of the dishes cooked by the young girl is──

"It was delicious."

I muttered to myself as if I were talking to myself.

I tried to put poker face as much as I could and worried that she might tell if she notice it.

"Yes, it was delicious. Thanks for the hospitality."

She stared at me intently, as I praised her again and after finishing my meal.

If you judge her casually, you will make her feel that cooking is meaningless.

In fact, I think it's not bad to be able to cook like this for the first time.

It's just that if it's she cook like this every day, she will probably improve. However, compared to yesterday's pineapple, there is indeed an improvement.

"It's just a light meal."

Along with the usual expression, the young girl wiped the ketchup from my lips with her index finger.

Then, she pull her mask out slitting a little, and licked her fingertips.

I was humiliated to be treated like a child, but in that moment I was so focus on her face. And she's extraordinarily beautiful.

The young lady stood up with the tray as if nothing had happened and turned away.

"Ahhh. I haven't finished my manga yet....."

I said with a slight apology.

"No need to repeat it, I already see it."

The young lady turned her back on me and said calmly.

Contrary to her tone of voice, and her footsteps seem to be very brisk.
