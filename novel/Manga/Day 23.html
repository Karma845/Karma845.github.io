Confinement - Day 23

"I'm already full. Today's food also delicious. Thank you for the meal."

After an elegant breakfast of French toast and broth, I folded my hands.

"It's just a light meal."

The girl is smiling slightly while picking up my finished plate, and left the room.

(Hmm... Since I'm full maybe I should take a nap then go back to work.)

I lay down on my bed and close my eyes.

Since my mind already in conscious of sleep I totally forgot about my manga and drifted myself in sleep.

*Swish, swish, swish, swish.*

"Hmmm...?"

I felt an itchy touch on my arm, so I braced myself.

When I opened my eyes, I saw a small teal spider.

I shrugged it off without even thinking about it.

The spider that fell to the ground wandered around doing nothing.

(I want to put the spider outside.)

I reached out to the spider and glanced at the window - and then I noticed.

I obviously can't because I'm chained.

I naturally forget about it.

(Oh, wait? Isn't this bad? I'm getting used to this kind of treatment and starting to turn into a useless person.)

I suddenly turned pale.

If I think about it calmly, am I in a pretty bad state of mind right now?

My relationship of trust with the girl is indeed growing and my living environment is improving, so I don't feel the need to escape from here.

In the second day of imprisonment I have been looking for the possibility of escaping, but until now I have not made even one attempt to escape.

(Well, Maybe I should try to think a escape plan for now.) I thought to myself.

"Oh just like others says. All day sitting in work is tiring."

Firstly, I'm going to pretend doing excercise to test the limits of the chain length.

If I want to reach the window. The best excercise is sit up.

(It really doesn't work.)

Not enough to reach the window.

Although the length of the chain is much longer than the first day of imprisonment, it's still not enough to reach the window unless it is twice longer.

(How about entryway..... Nope impossible.)

Since the entryway was kitchen that's where the girl is always cooking. So it's no doubt that challenging escaping there is impossible.

But even though the length of the chain can reach toilet and kitchen, it's still not enough to reach at doorway outside.

(Lastly, breaking the chain seems impossible too.)

There are no tools here enough to break this strong chain after all.

Smashing it on the walls will make noise. And if I try to break it with fork, it may result to injuries too.

(Then, how about shouting for help?)

This is probably the best way to ask for help but it's still seems impossible.

Assuming my voice will reach outside, someone might call for the police. However before the police will come, the girl might injure me unintentionally because just like I observe, she's already clumsy enough to injure me unintentionally. And I will probably end up like the rabbit apple.

(And if the police broke here and sees me injured by the girl, she will be likely perpetrator.)

This is the biggest reason why I shouldn't do shouting plan.

(Wait, Isn't more simple if I shout when the girl is outside shopping.)

I can't help but to sigh.

But the girl must be going outside when I was asleep, so in the end there was no chance at all.

(Night Ambush might work. I'll just wait for the time when she's outside then I'll try to sneak.)

In the current situation, it is impossible to escape by my own power, so it is better to think more options.

(Or should I just wait when the girl change her mind about imprisoning me.)

Such a vague hope.

(Maybe she's also having trouble on school that is why it's relaxing for her being here. So maybe I should just focus on my manga....)

In the end I didn't make any progress just like in past 10 days.

However, there is something different from before.

The manuscript of my manga is 80% finished.

However, I hit a bottleneck in the climax and couldn't figure out how to end it.

(There is a little bit more, there is a little bit more to finish.....)

My consciousness drifted away from the escape plan and crossed my mind on my imagination.
