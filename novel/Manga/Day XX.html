Confinement - Day XX

Many days have passed. I just keep drawing and drawing my manga.

Under the torment of fatigue and lack of sleep, my consciousness gradually becomes blurry.

While fighting this, I'm already at climax where the story is mixed with 2D fiction and reality.

Sooner, my current "now" will connect to the "now" that is drawn on the paper.

There will be no proper happy ending nor bad end. The series wil not get proper ending, it's just processing of daily lifes of two people.

If I were participate in Newcomer Award, I would undoubtedly lose.

Now, the story ends with poignant but tender life of the main character.

"I would like to see the girl again, if possible." I put down the pen while adding understandable idea.

(Done, now this is my new manga.)

I sent the comic I drew for the only person who matters to me to her account.

(I don't know if the girl will read it. Even if she reads it, I don't know if she will come back.)

In the end I'm just manga artist to the bone.

In that time, I can't think any ideas about my new manga. There's nothing I can't do. But now, I'm sure I can do it now.

After that, my body sense a strong feeling of fatigue, as if the Earth's gravity suddenly increased.

Then I lay down at my bed at once.
