“You can choose which you prefer. Drink it and be saved, or not drink it and die…after all, it’s all up to you.” 

While only pain spreading through his body, Domiro’s voice reached Dill’s ears.

And Dill, despite his hesitation, grabbed that bottle with his shaking hand.
