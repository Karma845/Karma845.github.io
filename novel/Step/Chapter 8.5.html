Chapter 8.5: August 25th (Wednesday)

Today is the opening ceremony for the second semester. I was so nervous that I woke up early.

When I saw students wearing the same school uniform as me on the station platform, I suddenly felt really nervous.

It seemed like Aniki noticed too, but he just walked silently beside me.

After leaving the station, we ran into Aniki's friends.

The older brother Ueda Hikari, a second-year senior, and his sister Ueda Hinata. Hikari was tall and handsome, but his blond hair and earrings made me a little scared. I found it strange, wondering how he became friends with Aniki.

Hinata had a cheerful personality, and she was cute, giving off a girlish vibe. She gave me the impression of being a very astute younger sister.

We exchanged contact information right away and became friends. And to my surprise, we were in the same class!

Practicing self-introductions with Aniki finally paid off, barely passing!

During lunch break, when I was eating lunch with Hinata, people started crowding around us.

I felt really uncomfortable, like a panda at the zoo.

Everyone bombarded us with questions, and I had no idea how to respond.

Just when I didn't know what to do, Aniki came to my rescue.

After school as well, when the scary third-year seniors tried to approach us,

Aniki wasn't afraid at all and directly called them out.

That was the first time I saw such a scary expression on Aniki's face.

It was completely different from his usual demeanor, and it was really terrifying...

But after the third-year students walked away, he gently stroked my head. Aniki was indeed very gentle.

His touch was warm and comforting, making me want to stay by his side.

But is Aniki only gentle to me because I'm his sister? Or is it because I'm a girl?

How does Aniki really see me?
