And this incident happened after I had such thoughts.

As high school students, it was only natural for us.

Or should I say, it was something I unilaterally decided to do...

"I can't pinpoint where exactly, it's all over your mouth."

"Huh? Are you kidding? Is it true?"

Akira picked up a tissue and wiped, but the tomato sauce unexpectedly clung stubbornly. He wiped it off, but it spread to the side.

"Turn this way for a moment... There, now it's gone."

I wiped it off with a wet tissue for him, but his face turned red to the roots of his ears, and he quickly turned away.

Was it because we were in high school and he felt embarrassed for making such a cute and childish mistake, as if he was losing face?

"Th-thank you..."

"Really, you were too focused on playing video games, weren't you?"

Although I had a smile on my face, seeing his shy expression made me feel embarrassed as well.

Looking at him like this, I realized that Akira was really good-looking.

Not only good-looking, but I could also see the changes in his expressions, which made me feel happy.

Although he used to be unsmiling and not very likable, now he could smile, show disdain, get sulky, get angry, and even become shy like he did just now. His expressions became much more expressive.

From a positive perspective, he had let down his guard against me. As his older brother, seeing him without defenses made me very happy. So...

"...Akira, I'm glad to have you as my sibling."

I blurted out my current feelings.

"W-why are you suddenly saying that..."

Rather than being shocked, he seemed more embarrassed.

"That's how I truly feel. Without you, my summer vacation would just be me wasting time alone."

In fact, since Akira and Aunt Miyuki came to this house, the atmosphere had become much brighter than before.

Recently, we were able to naturally get along with each other instead of seeking a balance.

The new life that initially made me anxious had now revealed its true nature, and it only made people laugh and say, "Oh, so that's how it is?"

"E-even if you don't play with me, you can find someone else, right? Don't you have friends? You've been with me for the whole summer vacation, right?"

"Well, I do have some, but not many."

I accidentally let my vanity get the better of me and said "not many." When it came to friends, I only thought of Koukei.

Thinking about it, if it weren't for him, I might have been left alone at school.

"But you seem like you should have many friends..."

"You're overestimating me. Rather than making a lot of friends, it's better to have a few close ones..."

--That's what we call the theory of being a loner. Simply put, I'm not good at interacting with a large number of people, but it's easier for me to be with a select few.

In that sense, it was much easier to hang out with Koukei, who I've been acquainted with since junior high.

As for Yōko... I still felt a little awkward.

"Be-because you're my brother, right? Ahahaha..."

Since we've opened up about this topic, I've finally made up my mind.

"Alright! How about we take a bath together, Akira?"

I thought that the only way to bridge the distance between us was to suggest doing something I had decided to do before.

"Eh... ehhhhhhhhh?!"

Precisely because we're siblings. Occasionally, I go to the public bathhouse with our father and help him wash his back."

Exactly. Akira, when you went on school trips in elementary and middle school, how did you take baths?"

"Did everyone go to the public bathhouse together?"

"No... we bathed in the hotel rooms, filled the bathtub with water, and took turns washing..."

"Have you ever been to a hot spring or sauna?"

"I haven't..."

I knew it.

Akira probably wasn't accustomed to seeing other people's bodies.

Closing the distance between Akira and me is certainly important, but I'm also concerned about this aspect.

"In the future, our whole family might go on a hot spring trip, and there will also be school trips in high school. We won't always be able to take baths in our own rooms. If you don't accumulate the experience of bathing with others now, it might become troublesome later on."

"But... I still can't! If Uncle or Mom finds out..."

"Why are we bringing Dad and Mom into this? It's just a small matter."

"Is this considered a small matter?!"

Even if they were to find out, I could imagine their reaction.

"I guess Dad and Aunt Miyuki would just think that our bond has become stronger, right?"

"No, no, no, no, I'm telling you, it's not that simple... If they find out, it won't be good..."

It seemed that Akira was quite particular about this.

But since it wouldn't be good if we got caught, let's just not get caught then.

"Then let's treat it as our (sibling) secret and not let them know. Anyway, Dad won't be coming home today, and Aunt Miyuki will be back very late. It's not a problem."

"Our (sibling) secret..."

"Yes. If you really don't want them to know, let's go with that."

Akira continued to rub his left elbow with his right hand, his face flushed, and finally, he raised his gaze to look at me.

"Aniki... do you like me...?"

"Huh? Yeah... of course, I like you."

I felt that my mood had become strange, but I still answered that way.

Of course, what I meant was "liking you as a family," without any deeper meaning. But for Akira, it seemed to be important.

"If we get caught... will you take responsibility?"

"Of course. If anything happens, I'll take responsibility."

"............"

Perhaps my way of inviting him was a bit forceful.

But I won't force him. If he doesn't like it, I'll give up...

"Then, alright. If it's just washing each other's backs..."

Akira reluctantly agreed... or rather, he couldn't resist.

In the literal sense, I'm feeling a bit nervous right now.

My intention, of course, is not only to get closer to Akira, but also to think about his future.

In the past, Akira would run away at the sight of my naked body. If he could develop some immunity through this opportunity, I would be glad.

"Um... sorry to disturb..."

The bathroom door opened, and Akira finally entered.

"Hey. We'll start quickly!"

"Understood...."

Why does he sound so formal? At first, I thought that, but then I realized that Akira must be nervous too.

But as long as everything goes smoothly this time, perhaps we can go to the public bath together in the future or occasionally wash each other's backs at home.

Once humans compromise once, it becomes a habit after the second or third time, and they start thinking, "If it's just this, then it's okay."

So next time, Akira shouldn't resist like he did today.

While I was thinking about these things, Akira slowly squatted behind me.

The mirror in front of me had already fogged up, so the view behind me was blurry.

But I could easily imagine the expression on Akira's face.

I guess he must be enduring his embarrassment. I'll try to ease his nervousness a bit.

"By the way, Akira. Have you ever helped someone wash their back before?"

"Maybe when I was little, I helped Dad wash his back..."

"Do you remember how to do it?"

"No, I don't really remember..."

"Then start by scooping water from the bathtub and pouring it over my back."

"O-okay, then...?"

"You can pour it all at once, like this, splash──"

"Mm, okay..."

Akira continued to do so.

When the hot water rushed over my back all at once...

"Mm... feels so good~!"

My inner thoughts slipped out unintentionally.

"Please! Don't make weird sounds, Aniki!"

He poured the water a second and third time, and the hot water spread from my back to my whole body.

"Okay..."

Then I heard the sound of squeezing the shower gel from behind, followed by the sound of lathering.

─Is it finally happening?

The long-awaited moment of enjoyment is about to begin─

If that's the case, you should have used the washcloth from the beginning...

"Using your hands to wash? Oh, there's a blue washcloth over there, right? Use that."

"Uh, okay..."

With the washcloth lathered in shower gel, Akira placed it on my back.

"So, should I start scrubbing?"

"Of course, go ahead!"

As for the strength... it was incredibly gentle.

It felt like he was caressing my back, no different from when he used his hands to wash.

"Akira, my back is not that fragile."

"Yes, it's fine. So, put in some more strength and scrub in one go!"

"Alright, I got it..."

With my back at the center, Akira placed the washcloth below my neck.

And then, using his arm strength—or rather, putting some of his weight on my back—he proceeded to...

"Yikes!"

"Oh wow!"

It seemed that in the moment he exerted force, his hand or foot slipped, and Akira's weight ended up directly on my back.

I tried to support my body from falling forward, barely managing to hold Akira up, but...

...what is this sensation?

I felt something soft collide with my shoulder blades.

That soft thing, along with Akira's weight, pressed against my back, causing the area of contact to expand. However, at the same time, it retained its elasticity. I could feel it resisting, trying to regain its original shape.

In the next moment, as that thing made contact with my back, it separated into two.

When my back registered this series of movements, an indescribable palpitation surged within me.

I couldn't bear that palpitation, and my body leaned forward.

But that only made the situation worse.

"Yikes!"

As I leaned forward, Akira's body pressed even closer to me than before.

And the soft sensation on my back expanded along with it.

At that moment, a revelation... or rather, an overwhelming epiphany...

Knowledge, experience, instinct, imagination, science, mystery... all these things converged in an instant, transforming that indefinite soft object into a clear answer in my mind.

And now, I understood it clearly.

Without a doubt, that thing was none other than a "breast."

Akira moved away from me, and a brief silence filled the space between us.

It was only when a droplet fell from the ceiling into the bathtub that I snapped back to reality.

"...Um, Akira-sama? May I ask you something...?"

I was the first to break the silence.

"What, what is it? And why are you speaking so respectfully...?"

Embarrassment and confusion.

I didn't know which came first, but these feelings spread through me like a toxin from the top of my head to the rest of my body. My senses were numbed, and my heartbeat reached its limit.

I struggled to calm myself down first.

After regaining my composure, a question that needed clarification emerged.

"I'm sorry to ask this, but may I inquire... Akira-sama, aren't you not my younger brother, but my younger sister?"

As soon as the words left my mouth, everything that had happened from the moment I encountered Akira up until today flashed before my eyes like a revolving lantern.
