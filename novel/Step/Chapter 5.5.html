Chapter 5.5: August 11th (Wednesday)

It has been three weeks since I came to this family.

Today, Uncle is not home because of work, and Mom won't be back until late at night.

I had pizza and played video games with Aniki. He said he's happy to be siblings with me.

At first, I thought it would be difficult for me to get along with a boy other than Dad, but when I started to think about it, I realized that it was me who actively approached him.

It feels like a remarkable change.

Aniki said he wants to get closer.

Seeing that Aniki really values me like this, I feel happy and embarrassed at the same time.

But even if we're close, taking a bath together is just weird...

Even though he said it's just washing each other's backs, we're already high school students.

Do normal siblings take a bath together? And we're not blood related...

I found it strange, but now I understand...

I never thought Aniki actually believed I was his younger brother...

Is that even possible? We've been living together for three weeks, is this kind of misunderstanding reasonable?

But even before, I thought Aniki was strange, and now I know for sure that he's just a bit slow.

He's just like Uncle, a person who makes you feel a little sorry. How should I face him with what expression from tomorrow onwards?

Is Aniki disappointed after knowing I'm his younger sister?

Will he be more cautious around me starting from tomorrow?

I don't want that. I want to maintain the current relationship with him...

No, I want to continue to get closer to him...
