Afterword

Let’s get between the breasts rather than between the boards.

Hiroyuki Kagami.

Recently, I’ve only been novelising games for which I’ve written the scenario, but this is finally my first original erotic novel in five years. It seems that the term [juvenile pornography] is used in the world, but I don’t like this terminology.

When it comes to men, the heart of Akiba-kei culture is the ‘bishojo’ (beautiful girl). Or, to put it another way, Akiba-kei two-dimensional heroines. Bishojo” = “Akiba-kei two-dimensional heroines” are two-dimensional heroines who are romantic and sexual objects for adolescent males.

It was in the early nineties that ‘bishojo’ = ‘Akiba-kei two-dimensional heroines’ became a genre in the world of fiction. Juvenile is a term that was used before entertainment with ‘bishojo’ as the main product was established, in other words, before the birth of bishojo. There, the sexual phase of bishojo has been stripped away. It is an anachronistic term that is totally inappropriate for today’s world, where bishojo has become the axis of entertainment.

In my mind, erotic novels are more appropriate.

In English, it would be HENTAI NOVEL right?

Now, let’s talk about the story behind the creation of this work.

For several years, I had been thinking about whether I could write a pornography with the story of a righteous ally and an evil organisation. I thought that a confrontation between justice and evil would be very old-fashioned philosophically, but very appealing from a pornographic point of view.

Then, around June 2011, I received an email from Editor K asking me if I had any good porno manuscripts to which I replied that I had something like this. The original idea was lost, but then I spoke……over the phone and this is what I came up with. What if we did a novel version of 『Kyonyuu Fantasy』, but set in the present day, with a justice vs. evil structure?　That was the start. At first, the heroines were code-named Busty Red, Busty Pink and Busty Blue, but the name was changed because the editors thought it didn’t fit the current era. I would like to thank the editors, Mr M and Mr K.

Around the same time, the novelisation of Kyonyuu Fantasy 2, in both upper and lower volumes, will also be released. Please look forward to it too. See you at the second volume. Ji〜〜〜〜ku-boin!

1 May 2012, watching rice planting at the family home in Shiga.
