Chapter 7: Actually, My "Stepsister" Came Home Wearing a Uniform...

It has been a week since I found out that Akira is my sister.

During this period, I couldn't sleep, and I continued to live a life where I would only fall asleep shortly before sunrise.

And the reason for all this is Akira.

For example, when I'm lying alone in bed reading manga...

"Aniki, can I also read manga here?"

Akira would come to my room.

I have no reason to refuse, and besides, this is clearly my room, so it would be strange for me to leave.

So we stay in the room together, but when I lie on the bed...

"Pounce!"

"Ouch! My spine..."

Akira often does things like that, directly leaping onto me.

And then she sticks tightly to my back, which is really awkward.

"Ahahaha, You're so careless, Aniki! By the way, what are you reading~?"

"Akira, can you get down?"

"Ah~... the firmness and size are just right... perfect for me~..."

"Is that so? If you're done confirming, can you get down?"

"Huuuh... it's so warm, I feel so sleepy..."

"No sleeping, okay?"

"It feels like Dad's back..."

"Who do you think is Dad?"

"Snore... snore..."

"Don't fall asleep..."

If it were my little brother, this innocent feeling would be acceptable, but if it's a sister... if it's a girl, that's a different story. Soft all over, with a pleasant smell, it leaves me at a loss.

In short, the room becomes a space for just the two of us, and now I know it's very dangerous.

So I started spending time in the living room...

"Aniki, let's play 'End of the Warrior 2'!"

"Alright..."

What the heck, playing video games? Just when I started to relax because of that...

"I won again! My winning streak continues!"

"Oh...!"

--She suddenly hugs me.

I completely don't understand why she would hug the person who lost.

"--I won't marry someone weaker than myself. If you want me, come defeat me."

"Mmmph..."

Now even Nakazawa's classic line makes me feel uncomfortable.

"Darn it... let's play again, one more time!"

"Anyway, I'll win the next round~"

This feeling has been going on for a week. Akira always finds a chance to cling to me. And she even has physical contact with me. It's not about being close or not anymore.

Although I act the same as usual, I'm always feeling restless inside.

As for the aunt Miyuki who witnessed this scene in the living room--

"Oh my, oh my, your relationship is so good~"

She simplifies things too much.

Come on, it's not just a matter of having a good relationship.

It used to be possible indeed. Because I thought Akira was my little brother, I didn't think too much about it.

But after knowing that she's my sister, I can no longer see her as just a sibling. I'm really troubled about how to handle this sensitive issue.

Aunt Miyuki has that kind of attitude. Even if I go talk to Dad about it, I don't think he'll give me a serious answer.

So I called my senior who has a sister for help--

"Hey, Mitsuru. My little brother is actually my sister..."

"That makes no sense. If your eyes are not working well, go see a doctor. If your head is not working well, go to school. I'm busy, I won't talk to you anymore."

He hung up on me in an instant.

Lately, Mitsuru has been busy with his part-time job, and I haven't been able to reach him for days. When I finally managed to reach him, this was his attitude. I don't even understand myself, how did we become so close?

I could send a message to Lime, but he would definitely just tell me to talk to Yoko as usual, and then he wouldn't care anymore.

I can't possibly discuss such a sensitive matter with Yoko.

If I were to say to Yoko, "Actually, our stepmother's child is not my little brother but my sister," how would she react...? She'd probably be dumbfounded.

In short.

As they say, if you care, you lose. I can only try my best not to care.

By the way, will this "training," as Akira said, continue until I get used to it?

Once I start worrying about this, I can't even sleep at night.

* * *

August 20th.

I woke up around noon this day and came to the living room, but Akira and Aunt Miyuki were not there.

There was a note on the table saying, "We went out for a bit, just the two of us."

Dad was also not at home in the morning due to work.

In other words, I'm the only one at home now. It's been a while since I had some alone time, and it feels so relaxing.

I'm eating snacks and watching recorded anime, enjoying some brief moments by myself. Around three o'clock, Akira and Aunt Miyuki came back.

"We're back~"

"Welcome back. Where did you guys go... huh?"

As soon as I said that, I knew.

I saw Akira standing behind Aunt Miyuki.

Akira was wearing a school uniform. It was the uniform of Jōyūki Academy.

Our school uniform was designed through collaboration between a costume production company responsible for a certain idol group's stage outfits and a long-established uniform manufacturer.

It's considered cute both inside and outside the academy, and some girls even apply for admission just because of the uniform.

How would Akira look in such clothes?

Of course, she would become even cuter.

"Hehehehe. Um, what do you think... Aniki?"

"What... what else can I say... It looks really good, um..."

As I said that, Akira's face turned bright red. Even I started feeling embarrassed.

"The second semester is about to start, so we went to pick up the uniforms."

"Oh, I see..."

"But, um, isn't the skirt too short?"

After Aunt Miyuki said that, Akira had an expression like "here we go again."

"I told you, this is normal!"

"But didn't your previous school have skirts below the knees?"

"That's because they had strict regulations. I've always wanted to wear a skirt this short."

After Aunt Miyuki said that, I also felt that the skirt was a bit short.

In reality, it's about the same length as Yoko's, but maybe because I'm not used to seeing Akira in a skirt, it looks even shorter.

"But with such a short skirt, won't it easily expose too much if the wind blows?"

"It won't just easily expose everything... Aniki, don't you like this length?"

"What! Me?"

I'm telling you, please don't throw this topic at me.

Besides, can we now determine the length of the skirt based on my preferences?

And when did I ever say that I like short skirts? Assuming things from the start isn't really good, although I don't dislike them.

"Actually, most students at our school have skirts this short. Even if they're slightly longer, they won't go beyond the knees."

I tried to support her, but Aunt Miyuki still seemed reluctant to accept it.

* * *

After a while, Akira said she was going to change clothes and went upstairs, so it was just me and Aunt Miyuki in the living room.

"By the way, I didn't expect Akira to come back wearing our school uniform."

"After trying it on, she seemed to really like it and said it's cute, so she decided to wear it back..."

Maybe because I'm already used to the usual Akira, hearing her show interest in cute things surprised me.

"But our school uniform is indeed considered cute by many people. Akira seems to have fallen for it too."

"But... you know..."

"What's wrong?"

"On the way back home just now, the gazes of men around made me uncomfortable..."

It can't be helped because Akira is very cute. Once she wears the uniform, she'll definitely attract more attention.

But Aunt Miyuki, you're also partly responsible for that—I wanted to say that from the bottom of my heart.

Today, Aunt Miyuki was dressed entirely for going out, wearing a body-hugging dress that emphasized her curves. She's naturally beautiful, and her figure is beyond words. Wearing such clothes, her impact is extraordinary.

I wonder how many men's hearts she stirred up along the way with her seductive appearance.

And she doesn't even seem aware of it, which is really tactless.

What will happen when this mother with maximum destructive power and the super beautiful girl in a school uniform walk down the street?

I imagine they'll naturally attract the attention of men passing by.

"Will that child be approached by weirdos? It happened several times during junior high."

"I'll be accompanying her to and from school during this time, so don't worry."

"With you around, I feel much more relieved. I've also heard that there have been suspicious individuals lurking around this neighborhood lately..."

"If there are any suspicious people, I'll scare them off. But considering Akira's personality, even if she doesn't walk with me, she should be fine, right?"

"Well, not necessarily..."

"Huh?"

"Don't be fooled by her appearance; she's actually quite shy. When she's at school, she becomes well-behaved. She's very cautious, and her attitude becomes poor when strangers talk to her..."

"Is that so?"

The first day I met Akira, she did indeed show a disgusted attitude when I called her.

But what surprised me was Akira's behavior at school.

I thought that even at school, she would be the same as usual, living carefree. But it seems that's not the case.

"The homeroom teacher in junior high was also worried about her. They said she's not sociable, always concerned about her surroundings, and doesn't initiate conversations. So I was really worried when she transferred in the second semester..."

"That's true..."

Just as Aunt Miyuki and I were troubled, Akira came downstairs after changing her clothes.

"What are you talking about?"

"We were talking about how the second semester is starting soon."

I managed to get by with a response that wasn't a lie.

Sensitive topics like this shouldn't be told directly to her.

"Ohhh, Mom, can you teach me how to wear a bra?"

"Splooosh!"

"You're such a child... Aren't you embarrassed to say something like that in front of Ryota?"

"It doesn't matter to Aniki."

But it matters to me. By the way, Akira. You haven't been wearing them all this time, have you—

"Ah, sorry, it's not what Aniki was expecting. I usually wear sports bras. They don't have underwire, and they're very comfortable~"

I see, sports bras. I feel like a weight has been lifted off my shoulders.

"No, you don't need to report this kind of thing to me, and I wasn't expecting anything!"

"Ahahaha, don't be shy~ Okay, Mom, I'll wait for you upstairs~"

Then there was the sound of footsteps going up the stairs, and I let out a sigh of relief.

"Ryota, I'm sorry, that child is really..."

"Ah... It's okay... If she doesn't mind, then it's fine..."

"That child only started thinking about wearing a bra when she entered high school, so after we picked up the uniforms, we also went to a lingerie store."

"A... lingerie store...?"

"Because she said the ones I bought for her before don't fit anymore. In the end, I only saw her wear her first bra once... I even bought a really cute style for her..."

"Oh... oh..."

I'm telling you, there's no need to tell this kind of stuff to me.

And I never expected that one day I would hear the term "first bra and lingerie store" from the mouth of an older woman. It's giving me different kind of feeling.

"On another note, I never thought she would want to wear a bra. What kind of change has occurred in her mindset?"

"Well... um... I'm not sure either..."

Well, it's not because of me... right?

◇◇◇

Evening comes...

I had to start tackling the pile of summer homework I had accumulated, so I stayed in my room diligently writing with my pen.

Summer vacation goes until the 24th of August. Today is the 20th, which means there are only a few days left of summer vacation.

When I think about it, this summer vacation has gone by in an instant.

Mother and daughter moving into this house, spending lazy days with Akira, finding out Akira is my little sister... Well, I shouldn't think about these things while I'm trying to study. They would occupy most of my thoughts.

At that moment, Akira entered the room with the sound of knocking.

"Brother, are you studying?"

"Yes. What's up?"

"I brought some snacks and drinks for you."

"Oh, I see. Thank you. Just put them over there."

Akira placed the tray on the coffee table and took out a manga from the bookshelf, happily munching on the snacks.

"Um... Akira?"

"Crunch, crunch... What is it?"

"Why did you start eating and drinking?"

"Because I brought them."

"But they're not for you to eat..."

I was completely dumbfounded. I thought she brought them to reward my hard work, but it turns out she wanted to eat them herself.

"I was just teasing you. These are for you."

"Then don't start eating them yourself!"

"Ahahaha, I couldn't resist!"

"What do you mean you couldn't resist, you..."

I temporarily stopped writing and reached out for the snacks.

It had been some time since evening, and I was feeling a little hungry.

"By the way, Akira, have you finished all your summer homework?"

"I finished it a long time ago."

"Tsk... You're such a diligent person."

"It's your fault for not doing a little bit every day."

We exchanged smiles.

As we got closer, no matter what, I couldn't help but care about her, so the distance between us right now was just right.

"Oh, by the way, I'm wearing a bra now. How about that?"

Akira said, placing her right hand on her head and her left hand on her waist, striking a pose.

"Even if you ask me how I feel about it, Akira..."

"Yeah, you can't see anything like this..."

As she said that, Akira suddenly pulled down the neckline of her T-shirt, intending to show me what was underneath.

"Hey... hold on!"

"Ahahaha, just kidding."

She might have been serious about it, but it wasn't kind to my heart.

And I really wanted her to stop ambushing me like this... I caught a glimpse... just a little.

"Aren't you embarrassed to do something like this?"

"Well~... I am a little, but just a bit. Because it's for you, big brother, right?"

Does this mean she has already adapted?

It seems like she has completely transformed into a little sister, to the point where it doesn't matter if she shows off her body or discusses topics specific to women with me. But I haven't fully adapted yet...

"My friend told me that some people walk around the house wearing their underwear. They say it's because they're family, so they don't mind."

"That's in other people's houses. In our house, it's not allowed."

"Huh..."

"Don't argue. It's a firm rule."

I have no idea what kind of emotions I'm holding.

If Akira ever starts walking around in her underwear, I'll seriously scold her.

"The break is over. I'm going to continue doing my homework."

"Okay. Then I'll continue reading my manga."

"You should go back to your own room."

"Nope. If no one is monitoring you, you might slack off."

"Who do you think I am? I won't slack off."

"Alright, alright, don't mind me~"

After saying that, Akira unexpectedly sprawled on my bed and started reading her manga.

I thought she would eventually go back to her own room, so I ignored her. But about an hour later, I heard a comfortable breathing sound behind me.

"Hey, Akira?"

"Snore... snore..."

This person really has no idea what kind of emotions I'm holding...

I gently covered Akira with a blanket and dimmed the light.

As I continued working on my homework, relying on the brightness of the desk lamp, I heard a small murmur from behind me:

"...Dad, thank you..."

I pretended not to hear what she said.

To Akira, her father's presence must be so important that he appears in her dreams.

By the time I noticed, it was already morning, and I had fallen asleep leaning on the desk.

When I woke up, Akira was nowhere to be seen, and the blanket I had covered her with was now covering my back.
