「….I’ve warned everyone in the class about you.」

I’m somewhat amused by the sudden change of subject but I think that’s her main purpose of coming here.

Without saying anything else, I shifted my gaze from Kurumi-san to Ogura. She looked at me as if she didn’t know what to say or how to respond and then, turned her head down while playing with her fingers and acting fidgety—

「T-thank you.」

She muttered that.

In contrast, Kurumi-san’s words are frosty.

「……」

That should be her true feelings.

No matter how much Ogura wants to make up with Kurumi-san, it will never be an easy task. The relationship between the two of them is that of a perpetrator and a victim. The contrasting relationship is irresistibly clear.

Should I interject or something like a 3rd party?

I thought for a moment—but when I saw the look on Kurumi-san’s face, I kept my mouth shut.

「—even so, I’m the only one who can sympathize with you.」

「Eh?」

「I know what Ogura-san is going through right now so….that’s why I helped you. That’s all. I really hated that though. Not having any allies, everyone’s against you, and no one’s reaching out to you…it was so painful so I don’t want anyone else to feel the same….」

She tried to speak only the facts in a straightforward and unemotional manner. However, her voice began to sound wet….

「—Kurumi-san.」

She had tears in her eyes.

I don’t know why. Is it because she’s getting too emotional unconsciously?

I love Kurumi-san and I know everything about her but I don’t know how she feels. And I also don’t understand how she feels….

To say that I understand her is simply blasphemy against her.

「That’s why I….I helped Ogura-san for myself. That’s why…don’t misunderstand that you were forgiven or anything similar….」

Kurumi-san sniffed and wiped her eyes with her cuff. That statement is very Kurumi-san-like.

「….yeah, I know, so……I also don’t think I’ll be forgiven………………bu-but, can you listen to what I’m going to say………please.」

Ogura’s voice was also trembling.

Her hands were shaking, her legs were trembling, and she was speaking with emotions.

She took a deep breath in to suppress the tremor of her voice—

「For bullying you all these times—I’m really sorry.」

She bowed to Kurumi-san.

Silence falls on the rooftop. From the floor below, I could hear the voices of the teachers conducting their lessons and from the ground, I could hear the hustle of PE classes.

It’s an ordinary day but not an ordinary day too.

The girl kept her head down in front of us. Her shoulder’s shaking and the degree of her tension is more apparent than what meets the eye.

After staring at her for a few seconds, Kurumi-san took a step or two closer to her.
