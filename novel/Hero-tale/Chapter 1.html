Chapter 1 - The Country that Bonded with a Dragon.

After leaving the royal castle of the Kingdom of Intail, I was walking directly through the castle town.

I had already decided where I was going. Out of this country. If I told the princess about this, she was likely to vehemently object, so I kept quiet when we were talking in the royal castle.

“From here, ……is the kingdom of Enubidia?”

(エーヌビディア)

If I take a boat from the port town, I will soon reach the Kingdom of Enubidia.

As soon as possible, I want to escape to a place where His Majesty’s power does not extend to me.

At any rate, the members of the 『Stardust Light』are all idiots with one or two quirks. If only he could control them, he would bring back the best results, but unfortunately Julius will not be able to hold the reins of their power.

If he cannot control them – there will be horrendous damage.

I don’t want to be held responsible for it afterwards.

So I want to get the hell out of here and escape to the outside world.

‘Well, I guess I’ll……enjoy my freedom’

I don’t mind travelling alone.

I’m always with someone, so I feel like I’m spending precious time now.

“Welcome! We’ve got the latest in grimoires for sale!”

A shopkeeper’s voice can be heard coming from the stall.

Speaking of which, it is time for a new set of magical tools. I already have enough for what I need, but magic tools are delicate items and can get damaged during adventures and other rough activities. It is better to replace them with new ones around here.

“Do you have a comms stone?”

“Yes, of course.It’s pricey, though……. is 20,000 zeni a piece.

Expensive, but in line with the market.

“Then give me six.”

“Six?…… Aaa, well, customer, you’ve got it all wrong.”

The shopkeeper chuckled and said.

“One communication stone can hold the contact details of a hundred people.”

“Hundreds?”

“Aa. So one is more than enough…”

“Then give me eight.”

“Ha ……?”

The selled widened his eyes in surprise. Ignoring his shock, I paid up.

The number of contacts I want to register is seven hundred and ten. I had thought that six would be enough, since a single communication stone in another country can register one hundred and twenty contacts.

“Well,thank you for your continued patronage…”

I put the communication stones I’d have purchased in the pouch around my waist.

Leaving the shopkeeper, who remained surprised, I headed for the port town.

“Next, I’d better finish the formalities of getting on board the ship.”

The time is already midday, chances are castle and port towns are close

I probably won’t be able to get on the boat today, so I’ll have to stay overnight. So I decided to hire a carriage, thinking it would be better to get to the port early enough to book accommodation.

◆

When I arrived in the port town, I immediately headed for the boat station.

“I want to board a ship to the Kingdom of Aenebidia, are there any seats still available?”

I went to the counter and spoke to the man at reception who was reading the newspaper.

“Yeah, there are seats available, but …… we’re actually having a bit of a problem. There are restrictions on boarding for a while.”

“Restrictions?”

“There’s been an outbreak of a large monster called the Kraken in the waters on the way to the Kingdom of Enubidia. Because of it, the only people allowed on board now are adventurers of A-level or above, or those on official missions who have to cross the sea right now.”

Kraken are dangerous monsters.

I can agree with the restrictions imposed, but…..

“Then no problem. I’m a grade A adventurer.”

Saying this, I take out my adventurer’s card from my pocket and show it to the man.

An adventurer’s card is a kind of identity card issued by the adventurer’s guild to which you belong. In addition to the name of the registered person, the card also lists the adventurer’s grade.

“Oh~…… you’re not what you seem, are you?”

“I get told that a lot.”

Although His Majesty made fun of me, being a Class A adventurer is an inherently admirable enough status.

It is also a privilege of Class A to be able to overcome these restrictions.

“All right, I’ve saved you a seat. The boat will leave in three days’ time, so don’t be late.”

“Aa…… ah, wait a minute.”

Just before I turned on my heel, I approached the man again.

“Can I see your charts? I want to know which waters the Kraken will be in.”

“I don’t think there’s any point in knowing….Well, okay. Give me a minute.”

After a short wait, the man unfolds a chart on the counter.

“I think the Kraken has appeared here.”

A man pointed and told me where the Kraken was.

‘…in these waters, they can just barely reach the sound of the whistle.’

I’m developing a contingency plan for the eventuality.

At any rate, I was able to work out a way to manage even if the Kraken came out.

“Thank you for your help.”

“Oh. I don’t know what it is, but I’m glad it helped.”

After I left the window, I decided to look for accommodation to sleep in for the next three days.

As a port town, there were plenty of places to stay. I decided to stay at an inn that serves delicious seafood dishes.

◆

Three days later.

I boarded a ship bound for the Kingdom of Enubidia, as planned.

“But it will take a week until the ship arrives…..”

As I squinted my eyelids in the brilliant sunshine, the sea breeze caressed my cheeks.

I stroke my slightly sticky cheeks with my thumbs, thinking of my former companions.

According to the newspaper I bought this morning, the hero party is leaving the Royal Capital today.

There seems to be a grand parade celebrating their departure, but the noise and bustle of the parade does not reach the ocean. Not long ago I should have been spending time with them as a member of the 『Stardust Light』, but when I’m rocking leisurely on the boat, those days seem like someone else’s business.

Julius joined the hero party instead of me, as His Majesty wanted.

However, I still don’t think that man can hold the reins of the members, so….

“By the time I reach the Kingdom of Enubidia, one of our cities will probably have been crushed.”

Farewell, Julius.

I offer one second of silent prayer for the sacrifice of an old friend.

“Oops, I guess I should call ahead.”

I remembered that I wanted to contact someone before I arrived in the Kingdom of Enubidia.

I immediately activated the communication stone I had purchased in the castle town and communicated with the person.

“『Ned?』”

A female voice can be heard on the communication stone.

“Ah, It’s been a long time isn’t it? Are you on the clock now?”

“『No problem. I’ll make as much time as I can for you?』

Loyalty is as high as ever. How that came about is unclear.

“Actually, I’m on a ship heading for the Kingdom of Enubidia.”

“『Oh, you’re coming? What do you want?　Since it’s you, I wouldn’t be surprised if there’s a natural disaster on the horizon.』”

What’s more surprising than a natural disaster?

“I landed in some trouble and I’ve decided to leave the country. I’ll tell you more about it when I see you.”

『All right. I’ll show you around the city when I get there.』”

“Thank you. I don’t know how many days I’ll be staying, but …… I need some way of earning an income for now. I think I’ll register with the Adventurers’ Guild when I get there, so maybe you can help me with a few requests.”

“That’s no big deal. Do you want to hunt wyverns?”

“Ah, that’s a good choice. Wyvern tails are as delicious as steaks.”

As I was talking about these things, I felt a gaze from somewhere.

When I turned around, there was a young female knight with long blue hair fluttering in the air. She was about seventeen or eighteen years old, about the same as me. The fact that she is wearing armour on a boat trip suggests that she is a serious person.

“Well then, see you later.”

I disconnect communications.

The blue-haired female knight slowly approaches me.

“Sorry, were you on comms?”

“No, I’m done…..Do you need anything?”

“Not so much an errand, but I just wanted to say a quick hello.”

Greetings?　She corrects her posture in response to me tilting my head.

“I am May Ascent. I am a knight of the Kingdom of Enubidia.”

“I’m Ned Wolkinter. I used to be an adventurer in the Kingdom of Intail.”

I told her in the past tense, as I no longer wish to operate as an adventurer in that country.

But May did not show any sign of concern and opened her mouth.

“If you’re on this ship, that means you’re an A-level adventurer, right? That’s a good thing.”

“A good thing?”

“You’ve heard that there might be a Kraken attact, haven’t you?　If push comes to shove, we should be comrade-in-arms, to cover each other back. That’s why I’m saying hello to you like this.”

“I see, that’s an auspicious mindset.”

A polite or disciplined person……

She is probably a person of good upbringing. Her appearance is very well-defined, and her skin and hair are so beautiful that it is hard to believe that she is a knight who makes her living fighting. It is evidence that she lives in a good environment where she can take care of her appearance.

“I’m sorry, but please don’t count me among your forces.”

‘Hmm?　Why is that?”

“My title as an A-level adventurer was forced upon me by the same party of friends who pulled me along. As far as my individual abilities go, I’m afraid I’m not much different from an E-Level.”

“…… What is that? Isn’t that a scam?”

“Aa……they say that a lot.”

Unfortunately, it is really often said.

The faces of His Majesty and Julius passed through my mind.

“Hmm, I heard there was a Class A adventurer on board, so I was expecting to see him, but…… he’s just a fraud.”

May informed me with a sigh.

“Oh well. If it comes to a fight, at best stay out of the way and stay quiet.”

“I’ll let you do that. After all, my motto is ‘letting others do the job’.”

“……A joke. I really despise you.”

May turned on her heel and went away.

It was meant to be a joking comment, but apparently it only made the air worse. …..Well, it wasn’t really a joke, was it?

The best thing would be not to encounter the Kraken. ……

I tried to harbour such fleeting hopes, but they were easily dashed.

Four days later.

We encountered the Kraken.

◆

After midday on the fourth day.

Suddenly, the surface of the water ahead rolled up vigorously.

“What’s going on?”

The knights of the Kingdom of Enubidia on board the ship were bewildered.

The seawater that had rolled up turned into a large amount of rain and poured down on the ship. Someone shouted loudly as they were hit by the torrential water and their vision was blurred.

“The Kraken is out!!!”

That was the signal for battle.

The knights who had been waiting in the ship prepared their equipment and rushed to the deck.

A giant squid appeared in front of the knights holding up their shields.

“That’s the Kraken……”

It is a well-known category of sea-dwelling monsters but I recall that even I have never encountered one yet.

Kraken are extremely dangerous monsters, but they are also cowardly. As soon as they realise that their opponents are superior to them, they quickly retreat. So even if I encounter a Kraken, I can get through it unscathed as long as I take countermeasures.

“You, why are you here?”

While watching the Kraken on deck, I was approached by May.

“I told you to stay out of the way and keep quiet. Now go back inside.”

“I’ll do that, but….. can you guys handle it on your own?”

“Pfft, don’t worry about it. Don’t underestimate the knights of the Kingdom of Enubidia.”

Having said this, May went to the Kraken.

When fighting off a kraken, the important thing is to take initiative first. If we do not look frightened at all and keep showing the will to fight back, the Kraken will eventually leave.

For this reason, the knights should have attacked the Kraken as soon as possible.

‘No, ……, you’re undermanned by all accounts.’

From the looks of it, the knights are skilled, but with that number of men, they have their hands full just dealing with the Kraken’s attack.

At the same time they seem also having trouble. Their formation is already on the verge of collapse.

“…… It can’t be helped. I’ll call them.”

Before heading back inside, I took a shell-shaped whistle from my pouch.

◇

The battle with the Kraken was far fiercer and more intense than expected.

“Damn!”

May, a knight of the Kingdom of Enubidia, was knocked down by the violently shaking deck and looked at the Kraken with her hands on the floor.

“At this rate, we’ll get hit……”

The Kraken still remains unscatched.

It has begun to think of those on board this ship as inferior.

Why is this happening?

May asks the knights in the vicinity about the reasons for this.

“What happened to the other knights! There must have been more knights waiting on deck!”

“Of the ten who were waiting, four were dropped into the sea by the Kraken’s surprise attack. Two barely survived, but are currently undergoing treatment.”

“And the other four?”

“…………..are down with seasickness.”

My foot almost slipped.

“W-what a useless bunch…..!”

I put my hand on my forehead.

It is an abomination that I do not want to think of as the same knight.

I remembered that a few days ago I had called an adventurer from the Kingdom of Intail a [fraud].

I owe him an apology. My colleagues are much more cowardly.

“I’ll take the lead, you guys follow!”

“Aa-wait, May-sama!　You, the Knight of the Kingsguard, if you’re hurt, the Princess…Rushia-sama will be sad!!”

“Don’t be stupid!　It’s a knight’s duty to fight physically at times like this!”

Besides, if I can’t fight off the Kraken at this point, it will be more than just me getting hurt. Along with the ship will be reduced to rubbish.

May approached the Kraken with determination.

The Kraken swung its thick tentacle down. May blocked it, using her sword as a shield.

“Ggh, ah……!!!”

Normally, knights with large shields would have gathered to defend against this blow. May’s skill in single-handedly defending against it was assured, but the burden was too much. Her arms and legs were numb and motionless, and the sword fell from her palms.

──It’s bad.

She is completely unprotected. If the Kraken continues its attack, it cannot be prevented.

If any more people leave the front line, the line of defeat will finally become thicker.

May gritted her teeth and tried to move, ignoring the numbness in his body.

Immediately afterwards – suddenly, a high-pitched whistle sounded.

“What was that sound ……?”

Is that some kind of signal?　However, there was no sign of anyone rushing on deck.

As May wondered – suddenly the Kraken screamed.

The kraken’s condition suddenly changes, and the knights on deck are also puzzled.

The paralysed May, now able to move, closes in on the Kraken.

Before he can draw his sword, May stops moving.

At the Kraken’s feet, a shadow of something is seen on the surface of the water.

“Is that a…..mermaid?”

It’s upper half of the body is human but the lower part is half fish. This is the characteristic of the mermaid race.

The mermaids that are living at the bottom of the sea swam freely in the water and poked the kraken incessantly with spearheads in their hands.

“Are the mermaids joining us in attacking the kraken,……?’”

A scene unfolded before their eyes that could only be perceived as such.

But it is a sight that is not supposed to be.

“That’s ridiculous…..Mermaids hate humans. What…… is this?”

The Kraken screams once more.

Eventually, the Kraken moved away from the ship and was dragged to the seabed by the….. Mermaids.

After the Kraken left the ship, I put the whistle away in my pouch.

After a while, the surface of the water in front of me bubbled and soon countless mermaids appeared.

“『Land Dwellers!』”

“『O people of the land dwellers!!　We have defeated the Kraken!』”

“『Give us, our reward!　People of the land dwellers!!』”

“Yes, yes. Apples, right?”

“『Oh ……! This is the very essence of life!』”

“『It is no exaggeration to say that the people of the land exist to produce this!』”

“That’s an exaggeration.”

To say it is an exaggeration for humans to produce apples is a bit too much.

But….I’m glad I bought apples in the port town for this kind of situation.

Mermaids like several things, and apples are one of them. I’ll buy as many as I can if it puts them in a good mood.

“Sorry to call you on such short notice.”

“『Don’t it mind!　Ned has made a covenant with us!　That’s why she gave you the whistle!』”

A male mermaid said loudly.

Generally, the mermaids who come out to fight are men. His upper body was wrapped in strong muscular armour and he held a very heavy spear, using the buoyancy of the water.

“『By the way, Ned!』”

“Hmm?”

“『The Queen wants to see Ned!　Come to the Ryugu Palace!』”

“Roger. Tell her I’ll come when things settle down.”

“If you don’t come soon, she will create a huge tsunami and sink this ship!”

“That’s not good. I’ll give you another apple, try to appease her.”

“『Yes, sir!』”

If the Kraken is defeated and the ship is sunk, it’s the end of the world. Perhaps I’ve just eliminated the second crisis that was looming over the ship……?　Well, as for the second crisis, it looks like it came about because of me.

When the mermaids are completely out of sight, I hear footsteps nearby.

“Aa, y-you ……, that was …….”

There was a wide-eyed and astonished May.

“When I was communicating, you stood somewhere near me and this time, is ……does May has a peeping-tom hobby?”

“Nah?! N-no, no!　It just happened, I’m standing here at the wrong time!”

May denied it with a red face.

“More importantly, what did you do? That was a mermaid, wasn’t it?　Mermaids hate humans. They’re the kind of people you’d normally have to fight with just to see each other, let alone have a conversation.”

“Normally that might be true, but …… well, I’m kind of a friend”

“Friends….? A mermaid and a human……?”

“To be precise, I’m friends with the Ryugu tribe who live in the western waters. In exchange, I have a bit of a disagreement with the Lorei tribe, who live in the eastern waters. But I know someone who has an alliance with Lorei, so I think I can negotiate peacefully with mermaids from almost any ocean.”

“??????”

May looked confused at the information I gave her.

I may have even given out a bit of unnecessary information but I don’t have a problem with that.

“……It’s hard to believe, but in short, you can negotiate with mermaids…..So, in the battle with the Kraken earlier, you were the one who called the mermaids?”

“Aa. I told you, my motto is ‘letting others do the job.”

With the help of others, no one can beat me. I even take such pride in it.

When I jokingly said this in a boastful manner, May’s lips tightened.

“It’s complicated, but ……whatever it is, it does look like you’re our saviour…”

I was a little surprised. I thought I was going to be mocked again, but it seems that May is more tolerant of other people’s values than I thought. I hope His Majesty the King of the Kingdom of Intail will learn something from her.

“Can you make some time after we arrive in the country?　I want to thank you.”

For me, for whom networking is the only weapon in my arsenal, there is no reason to refuse the proposal.

I welcome meeting and interacting with new people.

“It’ll be my pleasure.”

Three days had passed since the defeat of the Kraken.　

The seven-day boat trip came to an end and the land of the Kingdom of Enubidia was in sight.

“Ned.”

As the ship approaches the harbour, May calls out to her.

“As promised, let me thank you. Are you sure you’re free for today?”

“Yeah, I’m free all day.”

“Good. Then I have some places I’d like to show you.”

My schedule is free, or rather, I left it open.

I actually had someone I was meeting up with, but after consulting with her, I changed my meeting with her to tomorrow.

“Aa, um, May-sama. Are you sure?　Inviting adventurers from other countries into your castle. ……”

“He’s our benefactor, it won’t be a problem. Besides, Rushia-sama likes adventurers.”

The knights are having some kind of conversation.

I chuckled when I heard the words knights and castle. Apparently, I’ve been associated with those things of late.

“How did you end up in the Kingdom of Intail?”

“ I was escorting a nobleman from our country who was staying over there for a few days or so.”

“We’ve arrived safely”

May mutters.

“Welcome. To the country that has made a pact with a dragon.”

The Kingdom of Enuvidia, a country which made a pact with a dragon.

The country is said to have borrowed power from a great dragon when it was founded. Before, the land in this area was said to have dried up. As a result, crops did not grow, but with the dragon’s blessing, the land was enriched and blessed with resources.

The adventurer party 『The Seven Meteors』- is the most famous adventurers in the world.

The feats accomplished by that adventuring party are too numerous to mention: they have conquered impregnable dungeons, defeated large monsters that were threatening the world, and pioneered the development of lands that had never been explored before.

The strongest adventuring party, both in name and in reality, is the 『The Seven Meteors』.

Likewise, the fact that the….『The Seven Meteors』 was disbanded a year ago is also common knowledge known by people all over the world.

“Did the 『The Seven Meteors』 stop by this town?’

“Yes!　I’d like to be able to say with pride….but strictly speaking, they were just passing nearby. But when you’re dealing with the 『The Seven Meteors』, that alone makes me feel proud.”

“ooh …. I see. So that’s what this is about.”

Adventurer party 『White Dragon Chivalric Order』.

“The 『White Dragon Chivalric Order』 are a party of adventurers, but they are the object of admiration for the country’s knights. In particular, the leader of the Order, Lady Riese Von Aldiana, is an S-class adventurer and a former aristocrat, and has a reputation for being noble and beauty……. May also seems to know a little about the hero party of the Kingdom of Intail.

“Although countries around the world now dispatch their hero parties, I had thought that the 『The Seven Meteors』 would go on to defeat the Demon King.

May mutters.

I am sure many of us thought so. That’s how strong the 『The Seven Meteors』 was.

!No!　They must be holding important positions in the country, or carrying out top-secret missions …..doing things we can’t imagine!”
