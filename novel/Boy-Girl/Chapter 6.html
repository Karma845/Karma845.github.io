“Hey, isn’t that underwear a bit too revealing? It’s black with side strings, it’s clearly giving off a sl*tty vibe–“

“Wait. You said you won’t get mad—”

“No, I’m not angry! I just want to yell… But anyway, please listen, Kagisaka-kun! There’s a reason why I’m wearing something like this!”

“…what kind of reason?”

“Well… It’s all because of Chifuyu! She’s the one who makes the wind blow…!”

“Well, yeah, I mean, the wind earlier was quite strong, but…”

“Wait, that’s not what I wanted to say—”

“But blaming Kazami for it is unreasonable, don’t you think?”

“Hey, wait! That’s not what I meant to say!”

I didn’t want to hear these words!

While complaining in my mind, I decided to change my initial plans and walk with Kagisaka-kun along the commuting route to somehow explain myself.
