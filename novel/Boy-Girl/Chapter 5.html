Not only that, but because she was running up the stairs at such a high rate of sped, I accidentally caught a glimpse of a boxer-type panties with no-hint of sex appeal on it.

A strange feeling of guilt tormented me afterwards. 

[Thanks for the wait!]

[...Hm]

After waiting for a while, I was invited to her room by a breathless Haruki. At first glance, the room was tidy. Even though I’m pretty sure that she just slammed everything to the closet

　

The room is dominated by black and brown colored furniture. In the shelves, there were lots of manga, plastic models, and various game consoles. A typical room for a grown-up Haruki

If it weren’t for the mirror and cosmetic products that were placed on the desk, it wouldn’t be much different from my room

[I’ll just go see what I can find]

[Oh, ok. Hey, Haruki]

[Hm? Also, why don’t you take it off Hayato? Isn’t it hot wearing socks?]

Haruki threw a cushion to me and started to take off her socks. Unlike in the past, her bare feet, which were white and plump, reminded me that she’s a girl. I was flustered even though I knew that it’s Haruki

　

And then, she sat down on the cushion vigorously, then sat down on her knees and leaned forward. I couldn’t help but think of the Haruki I knew back then. Her sex appeal had faded away for some reason, that made me laughed for a bit.

[So, who was the one who said anything about a monkey monster?]

It seems that Haruki is still angry about my words from this morning

Not only that she’s serious, I could definitely see that she’s sulking right now.

　

But when those pouting lips and piercing eyes sneered up on me, I felt a strange sweat running down my spine

[Well, I’m sorry. Just consider it as a “lend”. I owe you]

[Hmmm, “lend”... Okay, fine]

Satisfied with my reply, she pulled back her sulky face. Then she muttered the word “loan” as if to chew on it, and started grinning

　

“Lending” has a special meaning for both of us

　

The loan was to be given on one side, never “borrowed”, it is never “owed” and it is never offset, it just keeps piling up

　

[“Lending”, huh… I miss it. How many do you think you owe me now?]

[That’s my line, you owe me lots of things]

[... Hehe]

[I mean, Haruki, that’s… illegal, right?]

[My face?]
