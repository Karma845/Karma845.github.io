[Hm, what is it?]

[Nothing in particular, it’s not like I don’t want to look up at you]

[Speaking of which, I wonder if her results are excellent]

And I’m sure Haruki’s aware of that.

But the way she replied with a small voice and in a friendly manner made me understand why she’s so popular.

Since I’m not a big fan of crowds, I decided to take refuge near the window and observe my childhood friend from a distance.

(I wonder if this is her ‘facade’)

At first, I was fooled by her facade.

I’m not going to do anything about it though, since I’ve found out the truth.

For me, Haruki is Haruki.
