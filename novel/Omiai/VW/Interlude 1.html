Interlude 1 – Circumstances of Adults

A few days after the summer festival, in a certain part of Tokyo.

Two people were sitting at the counter of a certain bar.

One is a mild-mannered man with blue eyes and black hair.

Kazuya Takasegawa.

He is Yuzuru’s father and the current head of the Takasegawa family.

The other was a dour-looking man with amber eyes and black hair.

Toranosuke Tachibana.

He is Ayaka’s uncle and the current head of the Tachibana family (he calls himself the “acting head”).

The two of them had empty glasses in their hands.

It seems that a lot of alcohol has already been consumed.

“Oh, by the way, I heard a rumor. Kazuya.”

Toranosuke called Kazuya by name in a friendly manner.

Just as Yuzuru and Ayaka are close, the two of them are close as well.

To the extent that they call each other by name in private.

“Hmm, a rumor. Is that a …… good rumor?　You mean it’s the result of my daily work?”

“Sure, it could be a result of your daily work. But …… what I’ve heard are bad rumors.”

The bartender silently placed the cocktail on the counter.

Toranosuke and Kazuya picked up their glasses and brought the alcohol to their mouths.

“I’ve heard that Takasegawa used the money to buy women.”

“…… Hmm.”

Toranosuke’s words were neither confirmed nor denied by Kazuya.

But his eyes narrowed slightly.

Toranosuke’s tone of voice was contemptuous, but his eyes were laughing as he condemned Kazuya.

“If you want my help, you’ll have to give me your daughter? You’re still as dirty as ever. You Takasegawa people.”

“You’re a bad listener, you know that? …… You’ve got the order backward. I gave a loan to support my son’s loved one’s house. I hope you don’t misunderstand that.”

Then Kazuya lightly swirled the glass of cocktail in his hand.

“And whether this marriage works or not, I’m going to continue my business with Naoki Amagi ……”

“Hoh……”

“He has something my family doesn’t have……. I don’t want to be one of those outdated families.”

Takasegawa,

Rather, it can be said of the entire Japanese business community. ……

When it comes to the IT industry, Japan lags in comparison to other countries.

Naoki Amagi can bring it to Takasegawa.

At least Kazuya judged so.

This is why he invested a huge amount of capital.

“Maybe it’s just because I’m a parent, but I think Yuzuru is more than up to par as a man. That’s why he has no trouble finding someone to marry. There’s no reason to pay for it. You understand that, right?”

It’s business and money that matters.

The support for Naoki Amagi was based on the judgment that there would be a great return for the Takasegawa family in the future.

It was definitely not for the “purchase” of Arisa.

The Takasegawas are not a family that is involved in human trafficking.

Kazuya made his point over a drink.

“That’s why, isn’t it?”

“…… What do you mean, Toranosuke.?”

“It’s because you don’t want to give the money growing tree to anyone else that you insisted on taking hostages, isn’t it?”

“You have a nasty way of saying that, don’t you?”

Kazuya picked up a piece of chocolate that was served as a snack.

“All I asked for was a token of trust. And it’s also a sign of trust from me. It was mutually agreed upon. Naoki Amagi also liked the idea of forming a blood relationship with us. It’s a win-win situation for both of us.”

Since the company is making a huge loan, it would be a huge loss if they escaped.　

And for Amagi, They didn’t want Takasegawa to suck up all their skills and knowledge and throw them away.

That’s why Amagi and Takasegawa offered their precious children to each other.

In other words, the main objective is not economic gain by providing support and loans to Amagi, but political gain – the announcement that Naoki Amagi will become a subsidiary of Takasegawa.

Kazuya explained this to Toranosuke. ……

“That’s sophistry. Think about the power relationship. You’re as smooth-talking as ever.”

“Haha, ……, I didn’t know you wouldn’t understand even if I tried to say so much. The money-grubbing Tachibana cannot understand this exchange between the two families, which is full of humanity.”

In fact, Naoki Amagi would probably be very grateful for Yuzuru and Arisa’s engagement.

This proves how much importance Takasegawa attaches to Amagi.

In all likelihood, …… the power relationship between the two families is obvious.

Even if both parties are satisfied, there are those who, like Toranosuke – and he is only making fun of him, not really saying it – see it as Takasegawa threatening them with money and power.

But let them say whatever they want.

Originally, the Takasegawa family had many enemies.

It is impossible to get along with all the people in this world.

Therefore, there is no choice but to make a decision.

“But there is something that concerns me.”

“Hmm, what is it?”

“Why did you choose the daughter who is not related to Amagi by blood?　Amagi has another daughter. And a son.”

In addition to Arisa Yukishiro, Naoki Amagi has a daughter and a son who are both blood-related.

Whether it’s hostages or ” tokens of trust” ……

The one who is blood-related works better than the one who is not blood-related.

“This is just between us. though”

“Mm.”

“At first, it was going to be Amagi’s …… blood-related daughter.”

As for Takasegawa, if you ask them which one they want, the one who is related by blood or the one who is not, the blood-related one is obviously the one he wants.

Even for Amagi, it would be too rude to …… Takasegawa to bring out the daughter who is not blood-related.

Therefore, it was only natural that the blood-related daughter would be chosen.

“It’s just that she is only in the sixth grade. And …… Yuzuru would never want to do that.”

“Well, that’s true.”

“Right?　That’s why I thought I’d give it more time……. I was going to tell Yuzuru that there was a marriage proposal, and I was going to take it in stages and have them meet at the right time.”

More specifically, four years from now.　

The original plan was to have them meet when Amagi’s daughter turned fifteen and Yuzuru turned nineteen.

“Hmm, so …… what made you rush?　Don’t tell me it’s the deal between Tachibana and the continent.”

“Well, I’m aware of that, too. But the main reason is something else.”

“…… What’s that?”

Kazuya didn’t answer the question right away. ……

Slowly, he savored his cocktail.

He then said with a face slightly flushed with alcohol.

” My son’s love, I guess”

“…… Haa?”

What is this guy talking about?

Toranosuke tilted his head.

Kazuya explained to Toranosuke.

“Originally, I knew that Yuzuru and Arisa ……, the non-blood-related daughter of Amagi, were in the same class.”

“Hmm. …… So?”

“When I hinted to Yuzuru about a marriage proposal, …… ‘I won’t get engaged unless I see a blonde-haired, blue-eyed beauty!’, is what he said.”

“……”

Arisa Yukishiro does not have blond hair and blue eyes.

However, her hair is close to blonde and her eyes are close to blue.

And best of all, she’s a beautiful girl.

Yuzuru, who was in the same class as her, said that he preferred blond hair and blue eyes.

This means ……

“No wonder he doesn’t want a marriage proposal. My son already had someone he liked.”

It’s no longer an exaggeration to say that he was referring to Arisa herself.

Yuzuru basically insisted that he wouldn’t marry anyone unless it was Arisa Yukishiro.

“I see, so that’s why it’s a win-win situation, huh?”

“Well, that’s how it is. As for me, I want to support my son’s love.”

The matchmaking between Takasegawa and Amagi is meant to strengthen the connection ……, in reverse, nothing more and nothing less.

So there is no reason why one should be concerned about direct blood relations.

However, Takasegawa would be troubled if Amagi didn’t formally adopt her before marrying her off and have her become Arisa Amagi.

But that can be done after the formal engagement.

Right now, it’s still in the tentative stage.

There’s no need to rush.

“By the way, what does Naoki Amagi think about this?　Wouldn’t it be more convenient for him to use …… his own daughter?”

It’s normal for him to want to marry off his own daughter if he’s going to strengthen the connection anyway.

The Takasegawa family is one of the most prestigious families in this country.

There are two daughters: one who is related by blood and one who is not. ……

Normally, they would tend to cherish the former and would want to provide a good match for her.

” He was pleased. He said the Takasegawa family would be safe for her.”

“Hmmm, …… is that another way of saying that you don’t want to marry off your own daughter because she is cute?”

“Who knows…..That is all.”

Kazuya shrugged his shoulders.

To Kazuya, Arisa’s position in the family was not that important.

The only thing that interests him is the next head of Amagi. ……

Even that won’t affect many people.

If Amagi’s successor is capable, then the relationship should be continued.

If he is incompetent, you can take advantage of the blood relationship that has been established and take it away from him.

The children of Yuzuru Takasegawa and Amagi Arisa will have the right to inherit Amagi’s estate.

Calm, cold-hearted, and cold-blooded.

That’s how the world views Kazuya Takasegawa, or rather the Takasegawa family.

“What about the people who are involved?　Amagi’s daughter, ……Arisa Yukishiro , was it? , 　If she doesn’t like your son, it’s a moot point.”

“Well, yes. As for me, I’d rather have a woman who loves my son as a family.”

Kazuya does not deny political marriage.

In fact, he actively supports them.

However, he did not want to force a marriage.

If internal conflicts occurred within the family, it would cause great damage.

He doesn’t want to create a spark of conflict.

If Arisa Yukishiro hated Yuzuru from the bottom of her heart, ……

He will have no choice but to give up.

But ……

“From my point of view, they seem to be in love with each other …….. I’d love for them to get married and show me, my lovely grandchildren.”

“Do you like her too?”

“Of course. She has a good personality and a good disposition. She’s not stupid. She can cook well. She is also able to help Yuzuru. She is the best person to be welcomed by Takasegawa as a bride.”

Kazuya said in a good mood.

Then, perhaps because he was drunk, his …… speech began to slip a little.

“I have a feeling she’s going to give birth to and raise a healthy child.”

“Don’t say that directly to her!　Unless you want your daughter-in-law to hate you.”

” I know. I know, but there is nothing better than a cute grandchild, right?　Grandson or granddaughter. …… We’ll soon be grandpas, too. ……”

“Grandchildren, huh? I don’t have any children, so I’m not interested in that.”

Toranosuke sniffed.

He was never married, so he had no children.

However,…… since there is Ayaka, the daughter of his deceased brother, there is no danger of Tachibana’s blood ceasing to flow.

“You have a lovely niece. …… Speaking of which, what’s going on?　Between Ayaka-san, Uenishi’s daughter, and Soichiro-kun ……”

“Ha~h, ……, don’t ask me.”

“By the looks of it, I’d say that mess over there is far from over.”

Toranosuke looked exhausted, but Kazuya was in a good mood.

He must be feeling relieved that the relationship between Yuzuru and Arisa seems to be going well.

” Takasegawa had a little to do with this matter. If your son had been docile and gotten engaged to Uenishi, the story would have been much simpler.”

“I told you earlier that I can’t force him. I’m expecting Yuzuru’s child to repair the relationship with Uenishi.”

“Hmm. That’s selfish.”

“You’re the selfish one, aren’t you?　If you had a son or daughter, you could have taken Ayaka-san as a daughter-in-law. Or you could have married her off to Satake, to our family, or to any other family. Well, it’s a little late for that.”

“Ayaka is the heir to Tachibana.”

“You’re stubborn…….”

“Say what you want.”

Until late that night.

The two of them continued to drink.
