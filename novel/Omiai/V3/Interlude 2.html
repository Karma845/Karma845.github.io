Interlude – Arisa-Chan


When did I fall in love with that person?

Suddenly, I thought back.

The first time I met that person was at the entrance ceremony.

My foster father had given me strict orders to never cause any trouble.

So I knew him from the beginning, and I remember my impression of him very well.

He was a very quiet and calm person.

That was my impression of him, and I think it was the impression of many of my classmates as well.

The girls in my class described him as a cool but quiet guy.

But I never thought of him as a quiet guy.

In hindsight, I thought he was… scary.

If I had to use an analogy, I would say a big giant tree. Or a dense forest of trees.

It is quiet and calm.

But…. he has very strong power.

I felt that way.

We were in the same class, but there was no conversation between us.

I didn’t want to get involved with men, and he didn’t seem particularly interested in me.

So I was a little surprised when my adoptive father told me that he wanted to arrange a marriage meeting with me.

He was clearly indifferent to me.

I wondered if he really liked me… I couldn’t refuse the matchmaking, so I agreed.

And as expected, my adoptive father had misunderstood that he was hoping for a marriage meeting with me.

On the contrary, he didn’t seem to want to engage in matchmaking.

I think it was natural.

It is unthinkable for a high school student to get engaged or married,…

So, maybe.

I thought that he might be willing to listen to my absurd request for a fake engagement.

And as it turns out, he did listen to my request.

To protect me.

He is a kind and caring person.

That added to my impression of him.

If someone asks me if I liked him from that time …, I don’t know.

At least, I didn’t have strong feelings for him at that time.

　

The reason I can’t say for sure is that when I think back on it now, I felt so happy and reliant on his care,… and it makes my heart ache so much.

Then, as it happened, I started going to his house once a week.

I got to know a lot about him.

I also found myself telling him about myself and my family situation.

When most people find out about my situation, they take two main actions: ……

They either tried to “greatly take care of me”, or they run away.

No, this expression may be too convenient for me.

My stepbrother has been a big help from time to time.

He couldn’t do much, but he went the extra mile and made things worse for me.

So I started to say I don’t need help, even though I wanted help.

Because they try to help me when they can’t help me.

If they did that, my position would be at an even greater disadvantage.

Because I refused the help, people began to turn a blind eye or run away.

I want help, but I don’t want to be helped.

I wanted help, in a convenient spoonful amount, in a convenient way.

I think this was really selfish, self-serving, and arrogant thinking.

There is no way that there is a convenient prince on a white horse, who would understand my weak, wimpy intentions and carry them out.

There’s no way there would be one…

But he did exactly that. 

He said he would help me.

He tried to help me as much as he could.

But he didn’t do anything forceful that would put me at a disadvantage.

Maybe I’m overstating the case.

It could be a coincidence.

 　

But even so,.. he looked at me carefully, understood my intentions without saying a word, respected my wishes, and did what I wanted him to do.

I felt that this person would protect me.

　

I came to have such a sense of security.

That’s why I went with him on a date to the pool.

And there, Ayaka-san and Chiharu-san, who happened to meet me, asked me,

Are you in love with him?

I don’t know when I first fell in love with him.

But when did I first become aware of it…? It must be at that time.

First of all, I was very relieved to know that Ayaka-san and Chiharu-san already had a boyfriend (? . if he is their boyfriend, that means Satake-san is two-timing, and those two are allowing it. I am not so sure, but that’s not important here.)

It was a great relief to be able to confirm that there was no such relationship or feeling between him and Ayaka-san or Chiharu-san.

In addition, when Ayaka and Chiharu asked me if I had any feelings for Yuzuru-san …, I became aware.

I am in love with him.

And on the day of the summer festival, it became definitive.

He forgave me for my “lie”.

I can trust him.　

I felt safe.

I could surrender myself to him.

That’s what I thought.

I was thrilled when he hugged me.

I felt relieved when he stroked my head.

On the other hand, I was tempted to stroke his head and play tricks on him.

It was clear to me that this was love.

I don’t know if he also liked me in the same way…

But he protected me from my mother and was happy with my poor birthday present.

So… I felt a bit of guilt.

Because I haven’t been able to give anything back to him.

He’s always helping me…, without me asking for help myself.

It’s an act that puts all the blame on him.

 It made me feel very ugly.

So… I went and poured it out on him.

That I am an ugly person.

And that he doesn’t know it.

I was really reckless, egotistical, and selfish.

But he accepted me for who I was.

He knew that I was an ugly person, and he affirmed it.

I still felt sorry for myself.　

However, my heart felt lighter.

At the same time, I felt that I had to give something back to him.

I thought that I had to do something for him, not just be done for.

However, the only thing I could do for him was to make him bento…

But he was happy to receive it.

He always said it was delicious.

I can continue to make lunch for him.

I want to make dinner for him every day.

I even started to think about it.

Then, on Christmas Day, he asked me to continue cooking for him.

I thought it was like a marriage proposal.

Of course, he didn’t mean it as a marriage proposal.

But even if it had been a proposal, I would have nodded my head in agreement.

I was happy and accepted.

At that time, I thought to myself,

I could marry this man.

With him, I could imagine getting married, becoming a couple, having children, growing old together, and being surrounded by grandchildren.

Marriage with him, which I had tried not to think about until now, was becoming a reality.

As I looked at the necklace he gave me, I began to envision my married life with him.

I had a sneaking suspicion that he liked me.

I could tell by looking at the necklace.

The necklace he gave me was brand new and very nice.

At first glance, I understood.

That he knows my taste… That he listened carefully and remembered what I told him when we had a date to the cinema before.

And that necklace is a very expensive item.

He was working part-time to make ends meet, so it wouldn’t be a cheap purchase for him.

He would never give something like that to a woman he didn’t like.

So he must like me…

My relationship with him is a fake “fiancé”.

However, this is based on the premise that there is no affection between me and him, and that we will break off our engagement once I am able to live on my own.

That assumption has been broken.

I like him.

He likes me.

Then, we should continue our relationship.

If we do that, we’ll eventually become normal fiancées and get married …

It was when I was thinking such a thing.

Suddenly, my foster father told me.　

“If you don’t like it, then it is fine not to get married.“
