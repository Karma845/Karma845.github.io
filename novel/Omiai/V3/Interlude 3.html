Interlude – Wants to Express

When he told me this, at first I thought I had done something wrong.

Because I had just gone to his parents’ house for New Year’s greetings.

Or maybe he had grown tired of me.

Feeling uneasy, I asked my foster father.

After thinking about it for a while, my foster father answered.

Originally, Mei-chan was supposed to be the one to get engaged to him, not me, according to my foster father.

However, his family had nominated me.

And since I was hoping for an arranged marriage, we decided to tie the knot.

But…

“I was wondering if you really don’t want to have a marriage proposal.”

That’s apparently what he meant.

I didn’t know how to answer, so I just stood there in a daze.

When I remained silent, my foster father said, “You don’t have to get married if you don’t want to. I have no intention of forcing you to do anything. If you don’t like it, just say no… I’ll wait a while to answer”.

It’s true that I didn’t like it until a while ago.

But I don’t hate it now.

It was a little too late to say anything, and the words were meaningless now.

Why did he start saying this now?

Suddenly, it hit me… there’s my adoptive mother.

My adoptive mother hates me.

She seems to be bitter about this marriage proposal, and about me marrying him.

Could it be that she wants to use my dislike as an excuse to break up our engagement?

And then get him engaged to her own daughter, Mei-chan.

My adoptive father also wants to marry him to his own daughter, since she is naturally cuter than me…

It may be that I am a little too paranoid.

But there is no denying it.

Or was it … that person again, telling my foster father or foster mother something unnecessary?

That person is really, always, always, always ……

No, let’s not do that.

It’s not decided just yet.

Whatever the case, there was no doubt that a dark cloud was gathering over my engagement with him.

This suddenly made me feel uneasy.　

Is he really in love with me? 

Until a little while ago, I was absolutely confident that he was in love with me,  …although that may sound a little arrogant.

He’s been so kind to me.

He also gave me a very nice gift.

Most importantly…, one would never say, “Can I hug you?” to someone you don’t like.


It’s definitely a mutual love.

I thought that he loved me so much that maybe he thought I was his lover already and that’s why he didn’t bother to tell me his feelings.

But maybe it’s all in my imagination.

I think that because I like him, I only see what is convenient for me…

This possibility crossed my mind.

He’s just trying to be a “fiancée”.

He might be thinking of me as just a friend of the opposite sex.

When I thought about it, I realized that he was very close to the girls he grew up with.

Maybe this level of closeness is normal for him…

No, no… indeed, it’s impossible.

You can’t try to hug someone you don’t like or pat them on the head.

Even if it’s not a strong romantic feeling, I’m sure he likes me in some way…Probably.

But even if that is the case, I still feel uneasy.

He is a wonderful man.

He doesn’t stand out much at school, but that’s because he doesn’t usually get his hair done.

When he goes out on a date with me, he dresses up nicely.

When he is properly dressed, he is very cool.

He is also tall.

He’s kind and a gentleman.

He is very caring.

He is smart, well-educated, and athletic.

He can tell jokes and is very fun to talk to.

And … although it would probably offend him to mention this when listing his good points, the Takasegawa family is very rich.

Until half a year ago, I was so naive that I didn’t know how much money and political power his family had, but now I do.

Of course, I didn’t like him because he’s rich and it’s not that I love him for that.

Even if his family were to fall, I would never lose my love for him.

Just that.. Girls who are attracted to that money might come after him.

A very good classic way to say it is ‘thieving cat’.

Of course, he’s not the kind of guy who would ever cheat on his wife.

I believe in his character.

But he has never confessed his love to me or proposed to me.

In other words, without the “fiancée” in the relationship, he and I are just friends of the opposite sex.

What if he doesn’t like me that much, or at least doesn’t like me as much as I like him?

And what if a very attractive woman approached him?

I don’t want to even think about it.

I want certainty.

I want him to say that he likes me, that he loves me.

I want him to express it in words.

This brings me back to the question… Why doesn’t he tell me that he likes me?

He is in love with me.

He should be …… in love with me.

And I think I have shown him by my actions that I like him.

I think it’s about time he told me how he feels about me.

Despite this, he has yet to share his feelings with me.

…Of course, that’s something I can tell myself as well.

If he doesn’t tell me he likes me, then I should tell him I like him.

That’s the way it should be.

I know that it was not good to remain silent forever, and have him do what I wanted him to do without saying out loud and enjoy it.

It’s a very bad habit of mine to just look at what I want without saying anything.

Just that… Even if it is very selfish of me to do so… I want him to say “I love you” to me.

It’s a bit of an exaggeration to call it a dream, but I have a desire for a romantic confession from the man I like.

I think this is a common desire of many women, not just me.

And … although this is a bit of an excuse, I’m sure he himself would like to confess to me.

His household is very old-fashioned (maybe not the best way to put it).

I think it’s ingrained in him.

In fact, he always walks on the side of the road when he walks with me and gives me a hand when I get out of the car.

Of course, I don’t think he even has a male chauvinistic mindset…

He might be thinking that confessions and proposals are supposed to be done by men.

So I’d rather wait for him to do it.

Let’s go back a little bit on this topic.

For some reason, he has not told me that he likes me.

Even though I’m always prepared to receive it.

Then, after much pondering, I suddenly came across a possibility.

Could it be that he is…

Could it be that he is incredibly dense?

Does he not realize that I like him?

Isn’t it possible that he doesn’t know that I have feelings for him?

Thinking back, at the New Year’s holiday.

He casually took my hand.

I was very happy and embarrassed at the same time.

I think it showed in my expression.

The fact that I was flattered to have his hand in mine should have shown on my face that I liked him.

But he was like, “Hmm? What’s wrong?”

He’s very good at hiding his emotions, so I thought he was probably just hiding his embarrassment. But…,

Maybe he doesn’t really understand my fondness for him.

It was like that when he suddenly asked me to go home with him.

I was the only one who seemed to be embarrassed.

What if he is incredibly dense and doesn’t know how much I like him?

That would explain why he hasn’t told me how he feels about me.

He’s a very brave man, but, understandably, he would be reluctant to share his feelings with someone who may or may not like him.

But this means he won’t be able to tell me his feelings for me anytime soon.

If he’s incredibly dense, he’ll never notice me from the way I’ve been treating him.

What should I do…?

It might have been a bad idea to worry about such things so seriously.

I caught a cold on a beautiful Saturday.

And just at the time when my foster mother and Mei-chan were not around.

… No, it was probably for the best, though, because I felt more at ease without my foster mother.

When I told him about it, he was very worried about me.

He said he would visit me.

At first, I felt very bad.

I didn’t have a serious cold at that time, and I didn’t want him to catch it.

But… It was also true that I felt vulnerable.

I guess he could feel it over the phone.

He brought up the fact that I had nursed him in the past and guided me so that I could easily ask for his help.

I was very happy to hear his concern.

At the same time, I was apologetic.

I decided to ask him to help me.

As it turned out, it was the right decision.

My cold had gotten worse after his arrival.

I had such a high fever that I couldn’t help but show my undergarments in front of him, and my judgment and consciousness were impaired.

It was really reassuring to have him there.

Besides…, I thought it was a bit of a perk that he gave me a princess carry.

Then he took me to the hospital and even prepared a meal for me after we returned.

He offered me a tray with a bowl and a spoon on it.

Then suddenly,… It occurred to me.

If I continue to treat him the way I usually do, he will not notice my affection.

And no matter how much I would wait for his confession, it would not be good for me to remain passive.

I need to change as well.

I need to be more proactive in communicating my feelings to him in a way that is easy to understand.

That’s why…

“Feed it to me, please.”

I selfishly expressed my desire.
