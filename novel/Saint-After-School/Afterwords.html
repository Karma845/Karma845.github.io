Afterword

It’s been a while. If you’re new here, it’s nice to meet you. I’m Tozuka Riku.

Thank you very much for picking up the third volume of “I Know That After School, The Saint Is More Than Noble.”

It was thanks to all of you who support us that we were able to publish the third volume in this way.

This volume finally marked the start of the summer vacation, and unlike the previous volumes, I think I was able to show you Yamato and Seira enjoying their long vacation instead of school life.

With the introduction of a new character, Tsubaki, Yamato learns more about Seira’s past and contemplates their relationship.

We hope that you will continue to watch as the two slowly come to terms with each other.

Of course, there are also many exciting and thrilling events typical of summer vacation, so we hope you will enjoy them.

The illustrations in this issue are also a highlight, and the cute and fresh appearance of Seira and Tsubaki is a must-see. Also, I personally think Seira eating ramen on the cover is just too cute, so please check it out!

Lastly, I would like to express my gratitude.

Thank you again to the editor in charge and to everyone involved in the publication of this work. I look forward to working with you in the future.

Thanks to Takubon-sama for the illustrations. Thank you again for your precious and summery illustrations. I look forward to working with you in the future.

And to our readers, thank you very much for reading the third volume. I will try my best to make you enjoy it even more in the future.

Thank you very much for reading this far.

Hope to see you again in the next volume.

March 2022 Tozuka Riku
