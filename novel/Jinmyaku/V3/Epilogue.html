Epilogue

Three days after investigating the castle district of the Airun Ruins.

After a well-deserved rest at the inn, we were once again discussing our future plans.

“What happened to Ziggrit and his party……?”

Alice says with an anxious look on her face.

I had invited Alice and Phylicia to the room that Kirch and I were using. We sit around a circular table and discuss.

“It’s not going to end too well. It won’t save the……party from breaking up.”

Ziggrit and his party are now going to see His Majesty the Emperor in person.

They are going to confess all their sins to His Majesty. From the expressions on the faces of the Ziggrit when we parted three days ago, it is clear that they will not repeat their sins.

“Let’s talk about our future plans.”

After a quick look at everyone’s faces, I open my mouth.

“We will continue to look for clues about the Demon King. But apparently, Yggdrasil’s dark activities have become conspicuous recently.”

Alice and the others nodded.

The Abandoned Dragon’s Nest and the Airun Ruins. We’ve been involved with Yggdrasil members twice in a row.

We can’t overlook it.

“So, our next destination……can we take a little detour?”

I continued to say to Alice and the others who had curiosity in their eyes.

“What do you know about the Assembly Of Great Powers?”

“I’ve heard of it……. I think it was…… a meeting of only the powerful and strongest people that takes place once a year……?”

I nodded at Kirch’s answer.

“That perception is generally correct. The Assembly Of Great Powers is a special event, an invitation-only meeting, where you have to be one of the strongest in the world to be allowed to participate. It takes place in two months’ time and this year it will be held on the eastern continent.”

Alice and the others nodded silently.

Participation in a meeting of the powers is a kind of status. For those who are obsessed with strength and power, such meetings are the object of admiration.

“This meetings bring together powerful people from all over the world, so there are often important meetings going on. What I’m trying to say is….. This is an event that lends itself to the exchange of information.”

With these words, they agreed.

I think they read my intentions.

“Yggdrasil was syphoning off the power of the Dark Eaters….they might be planning something on a large scale. It would be better to exchange information with various people at once.”

This may lead to clues about the Demon King.

“I understand it’s by invitation only but is it gonna be okay over there?”

Phylicia asked me.

“No worries. I’ve received a lot of invitations actually but so I’m going to accept invitations from the right people.”

“….. I kind of expected that.”

Phylicia nodded quietly and said.

For some reason, Alice and Kirch also nodded with mixed expressions, as if in agreement.

“Are you going to the Assembly Of Great Powers?”

“Hmm?”

As soon as the door to the room opened, a girl’s voice was heard.

When I turned around, I saw a girl with white hair standing at the entrance of the room. It was the girl who was kidnapped and almost sacrificed by Ziggrit and his party.

“Aa……well, is your body alright now?”

“Yes, thanks to you.”

It is very typical of Alice that her concern comes before the fact that she came into the room without permission.

Three days ago, when we left the ruins, we left the girl in the care of a clinic in town. I was afraid that she might be injured but from the looks of it, she seems to be fine.

“So,Ned-sama. Are you going there?”

“Yes, I intend to, but ……”

This is the first time that we’ve talked together and she spoke to me as if we’re close.

Besides – when did I tell her my name?

“Then may I accompany you?”

“Ha…..?”

Suddenly I was told something I didn’t understand.

But then…someone I recognize crosses my mind.

“Wait a minute.You, aren’t you……?”

Right in front of me, the girl’s body is enveloped in mist.

Her white hair turns peach-coloured, and the shape of her face and the colour of her skin also change.

“Long time no see. Ned-sama.”

The girl solemnly bows her head and greets me.

I recognised that figure.

I want to know the outside world. With this single-minded determination, she has mastered the magic of alter ego and disguise.

She sends her alter egos to countries,kingdoms and empires around the world as her curiosity takes her.

“…… Your Imperial Highness.”

The Imperial Princess of Eisu smiled softly.
