Preface

To XXX

 

 

I think this is the first time I’ve written a letter to you.

I think it may surprise you but I’m startled by this determined side of mine.

To think the last time I wrote any characters on writing paper like I’m doing now was during elementary school.

This is maybe the first time I’ve written one ever since I wrote a letter to myself 10 years into the future way back.

I wonder how much time has passed since I met Akiha and Haruka?

I felt like close to a month, a year, maybe even ten years.

Somehow, I never fully believed that the daily life that began on that day would ever end.

I innocently and simple-mindedly believed that the days with you would continue forever.

That’s why there certainly must have been things I’ve overlooked I think.

For example, the stars shining in the sky on the road to school, the right hand adjusting the satchel, the lies I told for others’ sake.

The parabola which the ball drew through the air, the rising smoke from the incinerator, the unconsciously repeated speech habits.

The things I could only experience at that time.

The things that already have been lost.

That’s why,

at these final moments, I want to reminiscence together with you, one by one.

(End of preface)
