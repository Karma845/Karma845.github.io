“I don’t like going out after school making a clamour, I continued telling dirty jokes forcefully, in the first place, I’m not a person who’s too cheerful… More than that”

I put my hands into the bag that was on my shoulders.

“…I like this kind of thing! Literature and Arts!”

I put up the paperback book I was reading the day I first met Akiha, Still Life.

“In other words——“

Saying this, I took a big breath and said,

“I was making a character! To get along with everyone, to enter into the group, I was forcing myself to do all that. But honestly, I hate all of that—-that’s why, I’m going to stop making characters from now on! I’m sorry to everyone but I’m going to be myself!”

Then, looking at the faces of everyone in the room again.

“So—–I hope you can accept me as I am!”

Silence swoops down in place after I finish saying everything.

In the gloomy silence, no one was able to move.

A dusty breeze danced past us.

“I see…”

Tomigaya was the first to speak up, crawling out of the silence.

“Well, aren’t things like that all right?”

“Beats me…”

Sakurai followed Tomigaya, saying this in a light tone.

And then both of them said,

“Well, I’m going home first. I have a part-time job.”

“Ah, I also need to go. See you later!”

After saying that, they quickly left the main gate.

“Oh… see you later”

I could feel my mouth rapidly drying up as I replied to their backs.

———Well, aren’t things like that all right?

On the surface, it sounded like they were accepting me. But in reality, there were no emotions in the line.

——-I am sure I wouldn’t be able to continue my relationship until now with them anymore.

The two of them taught me how to react at a good pace and how to position myself in a conversation.

I remembered it because of our friendship.

But still – we couldn’t be like that anymore.

I felt my fingertips grow cold as I realized this fact clearly.

Each beat of my heart grew heavier with anxiety.

After all, no one would accept me.

As long as I don’t make a character, as long as I don’t pretend to be something, I will not be needed anymore.

However,

“Tell me~~~!”

An unexpected cheerful voice suddenly rings out from next to me.

When I turned my gaze to that, Sudo was looking at me as if she was deflated.

“Ehm, seriously!? So Yano-kun was always making a character? I didn’t notice it at all! You should have told me.”

Her eyebrows were raised as if she was in trouble. She closed her eyes sulkily and pouted.

At her behavior and action which was just like as usual,

“Ah, N-No…”

I couldn’t find the words to reply right away.

“Somehow… I’m sorry”

“It’s all right.”

Then she crossed her arms violently and said,

“But then, I’ve been making a lot of crazy requests to Yano! Now I sound like a horrible person… You should have said so earlier!”

“….I was indeed feeling something like that”

Saying that, Shuji laughs bitterly.

“I thought Maybe Yano was exaggerating a little. But even I hadn’t thought that far ahead…”

Indeed, Compared to other members, I feel like Shuji never asked me to act like a cheerful character.

I thought he might be that kind of guy, but really, did he actually have a hunch about it?

“But still so, you’re overreacting too much… Also that novel, it’s quite a literary novel, isn’t it? It’s too different from your usual character…”

“…I’m really sorry”

“No, it’s okay though——“

The reaction was the same as before.

Sudo and Shuji are the same as always.

After a short pause, I understood what it meant.

That’s right.

Honestly, I knew it all along.

I knew that these two people would accept me like this.

Even if I don’t pretend, they would be friends with me,

“…Thank you”

These words spilled out naturally.

“I’m really glad that I am friends with you”

“What are you saying all of a sudden? It’s kind of embarrassing!”

“Indeed, it’s really embarrassing when you said it again”

While being thankful for the two who were squirming in embarrassment, I turned to look at Haruka.

She has been watching my affair from there since a while ago, with the expression of Akiha.

Apparently she still can’t seem to take the step forward.

That’s why I was holding a notebook in my hand.

I opened the page of our exchange diary and held it up to Haruka.

That page was filled with scribbles in Akiha’s writing.

———You are indispensable to me.

As she laughed somewhat dazed at the page, I could see a slight amount of determination filled within her face.

Looking down and with a slow gait, she took a step forward.

“Hmm, what’s wrong Akiha?”

“Did something happen?”

Then she stand up in front of them who had a dubious express and raised her face,

“…What?”

“Minase…San?”

Her eyebrows knitted in anxiety.

Her timid eyes that look as if they were going to cry anytime.

Her hands were tightly holding the bag and her feet were trembling.

This was the first time Haruka had shown Sudo and Shuji her own face.

Sudo and Shuji were surprised to see an expression that looked nothing like Akiha.

And to them, Haruka opened her mouth hesitantly.

She said her name for the first time——–

“N-Nice to meet you. I’m Minase…..Haruka.”
