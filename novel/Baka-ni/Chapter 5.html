She scolds me but doesn’t let go of my hand.

“Sorry, you’re just too understanding and amazing.”

“Ah, maybe I shouldn’t have said that.”

“It’s too late for regrets now.”

“I know. A woman doesn’t go back on her word.”

She sighs as she reluctantly accepts, her conflicted look is also adorable.

“If you’re hesitating, why not relax the conditions a little more?”

“Like what?”

I propose the most peaceful solution.

“Within the bounds of the law, why don’t we secretly have a wholesome relationship?”

I gaze at her beautiful face.

The once unattainable presence, too dazzling to even look up to, is now here beside me.

The joy of this reality naturally brings a smile to my face.

She seems to feel the same. And then, unable to hold back any longer, she confesses.

“No! If we do that, I won’t be able to control myself!”

“What? Does that mean…”

“Just go to sleep already!”

Tenjō-san finally shakes off my hand, but she doesn’t leave my side.

“You always try to escape when you’re troubled.”

I can’t help but laugh. It’s just like the first time she shared her food with me.

Challenged by my words, she stiffens in frustration.

“Anyway, I’ll cancel the moving! Everything else is on hold! And no complaints allowed!”

“That’s the best medicine for me. Now I can sleep peacefully.”

Relieved, I lie back down in bed.

“I’ll watch over you until you fall asleep.”

“That’s going to make me restless, you know.”

“Just close your eyes, and you’ll eventually fall asleep. Don’t worry about me.”

“That’s impossible.”

“Impossible or not, just bear with it.”

“Alright, let’s see who can endure more. Goodnight.”

“Goodnight. Sweet dreams.”

I try to sound tough, but I’m reaching my limits in so many ways.

With the comforting feeling of having the person I like close by, I quickly fell asleep.
