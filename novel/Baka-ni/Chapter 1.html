Could it have been an illusion borne from the loneliness that a man who lives alone unconsciously carries?

Class 2-C, attendance number 23, and I’m not a member of any clubs.

I take my studies seriously, as per the agreement with my father who pays my living expenses, so my grades aren’t bad.

I have friends to talk to during breaks, but no one is close enough to call my best friend. No girlfriend either.

And my class’s homeroom teacher is none other than Reiyu Tenjō.

“No, but we did touch hands.”

The sensation and warmth of our overlapping hands were real.

The strawberries she brought over are still in my room’s refrigerator.

Eating them for breakfast, they are as sweet, juicy, and delicious as she described.

Near the entrance of my room, I kept the sandal she left behind in case of her return.

It’s like a fairy tale of Cinderella for her to leave her glass slipper behind.

If she were called a princess, I would immediately agree.

Reiyu Tenjō is indeed that strikingly beautiful.

Nevertheless, the Onee-san next door ran off at full speed as soon as I introduced myself.

“If a man living next door who has had no interaction with her knows her name then yeah… even if not Sensei, maybe any woman would get scared and run away…”

Her reaction was perfectly natural for a young woman.

After all, the probability of a student and a teacher coincidentally becoming neighbors is far more unlikely.

“Sensei living next door, can such a thing really happen?”

Despite my surprise, I found myself slightly excited.

Just knowing that a beautiful woman lives beyond the wall under the same roof makes my heart race.

Meaning, I’m sitting right in front of Sensei.

Despite the pressure she must feel having a student she met privately the night before sitting right in front of her, she handles it impressively.

Even if you live a decent life, such an amazing woman seemed like someone you’d never encounter in a lifetime.

The expression ‘stunningly beautiful’ was made a reality for me by Reiyu Tenjō.

However, my interest in her was not solely because of her looks.

That was Reiyu Tenjō.

Even in the midst of the staff room, I was spellbound by her profile and couldn’t move.

I was struck by how cool a woman not much different in age from myself appeared as she worked seriously.

I envied her ability to be so engrossed in her work so much that I hesitated to interrupt her to ask about the math teacher.

Her seriousness was admirable.

Being someone who struggles to work hard myself, I found myself admiring her.

And yet, it was a mystery that someone with such beauty had chosen to be a teacher.

My curiosity was piqued by so many thoughts, one after the other.

In essence, I found Reiyu Tenjō’s dedication endearing.

『Oh? Do you need something?』
