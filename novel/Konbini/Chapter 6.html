Chapter 6: Heading in the Opposite Direction Towards Hope!!

"I wonder if Riku will really come."

I checked the time and sighed.

"I have no choice but to go to Ayana's place alone..." As I waited in front of the ticket gate at the station.

I don't know what happened those two. But that doesn't matter. Just because I don't know doesn't mean I want to abandon my important friend.

And Riku's reaction... I think something unimaginable happened to me. I guess I'll just have to find something on my own .

Just as I gave up on Riku and started walking...

"Kana! Wait!"

"Eh... Riku, you came──"

I turned around and Riku was sweating as if he had just taken a shower.

"You're gross. Why are you sweating like that?"

"I... I ran here... Overslept. It's too early in the morning."

"Overslept, huh... Well, whatever."

Riku caught his breath and walked up to me.

Somehow... Somehow, I felt a different atmosphere than before.

"Well then, shall we go, Kana?"

"...Did something change?"

"Oh, you noticed? I just trimmed my bangs a little."

"I don't care, and it's not about that... The previous you we're like, there's someone behind you. But now it feels like your walking on your own."

As I said that, Riku showed a slight sign of thinking and then exclaimed as if he had just realized something.

"Yeah... Today, I feel like my mind were clearest than ever been. I feel refreshed."

"Oh, really?"

There was no lie in those words. Riku's face is truly brightened.

It was as if his heavy burden had been lifted.

We passed through the ticket gate and headed towards the platform. Just then, a train arrived.

After some time staring at train, Riku said....

───I can't take this anymore, I want to die.
