Interlude

At the temple of darkness.

However, the one being worship there wasn’t God, but the Demon King.

Abode of Demons(Fukumaden)—— the home of the Dark Magic Association Prospero.

Seven people, are prostrating in that place.

Living in the darkness, sacrificing themselves for the darkness, reformer and destroyer —— and, slaughterer.

In the back of the Fukumaden, a purple flame is burning like it’s the principal object of their worship.

The man standing in front of the flame, says.

“Weak man, thou name is —— human being”

The voice is beautiful, and it’s somehow melancholy.

“Can you hear, the griefs of man. The mourning actor on this foolish stage which is the world.”

“——Fartus”

The seven people respond solemnly. It’s Prospero’s prayer. A word that means 『Fate』. (TN: From the Latin word Fatum)

The man who was standing up in the purple flame, was a beautiful boy.

He looks like he’s in his mid-teens. He has white hair that extends to his shoulders and purple eyes.

His slightly melancholic mouth opens.

“I ask you. Should he live, or die?”

“——At the will of our Prophet, Master Lawrence.”

The seven people responded.

“Then, let’s hear him. Our comrade and brother, Sazaku.”

“……Ha”

One of the seven people who were prostrating —— Sazaku raises his masked face.

“Let me humbly say, Master Lawrence. This Sazaku, have gotten a clue about the 『Demon King’s Half Body (Juliet) 』.”

In his single line, the complexion of the other six people changed. One of them speaks in a threatening voice.

“Sazaku. If you’re playing a prank, I won’t forgive you.”

“Master Lawrence, the 『Demon King’s Half Body (Juliet) 』, whose whereabouts are still unknown, you also want to know it, right?”

An atmosphere that resembled bloodlust swirled.

“It’s not an official report yet. It’s still in the investigation stage …… However, the obstacles are bigger than I thought, so my power right now doesn’t match for it. By all means——”

The eyes of the mask glistened.

“Give me more power of the Demon King! The Sacred Demon King’s Flame!!”

Of the six, one speaks in a low voice.

“That is not needed. Leave it to me. Brother Sazaku.”

“No, this is still an uncertain story. If you can’t find a single mouse*, it will be an embarrassment for you, Brother Vauxhall” (TN: something like a play thing)

Bloodlust crosses between the two.

Filled with magic power, magic formulas are assembled between the two people.

“——Good job. Sazaku”

Lawrence’s words, eliminate the bloodlust of the two.

“That was close”

When Sazaku stood up, he bowed his head and stepped forward, as if he was scared.

Lawrence opens his clasped hand.

In the palm of his hand, a purple flame was burning.

It’s beautiful, but ominous —— a shimmer that can snatch away one’s heart.

Lawrence pressed his hand which was holding that against Sazaku’s chest.

The purple flame disappears, as if it was sucked into Sazaku’s body.

The next moment,

“Uooaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaahhh!!”

A scream of delight and despair burst out of Sazaku’s throat. His eyes are wide open under his mask, and blood vessels throughout his body emerge.

Lawrence stared expressionlessly at the scene, and took his hand off Sazaku.

“Bring back good news”

Sazaku was feeling full of power throughout his body.

The magic power he feels inside his body is also different.

It was a pleasure, as if he had been reborn as a higher-grade being.

“……I respectfully obey”

Sazaku puts his hand on his chest. The purple flame shimmered there.

“This —— with the flames of the 『Demon King’s Soul(Romesai)』 .”
