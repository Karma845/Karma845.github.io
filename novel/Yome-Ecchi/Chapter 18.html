It’s a fine line between standing out in a strange way and having it become known to everyone around you.

I guess we don’t have to be so careful.

Then we bought a toaster, a microwave, and so on.

There are other appliances I would like to buy, but they are not as expensive as the refrigerator.

I could probably get by with online shopping, where the after-sale service is a little worse.

It is a common occurrence that an appliance bought online has an initial defect, but the customer is told that the appliance may have been broken at home. For some reason, the shipping costs are paid by the customer, and the customer is often told to make the arrangements on their own.

Also, if we don’t arrange for delivery early, we will be put on a waiting list because they don’t have them in stock.

They tend to be in short supply now, just in time for the new season.

“Well, that’s……”

Mom must come by every so often to see how things are going.

We are sleeping separately, but it is because the bed is too small, and it would be strange to continue after we start our new life.

“Yeah, you did.”

“Let’s just sleep together like normal.”

“No, well, it’s that ……”

Suzuka’s argument is that it’s not strange.

It’s certainly not strange, and it’s not weird.

I can clearly see that my vocabulary has collapsed to the point of being unintelligible.

“Yes, it is. If you’re worried, we can sleep in the same bed today, even though it’s a little cramped.”

“Uh, eh, uh, yes.”

Me and Suzuka only proceeded slowly.

I’m so embarrassed, I can’t sleep.

“Ugh. Yuki, you’re heavy.”
