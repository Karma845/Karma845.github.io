Aristocratic Class Settings

The authority and post that the nobles within the story had, fitted (generally) within the modern terms.

* These were unique to this story. Some parts were different from the real world nobles.

[High Ranked Noble] 上級貴族

Noble who governed territory and commanded the middle and low ranked nobles in it as their caregiver.

Royal Family (王族)=Old-established family, financial conglomerate type. Cabinet and prime minister. Army’s supreme commander.

Duke (公爵)= Old-established family. Prefectural governor. Chief of police station and court. Doubled as diet member that also had minister post.

Margrave (辺境伯)=Equal to duke. In this story Dandall House and Melrose House had financial power and influence at the level of Tokyo Governor and Osaka Governor. Doubled as diet member that also had minister post.

Marquis (侯爵)=Prefectural governor that possessed ordinance-designated city or international airport. Chief of police station and court. Doubled as diet member.

Count (伯爵)=Prefectural governor. Chief of police station and court. Doubled as diet member.

[Middle Ranked Noble] 中級貴族

Noble who governed territory and commanded the low ranked nobles in it as their caregiver.

Viscount (子爵)=Mayor of ordinance-designated city. Doubled as chief of police station and judge.

Baron (男爵)=Mayor and chief of police station, and also judge.

.

[Low Ranked Noble] 下級貴族

Civil official or knight that served the country or noble house. Individual whose achievement was recognized by the country.

Baronet (準男爵)=Town mayor and chief of police station, also doubled as city council member or simple court.

Honorable noble (one generational noble)=Owner of a famous company in a town. Doubled as town council member.

Knight (士爵/騎士爵)=Town chief or landlord. Doubled as town council member and police box.
