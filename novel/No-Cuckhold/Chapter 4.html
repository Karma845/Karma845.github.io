But it was already past five o’clock. I was thinking of killing some time appropriately because I don’t intend to stay that late,……, but I wonder why I came here.

“Uwaa,……, it’s as lively as usual here”

Despite tilting my head at Ayana’s words, the place we were headed for was an arcade.

I know this is not the best place to take a girl, but strangely enough, this place caught my eye as I passed by.

“Towa-kun, let’s have some fun.”

“O-ou.”

Ayana was more enthusiastic than I expected and took me by the hand. We looked around for something we could both enjoy and found a field hockey game.

“Let’s try this, okay?”

The battle began with both of us on the other side of the table, but ……uhm it was my overwhelming victory.

“I lost. ……”

She looked downcast, but she was not in any way unfaithful. She seemed to really enjoy being with me, and immediately smiled.

Looking at that smile, my own cheeks naturally relax and I feel like I should be enjoying the moment to the fullest.

“Ah, Towa-kun, how about that one?”

“Let’s do it!”

Ayana pointed her finger at a game where you beat a drum rhythmically to see how high you can get a score.

Ayana’s confidence with the stick in her hands made me think I needed to get into the game as well,……, and I won again by a huge margin over Ayana.

“I lost this one too. ……”

Sadly, Ayana was found to be quite weak in games like this.

It’s not every day that I come to an arcade with a girl …… and that’s exactly why I never had this experience in my previous life.

“I knew there was such a thing.”

“….Kuku”

“Wait for me. I’ll help you out of there in a minute.”

But what am I supposed to do with it now that I’ve taken it? I’m not going to put it on my bag or baggage,……. I was just thinking about this when Ayana came back and I offered her the keychain to see how she would react.

Ayana’s cheeks relaxed with happiness when she saw the key chain.

“It’s sooooo cute! Is it okay?””

This thing is cute? Ayana took the keychain from my hand that had such a question.

I was surprised to see Ayana holding the keychain to her chest, but at the same time, for some reason, I also felt nostalgic about her appearance.

“…… Ah.”

In that moment of nostalgia, I thought I saw little Ayana right next to Ayana again.

In the end, it soon disappeared as if it were an optical illusion, but it was a fact that I felt something special in this place where I played with Ayana. I don’t think I’ll bring Ayana with me so often, but if I have time, it doesn’t seem bad to visit here alone and feel this atmosphere again.

Basically, it’s something that’s always placed in these types of arcades, but it’s something that I’ve never really been a part of, including in my previous life.

“Let’s take a picture, Towa-kun.”

“I often come here with my friends. I like to make her eyes extremely large, but I always end up making a monster.”

Ayana seemed to be enjoying herself as she recalled that time.

It’s true that these things come with processing, but I’m not sure if even a beautiful girl like Ayana would end up looking like a youkai …… that’s what I’m wondering.

As soon as the cover was rolled up, I was greeted by the photo, which was indeed a face that would convince me if it were called a monster.

It was a bit peculiar to look at, and it would naturally bring a smile to one’s face if one saw it when one was feeling down.

“Okay, I’m ready to go. Towa-kun, please stand next to me.”

Now you have one more memory to add to your collection, right?”
