“Shu-kun, this part is incorrect, you know?”

“Eh”

“The digits are completely off. And here, the rows are off by one.”

“…..”

I realized my mistakes when Ayana pointed them out. I understood that everyone made small errors while thinking about something else while working, but since I had been asked by Iori-senpai to help, I had to make sure to be careful.

I slapped my cheek as Towa had done earlier, making a satisfying sound. It stung a bit, and I turned a little red, but it re-energized me.

“Shu-senpai is really fired up now!”

“Fufu, it’s a good thing to have motivation. Keep up the good work, Shu-kun.”

When Ayana praised me, I couldn’t help but feel a sense of accomplishment, even though I hadn’t sought praise.

“Tell me if you need any help, okay? I’ll assist with anything.”

Ayana leaned over, looking into my face. It was the first time she had looked at me so closely. “Anything?” I thought to myself, remembering those words I had seen online. But I knew I would never make any weird requests.

“It’s alright. I can handle it by myself.”

“Waa… You look so cool, Shu-senpai!”

I didn’t mean to show off, but hearing it from Mari made me feel genuinely happy.

Ayana looked surprised at my response.

“What’s wrong?”

“Nothing. I’m just a bit surprised.”

“Why?”

“I thought you would ask for help right away, Shu-kun. I didn’t expect you to decline so confidently.”

“…I just wanted to look cool, that’s all.”

“Eh?”

“Nothing!”

I had raised my voice, and I felt a bit embarrassed about it, but I focused on my work again.

I wondered if I might have been showing off a bit too much. I also worried if I might come off as weird. While thinking about this, I glanced over at Towa and Iori-senpai.

“Yeah, that’s right. It’s fine like this.”

“Good to know.”

“As for the next set of documents… Can you handle them?”

“Of course, leave it to me.”

“You’re reliable.”

Their conversation flowed so smoothly.

Iori-senpai was often seen as cold and distant because of her cool appearance and way of speaking, but she was indeed a kind person.

…But I couldn’t help feeling a bit uncomfortable about how close she and Towa seemed to be.

“…Fufu.”

Ayana laughed as she watched the two of them.

Even just her profile had a significant impact, and Ayana was most lovely when she smiled like that.

As I kept watching, Ayana turned her face towards me. When she tilted her head, I couldn’t help but say:

“Hey… You’re smiling more today than usual.”

“Really?”

“Yeah. Towa being here definitely contributes, but you seem to have a lot of fun when you’re talking with Iori-senpai and the others.”

“…”

“Huh…? Did I say something strange?”

Ayana seemed even more surprised by my words.

Ayana appeared completely bewildered as she averted her gaze from me, her focus shifting toward Towa and Iori-senpai. She even began to look at Mari, who was sitting next to her.

“Ayana-senpai?”

“…It’s nothing, really.”

She replied, looking down, her behavior obviously out of the ordinary.

I was about to ask her what was wrong, but Towa acted before I did.

“Ayana, what’s wrong?”

“…Towa-kun.”

Towa. Even though he had been talking with Iori-senpai just moments ago, he naturally approached Ayana like a hero coming to her rescue.

“Are you okay?”

“Yes, well… I just zoned out for a moment.”

Towa leaned over Ayana’s face with a worried expression. Even someone of the same sex as me had to admit that Towa looked cool. Yet, at the same time, I couldn’t help but feel a tinge of jealousy.

Although I realized it too, it seemed that the situation was being dominated by Towa, making me feel left out. Everyone, including Iori-senpai and Mari, was watching Towa’s actions closely.

“I’m really fine. Besides, Towa-kun, you’ve been spacing out quite a bit recently, so maybe I caught it from you.”

“That’s… not a pleasant way to catch it.”

I had no idea what they were talking about. They were chuckling, and Towa had a slightly worried expression, a conversation that only the two of them seemed to understand. I felt like an outsider.

Despite thinking that, I chose not to point it out, wanting to avoid making things awkward. We pushed through and finished our tasks. By the time we were done tidying up, everyone seemed satisfied. I felt a strong sense of accomplishment, even more than usual.

“Today, it was great that we got to chat not only while working but also with Ayana and Yukishiro-kun.”

“That’s true. I usually have club activities, so I don’t get many opportunities like this. It was really enjoyable!”

Iori-senpai and Mari seemed content. As the tasks were completed, it was time to disband. However, I found myself pulled away by Iori-senpai and Mari, losing my chance to leave.

“You really did your best today, Shu-kun.”

“Ah, I guess so…”

“Absolutely! You were amazing, Shu-senpai!”

“Ahaha… Thank you, both of you.”

Although I had been shown as a cool guy to Ayana, I couldn’t deny that it was a bit of an overstatement. Nevertheless, I felt a strong sense of achievement, and the warm sensation of my arms being wrapped by both of them enhanced the feeling.

(But…)

Even amidst all this, I couldn’t help but wonder about Ayana’s bewildered expression. I had never seen her like that before. I couldn’t help but wonder what she was thinking at that moment.
