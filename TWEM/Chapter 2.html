Chapter 2 – The Heroes’ Labyrinth Raid

—The day Haruto and his group departed from the royal capital, a little back in time.

Tendo, Ichinomiya, Mogami, Asakura, and Shinonome were on the 19th floor, the middle floor of the dungeon.

According to prior information, the dungeon was expected to have a total of thirty floors. Halfway through the dungeon, from the 15th floor, the monsters became stronger, making it difficult for even the best adventurers to advance.

However, the heroes had successfully reached that point, despite their struggles.

And among them, Tendo’s party was at the forefront of the raid.

“Aaarghh!!”

“Doryaa”

“Not yet.”

Two level 60 monsters were defeated by the attacks of the three vanguards, Tendo, Mogami, and Shinonome.

Thanks to the coordination of Ichinomiya, who was in charge of recovery, and Asakura, who was in charge of magic attack, the five of them were able to attack without difficulty.

It took them three days to reach the Nargadia Labyrinth after leaving the royal capital of Glicente, and four days to reach the 19th floor.

Their raid speed was unbelievable.

“Do you think there are any stairs leading down?”

Tendo replied to Asakura’s words.

“I haven’t found it yet. We’ve been lucky so far, so we’ll just have to search slowly…”

“I see…”

Asakura said regretfully, but Ichinomiya tilted her head.

“You don’t look disappointed, Natsuki?”

“Was I found out? Actually, I like this kind of thing because it’s fun, like a game.”

Asakura, who doesn’t hesitate to call herself an otaku, said with a laugh, and Tendo called for attention.

“Natsuki. Quite a number of adventurers have died in this labyrinth. So we need to keep our heads up as well.”

“Sheesh, I know that much!”

Asakura puffed out her cheeks like a child and stuck out her tongue, causing the group to laugh at her.

The atmosphere was cheerful, but they did not let their guard down.

The group’s current level was approximately 70. Which was quite high for a human considering that Kusel’s level was 64 when Haruto first met her.

In addition, all four of them, except for Tendo who has the holy sword Mistilteinn, had been given weapons by Haruto that he claimed are prototypes.

The weapons are not prototypes at all, but were first-class products that would make the craftsmen of this world swoon.

The level of the enemies in this floor was 60.

The dungeon had a mid-level boss every five floors, and a powerful monster as boss monster every ten floors.

The boss battle on the next floor would be even stronger than the previous one.

All five of them knew this, and so they kept their vigilance and tension intact.

Then, after searching for the stairs for a while, Asakura finally spoke up.

“There it is——‼”

As Tendo and the others gathered, they found a hole hidden in the shadows and a staircase leading to the bottom.

“Found it at last…geez, how I wish it wouldn’t be so hard to find.”

“Honestly…”

Tendo said disgustedly, and everyone nodded, including Ichinomiya.

～

The party descended the stairs and sat down in front of the boss room.

“Let’s take a break to prepare for the boss battle. What do you think guys?”

No one objected to Tendo’s suggestion.

This floor only had the boss room, with no other monster in sight.

Since this was not the case in the other floors, the party took turns resting, but their fatigue had reached its peak.

After setting up their tents, eating, and cleaning themselves up, Tendo and the others took their time to rest for the first time in a long time. When they woke up, they began their strategy meeting.

“Then, as usual, Shinya, Aoi and I will be in the vanguard. Natsuki and Suzuno will be in the rear guard as support. That would be our formation. Since we can’t say for sure what’s going to happen, we will be relying on you two in the rear guard.”

“Got it.”

“Leave it to us!”

Nodding at their reply, Tendo continued to explain the strategy.

As usual, Mogami and Shinonome would draw the enemy’s attention with their close-quarters attacks, while Ichinomiya and Asakura in the rear guard would take charge of recovery and guerilla attacks. The idea is to take advantage of the gaps in the enemy’s defense, to allow Tendo to release his powerful attacks.

“Like I said earlier, we never know what’s around the corner. So be sure to keep your guards up.”

 ““““Roger!””””

With that voice, Tendo and the others stepped into the boss monster’s room.

In the boss’ room was a Minotaur that the party had defeated in the mid-level boss battle back on the fifth floor.

“A Minotaur, here of all places?”

Ichinomiya replied to Mogami’s puzzled head.

“There’s something different about it. It’s bluish-purple in color, and it’s…”

Tendo said, following Ichinomiya’s words.

“Yeah, that weapon isn’t just some random axe, it’s some kind of magic sword. Moreover, even my appraisal couldn’t identify it and only displays as unknown. There’s something disturbing about it, and you can bet that it’s much, much stronger.”

At Tendo’s words, Mogami and the others, all four of them became visibly nervous.

At the same time as the Minotaur looked at Tendo and his party, the door slammed shut behind them with great force.

 —GUOOOOOOOHHhh!

The minotaur let out a roar.

It was an intimidating roar that would make any normal person freeze up while pissing their pants.

However, contrary to the Minotaur’s expectations, Tendo and the others were calm.

“Intimidation huh, it’s nothing compared to Haruto’s.”

“Well, Haruto is amazing in many ways like that…”

Mogami, Asakura and Shinonome nodded as Tendo and Ichinomiya chuckled saying that.

However, all five of them were not letting their guards down.

Even though they were not as good as Haruto, they could tell that the Minotaur was a powerful enemy.

It was also in a different league compared to the monsters they’ve faced in the past.

Tendo was almost frightened for a moment, but then he reminded himself that he would not be able to move forward without defeating it.

“—Shall we?”

At Tendo’s words, the four of them nodded, their expressions tightening.

At the same time, the Minotaur thrust out its horns and charged forward.

It closed the distance in an instant, but even so, Tendo and the others were able to evade it without danger and prepared to attack.

—BUMOOOOO!

However, the Minotaur braked and swung the battle axe in its hand at Mogami, who was about to attack it.

The Minotaur hurriedly distanced itself as Asakura’s magic hit it in the face, while Mogami braced himself for a direct hit.

“Wow, not even a scratch! This Mr. Mino has such a strong defense!”

“Mr. Mino…?”

“You know, saying Minotaur is kinda of a drag and long.”

While listening to the exchange between the dissatisfied Asakura and Ichinomiya, who seemed like she had something to say, the vanguards, Tendo, Mogami, and Shinonome, readied their weapons.

Asakura applied a support spell that increased attack power and speed to the three of them.

“Oh nice!”

“Thanks, Natsuki!”

“Thank you, Natsuki!”

The three of them thanked Asakura and headed towards the Minotaur.

The Minotaur responded by swinging its battle axe, but it could not handle the simultaneous attacks of the three of them, and its body was gradually scarred.

And then, when its strength had been reduced considerably, the atmosphere suddenly changed.

Tendo and the others took their distance at once and prepared themselves.

They watched intently, trying not to miss the Minotaur’s every move—and then it made its move.

The Minotaur slammed its battle-axe into the ground with all its might, and the debris and stones that flew off the floor were flung away by the battle-axe.

“Everyone, get behind me!”

The four of them moved behind her as quickly as Ichinomiya instructed.

“—Sacred Barrier!”

The Sacred Barrier is a light magic that creates a hemispherical defensive barrier with a radius of two meters.

At the same time as its deployment, multiple fragments hit the barrier.

The barrier repelled the incoming attacks, and just as Ichinomiya was about to breathe a sigh of relief—a crack appeared in the barrier.

“““““Oh no?”””””

Tendo and the others all shouted in surprise.

 

Although they could not see it through the debris and smoke, the Minotaur had approached them before they knew it and was wielding its axe.

The barrier that was struck by the battle axe gradually widened the crack with an unpleasant creaking sound, probably because the minotaur applied even more force.

Ichinomiya was visibly upset when the strongest barrier she could use was about to be broken, but she quickly raised her voice.

“I’ll count to three, so fall back!”

At the sound of her voice, Tendo and the others prepared themselves.

“Three, two, one —now!”

As soon as the count was over, Ichinomiya broke the spell and the Tendo and the rest jumped back from the spot.

The Minotaur, on the other hand, had been leaning forward with all its strength, and when the barrier suddenly disappeared, it lost its balance and thrust its battle axe into the ground.

In the meantime, Tendo, Mogami, and Shinonome approached the Minotaur, and Ichinomiya unleashed her magic.

“Everyone shut your eyes!”

The moment Tendou and the others closed their eyes, Ichinomiya’s Light Magic, Flash burned the Minotaur’s eyes.

—Boo-moooow!?

The Minotaur, which had lost its stance and vision, let out a cry.

“Now!”

Hearing Ichinomiya’s voice, Tendo and the others opened their eyes and immediately went on the offensive.

First, Mogami uses all his strength to thrust his right fist at the Minotaur’s abdomen.

“Go to hell! You son of a b*tch!”

The Minotaur let go of its battle axe and fell to one knee.

Bmoooo…it made a pained sound, but still didn’t collapse.

Next, Shinonome sheathed her sword once and approached with the fast movement skill, Ground Shrink…

“Shinonome Style Technique Eight Stance—Shiden Raiko!”

She unleashed a swordflash.

At the same time as her blade loosened from its sheath, a blue-white lightning flashed around the blade, and the blade swung at ultra-high speed.

The Minotaur’s right arm, from the shoulder up, fell off, but there was no blood. This is because the cut was burned by lightning.

Shinonome has fused the techniques she learned at her family’s dojo with the magic and skills of this other world.

This is one of those techniques, and by using lightning magic to speed up the extraction of the sword, and then applying lightning magic to the slash, it is possible to apply a burn effect, as in this case, or to shower it with electric shocks even if it is blocked.

The Minotaur groaned, but managed to get up and tried to pick up the battle axe with its left hand.

But to stop it, Asakura cast an attack magic.

“No you don’t! —Fire Wolf!”

The fire magic created flames in the shape of three wolves. They ran on the ground with such sense of movement as if they were alive, and bit into the Minotaur’s left arm and both legs.

At the same time, more flames leaked from the wolves’ mouths.

“Just get burned to ashes!”

Just as Asakura had said, the wolves that had taken a bite at the Minotaur began to burn fiercely as it clung to the minotaur.

—BUMOOOOOOOOO!

The Minotaur, its entire body burned and screaming in agony, swung its left arm as if it was trying to muster its last bit of strength, and the flames disappeared.

Tendo immediately slashed at it.

The Minotaur tried to react as quickly as possible, but it couldn’t keep up with Tendo’s movements using both the Invincible skill, which the Holy Sword can only use once a day, and Ground Shrink.

“With this, it’s game over!”

Tendo leapt on top of the minotaur, wrapped the holy sword with holy magic, and swung it down with great momentum.

At the same time as Tendo landed, the Minotaur’s body split in half vertically and crumbled.

A moment of silence fell over the place…

“—We did it! We defeated it! We finally defeated it!”

Asakura’s voice echoed.

“Haa haa, phew, guess that finally did the trick…”

Tendo said, breathing heavily as his strength and magic power were severely depleted from the earlier attack.

“We did it!”

“That’s right!”

“If we hadn’t defeated it, we would have been in trouble…”

Ichinomiya, Mogami and Shinonome shouted with joy.

“Right. Since we defeated it! It looks like our level has risen, so let’s take a break and check it out.”

All four of them nodded at Tendo’s words.

After checking each of their levels, Tendo and the others shouted in surprise.

“Wasn’t Suzuno and Natsuki’s level around 69 or something? Their levels seem to have gone up a few.”

“Yeah, maybe because it’s a boss monster, and they got all the experience points they needed from it.”

After taking a short break while talking about this, the party headed to the lower floor.

～

“…Is it just me or are the monsters that have been appearing for a while now as strong as the Minotaur…?”

“That’s true…maybe because the next floor is the boss monster?”

Mogami tilted his head at Tendo’s words.

The group had reached the twenty-fourth floor over the course of a day.

Up until the previous floor, the number of enemies had not increased, but they had gradually become stronger, but here on the 24th floor, in return for the number decreasing significantly, the power of each and every one of them had increased.

Back in the Royal Capital, they were impacted with knowledge regarding dungeons, and have even been in a few.

But this was the first time they had seen such distribution of monsters.

With irregularities like this, it would be better to take every precaution and challenge the mid-level boss monster.

At Tendo’s suggestion, they decided to take a rest in front of the mid-level boss room on the 25th floor.

The next day, Tendo opened the door to the middle boss monster’s room and muttered to himself.

“I knew it…”

Normally, the mid-level boss monster on the 25th floor would be about as strong as the one on the 20th floor.

But the Chimera in front of them was clearly much stronger than the Minotaur.

The pressure was overwhelming on Tendo and the rest of the party.

“Something may very well be going on in this labyrinth in particular.”

They all nodded at Ichinomiya’s words.

The reason why this dungeon has suddenly become bizarre is probably due to the boss monster at the lowest floor.

It’s certain to be stronger than this middle boss, and I’m not sure if we can defeat it…

Tendo shook his head and put that thought out of his mind.

Anyway, for now, we have to defeat this chimera.

Together with Tendo, who had readied his weapon, the heroes dashed towards the Chimera.

～

—Two hours after the battle began.

“Haa, haa……fi, finally, we, won…”

Tendo and his party won the fierce battle and were able to defeat the Chimera.

It was Shinonome who dealt the finishing blow. This time, Tendo used his Invincible skill as a shield and she took advantage of the opening he created to send the chimera’s head flying.

And now, battered and bruised from the battle, Tendo and the others were lying on the ground.

“We somehow managed to pull it off…”

“True…”

“I thought I was a goner this time…”

“Me too honestly…”

“It was tough…”

Tendo, Ichinomiya, Mogami, Shinonome, and Asakura each shouted in exhaustion.

After a while, the five of them finally recovered and resumed their raid.

That day, they reached the 28th floor, and the next day, they finally broke through the 29th floor and were standing in front of the boss room on the 30th floor.

“So this is the last room…”

“Seems like it.”

Mogami nodded at Tendo’s words.

“Finally.”

“Yea. It was quite the hurdle.”

“I wonder how many times I almost died.”

Asakura, Ichinomiya, and Shinonome muttered as they looked at the door in that order.

Tendo looked around at the four of them with a serious look on his face.

“We may very well die here. But…”

There is a possibility that they could die here. Which was why Tendo emphasized it again.

“But, I’m not turning back now!”

The four of them nodded in agreement to his words.

The expression on their faces was full of determination.

—They were determined to see it through.

Tendo and the others put their hands on the door of the boss’s room.
