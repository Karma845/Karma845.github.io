When Falma taps into the divine power of the great treasure, Falma himself emits a strong light, giving rise to him being lifted, a sudden buoyancy, by unknown force. “What’s your plan? Are you trying to get rid of us at the bottom of this lake?!“

At that time… a huge, dark figure shadow appears from the bottom of the underground lake. The figure doesn’t show its whole body and begins to open its jaw to swallow Pierre who has fallen into the water and is currently floating at the surface. It’s mouth opens as wide as a whale?

However, just before Pierre is about to be swallowed by the monster’s mouth, Falma feels a tingling sensation on his hand as he touches the water of the underground lake.
