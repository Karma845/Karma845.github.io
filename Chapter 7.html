The Ashby family's resident butler - Satya's father, Karen's personal maid - was a very meticulous man, who respected a certain order when packing things into boxes.

Heavy items such as tea bags were placed on the bottom, and fragile items such as dishes and bottles were wrapped in cloth or paper and placed on top. The arrangement of the items was always the same, with the perfume <Aeris no. 6> sitting in the center of them.

However, this arrangement is clearly disordered.

There is no way that such a diligent butler could have been the cause of such a peculiar mishap. It would also be unthinkable for the shipping company to change the arrangement on its own.

─Could it be that someone else had intervened?

It seemed like a rather absurd notion, but she couldn't help but check.

The first thing she checked was the perfume.

At first glance, there was nothing unusual about the jade-colored bottle containing the liquid. She inspected it from all angles and held it up to the light to see if there was any discrepancy.

"...... This is."

She noticed that the adhesive on the label wrapped around the bottle had shifted just a little.

Of course, <Aeris no. 6> is a custom-made bottle, hand-filled by artisans just for Karen. A mistake of this magnitude would be acceptable.

But on that day, she could not miss the slightest change.

She peeled the jade-colored bottle bare, peeling her fingernail across the small gap between the tightly glued labels. In doing so, she discovered conclusive evidence.

The label was covering a hole so small that a needle could fit through it.

The hole had been sealed with a piece of glass of the same color, but there were clearly marks where it had been forcibly glued in place.

─Was a poison injected into the needle?

─No, what would be the point of putting something like that in a perfume?

─The purpose is something more .......

It didn't take her long to come to a conclusion.

"...... Forced Deactivation, huh?"

Karen utilized several <conditioning> techniques to control her overly powerful psychic abilities.

Not only did she forcibly increase her concentration with breathing techniques she learned at the shady dojo, but she also imposed on herself that she could only reach her maximum output if she were to fight against students from the Heiberg School who had harmed either her or Satya.

And then there comes the perfume.

By smelling the fragrance of this <Aeris no. 6>, she will be able to force herself to stop before her psychic ability goes out of control.

Most likely, the enemy took a few drops of the perfume from the bottle.

It is a shallow strategy to soak a cloth with it or put it in a spray can and then approach her during a <duel>.

"...... You filthy rats."

Although she could not hide her surprise at the fact that someone would do such a thing a week before she initiated the plan, she had nothing to fear once the seed of the trick had been cracked.

In short, all she has to do is rewrite it.

Karen is a genius at self-brainwashing.

Within a week from now, it should be possible to <re-condition> herself using a different perfume. Once that was done, it was time to take action.

As she walked to the public phone in the women's dormitory, she considered which perfume to choose with the same ease she would consider a gift for a friend.

Suddenly, an option popped into her mind.

Considering the time required for conditioning training, the perfume should be ready by the end of the day. If so, it couldn't be anything other than a popular product widely available in the market.

She put her ear to the phone and dialed the number of the shipping company.

The person that answered the phone was clearly horrified. Perhaps he was bribed with money, but someone so cowardly would not be suited for such treachery. Without needing to interrogate him, her hypothesis was confirmed.

She ruthlessly told him to deliver the perfume to the dormitory by the end of the day, and that if he betrayed her again, he would be dead, and slammed the phone down to further incite fear.
