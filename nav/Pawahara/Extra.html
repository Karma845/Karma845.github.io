Afterword

Nice to meet you, I’m Nekomata Nuko.

Thank you very much for picking up “How I, with Zero Otaku Knowledge, Ended Up Enjoying Otaku Activities with a Gal Who Hates Guys.”

This story revolves around a high school boy who never delved into the realms of anime, manga, or games and a hardcore otaku gal, as they enjoy otaku activities and deepen their bonds.

I’ve always been fond of lighthearted stories, and lately, my heart has been yearning for even more lightness. That’s why I made sure to keep the serious developments in this work close to zero.

I sincerely hope that those of you who read this story can feel a bit brighter and happier.

Now, let me express my gratitude.

I’d like to thank many people who lent their support in bringing this work to publication. Everyone in the Dash X Bunko editorial team, including my editor, thank you so much.

A special thanks to Chigusa Minori-sensei for creating wonderful illustrations amidst a busy schedule.

To the proofreaders, designers, and everyone involved in this work, thank you very much.

Above all, thank you to the readers who picked up this book. There is no greater happiness than knowing you found some enjoyment in it.

So, until we meet again somewhere.
