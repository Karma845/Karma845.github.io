The classification of computer classifies the different types of computer. This classification is mostly on what or how powerful your computer is. Example of this is Supercomputer, Mainframe computer, Minicomputer, and Micro Computer.

- Supercomputer is the very fastest and powerful, and expensive type of computer for processing data.

- Mainframe computers are multi-programming, high-performance computers, and multi-user, which means they can handle the workload of more than 100 users at a time on the computer.

- Minicomputer is a digital and multi-user computer system with the connection of more than one CPU.

- Micro Computer also known as "Personal Computer". The design of microcomputers is minimal in size and storage capacity. These computers consist of many parts like Input and Output devices, Software, operating systems, networks, and Servers all these need to connect to form a complete Personal Digital Computer.
