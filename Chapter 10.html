Afterword

Do you know the iconic item that often appears in movies about fraud?

Just by its appearance on the screen, the tension of the entire audience explodes. You can rewind and watch that scene over and over again without getting bored. If you want, you can even watch it as a still image. It is such a cool and powerful item.

Well, if you are an astute reader, you already know what I mean.

Yes, that's right.

The most powerful item is the [Schematic Diagram].

It is the usual scene where the villains gather in a dark and dusty room and have a strategy meeting while staring at the [Schematic Diagram] pasted on the wall...... Is there anyone in the world whose heart does not skip a beat at this?

Try to put up sticky notes with photos of the target, maps, and various other information in a disorderly manner, or write arrows or figures in the margins in detail. See? ....... Of course, that would be cool, right?

To tell you the truth, the moment I was able to depict the strategy meeting scene using [Schematic Diagram], it was as if the victory of this work was already decided. The deceptions and tricks that unfolded over the course of more than 300 pages were all conceived on the premise that [Schematic Diagram] would appear in the story. That is why [Schematic Diagram] is such a supreme existence for fraud schemes. It is no exaggeration to say that the reason why [Schematic Diagram] did not appear in the first volume was a prelude to producing this extraordinarily powerful catharsis. As a side note, I now hold the title of the author who has used the word [Schematic Diagram] the most in the afterword of all light novels. This is a great honor.

Of course, the [Schematic Diagram] will appear in the third volume as well. What kind of activities will be shown in the next volume, I guess only God knows. ...... I hope we have a blessed future.
