Prologue: Online Gaming Couples Chat Log

Mahorin: Hey, you’re here! I knew Jet Black-kun would log in!

Jet Black Yasha: Of course, I was waiting for you, Mahorin!

Mahorin: It’s the ‘Isekai Homeroom‘, right?

Jet Black Yasha: Absolutely! I was thinking of saving it for tomorrow, but I couldn’t resist chatting with you right away!

Mahorin: Me too! It was consistently interesting from the first episode, but the excitement in the final episode was extraordinary! The rendezvous with the students council president which leading to a joint effort was awesome!

Jet Black Yasha: Even the delinquent group undergoing a change of heart was so moving!

Mahorin: Totally! I cried when the teacher rushed in!

Jet Black Yasha: Right? Although I knew the plot, the roll call scene and such were so emotional! And at the very end, they dropped the bombshell, ‘Season 2 confirmed!’ I was so surprised; I made a weird noise, haha.

Mahorin: Me too, lol. Kotomi-san must have been screaming too.

Jet Black Yasha: She was screaming a lot, lol

Mahorin: As expected! By the way, Jet Black-kun, what time are you planning to sleep tonight?

Jet Black Yasha: I don’t think I’ll be able to sleep for another hour.

Mahorin: Then, how about a call?

Mahorin: Heeey ~

Mahorin: Jet Black-kun?

Jet Black Yasha: Sorry, sorry, I went to the bathroom! I’d love to share my thoughts with you over a call, but unfortunately, my phone seems to have disappeared. I think I can find it if I search, but who knows when that’ll happen! It’s not fair to keep you waiting until then, right? I think continuing our chat would be a more meaningful use of time. What do you think?

Mahorin: Should I try calling and see if it rings?

Jet Black Yasha: Wait, wait!

Jet Black Yasha: Found it!

Mahorin: That’s great. So, should we make a call?

Mahorin: Heeey ~

Mahorin: Are you not in the mood for a call?

Jet Black Yasha: No, no! I really want to call! But when I checked just now, Kotomi was already asleep! I’m sure if I talk to you on the phone, I’ll get all excited and start yelling, and then Kotomi will wake up!

Mahorin: Wasn’t she screaming a while ago?

Jet Black Yasha: Kotomi falls asleep easily!

Mahorin: I see. Then let’s stick to chatting for today! We can discuss our otaku activities another time!

Jet Black Yasha: Just the two of us?

Mahorin: Kotomi-san will join too!

Jet Black Yasha: Thanks! Kotomi will be thrilled! It’s fine if you girls want to chat alone, though!

Mahorin: I won’t leave you out, Jet Black-kun! Speaking of which, do you know that the campaign for ‘Passionate Warrior Princess‘ starts the day after tomorrow?

Jet Black Yasha: Of course. If you buy two ice creams at the convenience store, you get a clear file, right? They’re all cute, but the illustration with everyone together is especially amazing!

Mahorin: I know! It was the one where they were eating ice cream on the bench in front of the convenience store! It’s so precious how Hot Blood-chan dropped her ice cream, and Bit Hot-chan is feeding her own ice cream, lol.

Jet Black Yasha: Exactly. Hot Blood-chan, Bit Hot-chan, Baby Hot-chan, and Devotee-chan1, they’re all so cute. I want to collect them all!

Mahorin: Stuff like that is hard to use outside, but somehow you end up wanting to complete the set!

Jet Black Yasha: Totally! But I’m kinda short on cash. Anyway, I’ll definitely get the one with everyone together, and the rest will need a discussion with my wallet. What about you, Mahorin?

Mahorin: The only choice is completing the set. Especially, I must get BIt Hot-chan!

Jet Black Yasha: You really like Bit Hot-chan lol

Mahorin: I automatically like little sister characters. Plus, Bit Hot-chan has the same birthday as me!

Jet Black Yasha: Really? July 2nd is just two weeks away!

Mahorin: That was quick response! You know Bit Hot-chan’s birthday?

Jet Black Yasha: I saw it at the recent art exhibition.

Mahorin: Ah, that makes sense. By the way, when’s your birthday, Jet Black-kun?

Jet Black Yasha: Same as MioMio from Driste!

Mahorin: No way!? Lucky you. But it’s still a while away. You should call me senpai until then lol

Jet Black Yasha: Mahorin-senpai, I have something to ask!

Mahorin: What is it, kouhai?

Jet Black Yasha: What kind of present would you like?

Mahorin: A present, huh? Anime goods would be great. Are you getting me one?

Jet Black Yasha: Of course! I’ll prepare the best present!

Mahorin: Thanks! Then, I’ll look forward to it!

Translation notes:

  1. They do not have ‘furigana’ because they are adjective (We fell into a trap). The author shows ‘different’ kinds of hot. Such as, 熱血, 微熱, 知恵熱, and 熱愛. If you check closely they have one thing in common: 熱. These phrases are super common in JP phrases, such as JP video games. ↩︎
