“That’s why Takashi-kun! Let’s go to a bar today!!”

One evening, my Senpai, who had returned from university classes earlier than me, suddenly proposed something unusual.

Kureha Senpai, who rarely suggests eating out, was now saying, “Let’s go to a bar.”

I couldn’t help but be wary that something was up.

“Um, what do you mean by ‘that’s why’?”

I braced myself for some teasing, ready for whatever she might throw at me.

I couldn’t always be the other end of Senpai’s jokes. I had to fight back a little.

That’s what I was thinking.

However, it seemed my wariness was unnecessary as Kureha Senpai began to look at me with serious eyes.

“You know, I’ve been thinking a little since then. About how I could have a good time with Takashi-kun.”

“Since the day you came back from cheating on me with your best friend, having yakiniku and drinks.”

Her words dripped out, reminding me of my recent blunder.

Despite having Kureha Senpai, I had gone out for yakiniku and drinks with a friend of the opposite sex—a grave mistake I should not have made.

And then, unavoidably, I remembered the punishment that followed.

A stimulating punishment that was eighty percent reward and twenty percent hardship, typical of Senpai.

Even now, just thinking about it brought back the heat of that moment.

“But I explained that I couldn’t refuse, and you understood that, didn’t you? I told you that Yuu is just a close friend and there’s nothing romantic between us!”

I vehemently denied Senpai’s words as if to drive away the sensations that were starting to return.

Of course I was entirely at fault, and I had received a fitting punishment, which I had no complaints about.

But it was tough to have it dredged up again.

I had demonstrated my innocence. I had also clearly stated and demonstrated that I was devoted only to Senpai.

That’s why I thought she had “understood.”

I hadn’t expected her to bring it up again, and in my panic, I raised my voice.

But, once again, my worries were for naught.

“Yes, of course I understood that part. I knew how much you love me, Takashi-kun.”

“I do love you.”

“I love the blushing you, too~”

“There you go again, Senpai, doing something like that…!”

“Hmm~?”

“No, I just thought you seemed to be having too much fun by yourself…”

“Yes, everything is fun when I’m with Takashi-kun~”

Although I felt a twinge of frustration at being teased by Senpai again, seeing her cheeks flushed with joy and having fun made all my irritation seem trivial.

In fact, if my recent blunder had been elevated to a source of teasing for Senpai, then perhaps that was something to be happy about.

I had vowed not to do anything that would sadden Senpai again.

“By the way, what does what you just said have to do with going to a bar? We could just drink in our room.”

Having settled the past in my mind, I finally got back to the main topic.

Ever since Senpai started coming to my room, the collection of alcohol on the side of the kitchen has been steadily growing.

There’s the sweet liquor that Senpai prefers, the obligatory beer, and then the wine. I have more alcohol than the average “I drink alcohol~” kind of person.

Of course, it’s mainly Kureha Senpai who drinks. I can only drink a little, and just seeing Senpai enjoy her drinks is enough for me.

That’s why I’m a bit puzzled by the invitation to go to a bar this time.

It’s not because I can’t drink much. I just don’t want to show the slightly tipsy Senpai from alcohol to other people.

Senpai is already eye-catching enough on a regular day. Add to that a state of drunkenness, and she becomes an even more attractive target.

I want to avoid that. I don’t want Senpai to be the focus of other men’s gazes, and I wish only I could look at her that way.

Yet, despite all this, there’s a part of me that can’t resist the allure of a night date with Senpai, something we’ve never done even now that we’re already living together.

“So, you don’t want to go on a night date with me?”

“I want to.”

“I love how you answer so honestly and immediately.”

“Uhh…”

Behind Senpai’s beaming smile, I can’t shake the feeling that she understands my intentions, and even knowing that, she would still choose to tease me.

That’s Kureha Senpai for you, and I can’t help but love her for it, with no complaints whatsoever.

“We couldn’t date at night before because of my house curfew, but now that we’re living together, we can fully enjoy our night dates~”

“Well, the curfew was set by my father. No matter how I am his ‘cute daughter’, I’m over twenty years old. It’s too much to be told to come home by 21:00.”

I’ve heard several times about the strictness of Senpai’s father.

Senpai, who never speaks ill of others, only ever has complaints for one person – her father.

One of the hardships that made Senpai grimace was the curfew. Having a 21:00 curfew as a university student is just too unreasonable.

Perhaps because of that, we hardly ever went on dates after classes before we started living together.

The exception was when she had part-time work; she was allowed to stay out past curfew, but she had to report when she left her job, as if under surveillance…

So, the most couple-like things we did before living together were holding hands before the circle activities started or chatting in the cafeteria.

Knowing that Senpai stayed over on my birthday made me realize the idea of living together was serious.

Even if I had realized it, given my current financial situation, after accounting for necessary withdrawals, there wouldn’t have been much left anyway.

As I braced myself for Senpai’s disappointment, an unexpected response came.

“Don’t worry about that. Takashi-kun, you don’t have to worry about money tonight.”

For a moment, I didn’t understand the meaning of her words.

Don’t worry? About what? Was she not dissapointed? Or was it something else…?

Don’t worry about money? Could it be that she knew all along that I didn’t have any cash on me…?

Various speculations raced through my mind.

“…But I really can’t just let you treat me.”

Without fully understanding Senpai’s intentions, I got carried away with the idea of being treated on our first night date.

Of course, I knew well that Senpai wasn’t a demon. Rather, she was a kind girlfriend who forgave me for making her sad, only temporarily forbidding indulgence in desires.

“Yes?”

“I didn’t say I was treating you~? Of course, I’ll pay for today, but it’s not going to be for free. You should understand that much, right, Takashi-kun? You’re working part-time, after all~?”

“So, you’re saying I need to prepare something of equivalent value…?”

“That’s right. You catch on quick, Takashi-kun. I’m impressed.”

I know that Kureha Senpai is a mischievous troublemaker and that I’m her only target.

In fact, I’m happy and even proud of being the only one she teases.

There’s something to be said about Senpai’s clothing choices too.

Inside the room, her chest is dangerously exposed, or her thighs are completely bare, but when we go out for a date, she dresses nicely while making sure to guard herself properly.

Whether it’s intentional or not, only Senpai knows, but currently, I’m the only one who knows her unguarded appearance.

What more should I ask?

I’m the only one who knows Senpai’s other side.

As her boyfriend, I’m the one who knows the most about Senpai. I won’t share her with anyone. I’m the only one who knows Senpai well, and I’m fine with being the only one she teases.

As I think about this, the self-loathing I felt until now vanishes.

At the same time, I realize there’s a slightly more confident version of myself.

I can’t miss the chance while I’m feeling collected.

So, I casually ask Senpai a question.

“So, what should I do? Just make sure it was something possible for me, okay?”

“Don’t worry. I’ll have you do something that only Takashi-kun can do.”

Senpai answers with a smile, and the next moment, she positions herself right beside me, intertwining her arm and interlocking her finger with mine.

“This is…”

“Let’s stay like this until we get back to our place today.”

The confident me disappeared in an instant.

Senpai’s arm in mine, the interlocking finger, and her sweet voice—a triple combo—reduced me back to my usual self, the one who’s merely teased…

Still, if I can continue to feel Senpai like this even outside, maybe it’s not so bad, I think to myself.

What a lucky person I am.

Kahlua Milk.

Despite its smooth taste, it’s actually a drink with a high alcohol content.

Unlike beer or fruit wines, it has very little of bitterness or distinct taste often associated with alcohol, which has led to its misuse by men, earning it the nickname “Lady Killer” for its potency in intoxicating women.

Ironically, the one being “killed” by it right now is me, a guy.

Sitting next to me, Kureha Senpai is like a cunning female leopard, making her prey feel good and slowly waiting for the chance to pounce.

Just being on a date with Kureha Senpai had me on cloud nine. Add to that the continuous softness of Kureha Senpai I could feel against my arm and the sweet alcohol that could bring back memories of that kiss.

All the conditions were more than enough for Senpai to capture me.

Well, I didn’t feel bad about the situation at all.

On the contrary, I was even hoping that Senpai would have her way with me soon.

Suppose I could dissipate the frustration from the incident with Yuu over the barbecue right here. Even if I couldn’t let go of everything… if it made Senpai feel better…

My melted brain was sinking further.

“Hey, are you okay? Oh, it’s Takashi! What are you doing getting so smashed?”

“Rather, sober up completely. I don’t want to see my best friend all love-struck.”

Yuu’s familiar tomboyish tone.

I slowly turned my drunken head to look behind and there stood my best friend, Sonoda Yuu, in her usual hoodie and waiter’s outfit.

Despite being in front of customers, she didn’t seem to care and kept throwing insults at me as if we were back at the university.

Gradually, my head began to clear.

Probably because the ‘space alone with Kureha Senpai’ was no longer there.

For example, Yuu’s outfit.

She probably didn’t think I would care about such things.

She laughed carelessly, showing no signs of leaving.

“Um… what exactly is your relationship with Takashi-kun…?”

Perhaps reaching her limit of patience, Senpai spoke to Yuu with a displeased look, trying to fend her off.

Despite having finished our conversation, Yuu showed no signs of leaving.

“By the way, aren’t you going to take off your hood? I believe it’s customer service time now.”

Said my companion next to me, irritation evident in her voice.

Yuu said, apologizing while pulling her hood down deeper as if she didn’t want to show her face.
