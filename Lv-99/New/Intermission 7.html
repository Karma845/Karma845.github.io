Interlude

“He was drinking my alcohol, wasn’t he…”

When I close my eyes, I can’t help but picture my lover, who usually wouldn’t drink, choosing to drink on his own.

The fact that he prepared alcohol when I wasn’t looking, by his own volition, was surprising.

“I wonder what he was up to…”

I’m not angry. I’m not upset that he drank my alcohol.

After all, I bought it for us to drink together. In fact, I want him to drink more.

By seeing which bottles are getting emptier, I can tell Takashi-kun’s preferences, which is quite convenient for me.

Still… I can’t deny feeling a bit lonely that I couldn’t drink with Takashi-kun.

No matter how much I want to get along with Yuu-chan, the loneliness is still there even if we could drink together over a video call. But, that aside…

“Marriage, huh…”

I never imagined that Takashi-kun would propose to me. Even if it was spurred by the alcohol, I was incredibly happy.

And yet, there was a shock. I hadn’t anticipated a proposal from Takashi-kun at all.

Feeling unusually nervous, I spent the whole night awake.
