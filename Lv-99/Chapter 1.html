Chapter 1: That Girl I'm Interested In

Kinoshita-san is always alone.

This was not due to the fact that it was the first day of her second year.

Kinoshita-san’s test scores have always been at the top of the class since the first year, and her athletic ability was unrivaled in the entire school.

She’s tall and fashionable. The girls were always envious of her, and the boys always looked up to her as the ‘ideal girl’.

There was a reason as to why such a gifted and beautiful young lady was always alone.

Kinoshita-san has been keeping people away from her on her own volition.

Her interaction with her classmates was extremely minimal, and she refused to make eye contact with anyone. She never smiled or showed any emotion whatsoever. She would frequently ignore the people who would speak to her. She may appear to be a cool beauty, but she’s actually just a shy, unsociable person.

Rumors say that she was ‘Too beautiful and scary,’ they said that ‘You’ll get rejected just by approaching her,’ they also said that she was ‘A girl with an unbreakable ice wall,’ and so on, this all continued until she was said to have an aura of someone who said ‘Don’t approach me,’ and nobody approached her after that began sprouting around.

Even now, while everyone else in the class is trying to make friends with each other, Kinoshita-san is the only one who’s sitting silently in her seat, reading a book as if it were second nature. She appears to be attempting to conceal herself by being alone, but her glamorous and lonely figure is as divine as a William Adolphe Bouguereau painting, and so I was glued to her back portrait.

“We should go to the gym soon, Satsuki.”

“Uh-huh, I’ll be right there.”

I get up from my seat when a classmate calls out to me.

I was worried about Kinoshita-san for a reason.

I had only spoken to her once, that was when I was a freshman.

At the time, that one word she said made me unable to stop thinking about her.

It’s because of Kinoshita-san’s secret, which only I know about…

It was half a year ago, at the “Otomachi Festival” of our Otomachi High School. It was my first festival as a high school student.

Otomachi High School has a school culture of hosting really fun events, and it is a tradition to always go all out at the festival, with the involvement of many alumni and faculty members.

I had been in the gymnasium where the clubs were presenting their talks in the morning, but in the afternoon I slipped out on my own and wandered about, looking at the exhibits in each classroom.

From the standard haunted house and fashion show, some classes were even having beach houses.

A plastic pool had been brought into the classroom, and men and women in swimsuits were having fun with water guns in their hands.

I wanted to get into a bathing suit and mingle with them, but I was not that excited enough to do so and just passed by quietly.

I was looking for a place where I could relax, and I found myself at the Piloti. There, food and drink stalls were lined up in a row, symbolic of a cultural festival. “Welcome, welcome!”

“It’s delicious!”

It was just about lunchtime and the crowds were heating up when I spotted a food stall tucked away at the end of the street.

It appeared to be a cafe, but there were no customers there at all. There was only one girl sitting in the back of the stall with her back faced towards us.

The smell of coffee had lured me in, and I wandered over to the stall to see if I could find a place to relax for a bit.

“One coffee, please.”

“……”

When I ordered something, the girl who was tending to the store slammed the book she was reading shut.

Then she silently began to grind some coffee beans with a hand mill.

I was impressed by the authenticity of the store, but she never looked at me or said a single word of appreciation for entering the store.

Although I wanted to be alone, I felt uneasy and wanted to talk to her when she was blatantly ignoring me like this.

“It’s really authentic.”

“……”

“Where’s the coffee from?”

“……”

“Have you been tending the bar this morning all by yourself?”

“…Yes, something like that.”

I covered my mouth to say nothing more in response.

It was a silent wait while the coffee was being hand-brewed, and the saltiness of the place was evident. I could understand why there were no customers coming here.

Soon the fragrant aroma of the coffee began to emerge, and the girl turned around with a cup in her hand.

She looked back at me with a cup in her hand

“……!”

The sight of her made me take a second look.

There she was, Yayoi Kinoshita.

She had straight, semi-long black hair with big, dark eyes that seemed to draw me in.

Standing up, she was tall and slender, with long, graceful arms and legs that made her look like a model. Her apron tied around her waist emphasized the waistline of her body, further accentuating her good looks.

Even though we were in different classes, I had heard a lot of rumors about her, because even though we were both first-year students, she was already famous enough that she was the first name that came to mind when people talked about beautiful women.

Kinoshita-san’s cold voice and icy demeanor.

This brusque and salty attitude was the most spoken about bad rumor.

They even told me that she has a “Stay away from me” aura around her, and I understand that it’s no wonder she ignored me when I spoke to her earlier.

Yayoi Kinoshita, whom I met for the first time, is just as the rumors say, for better or worse, and I get a strange feeling that I don’t know whether I’ve gained or lost something.

I’m sure there’s a lot to be said for beautiful women around the world. It’s a world I don’t understand anything about.

“Oh, right!”

As I was about to quickly pay and walk away, Kinoshita-san, who had been keeping her eyes down for a long time, looked up at me.

The first time our eyes met, Kinoshita-san’s eyes were drawn to my face. She just stared at me without saying a single word in response.

What is it? Is there something on my face? We’ve never met before, right?

Suddenly I felt uneasy and tried to take the money out of my wallet, but I must have been too nervous to do so. In my haste, I dropped the money onto the table.

“Ah!”

“Ehhh?”

I thought my world had been turned upside down by the shock of the words I had just heard.

As if a ta-da! and Beethoven’s grand symphony had begun playing out loud, my whole body went numb and I couldn’t move an inch.

…Me, cool?

It had to be Kinoshita-san’s voice.

It was not the emotionless, barbed voice from a moment ago, but was instead a voice filled with solid emotion.

I looked up slowly, with our hands still on top of each other, and to my disbelief, Kinoshita-san’s cheeks were flushed red.

“Ah! I’m sorry!”

I reflexively apologize and withdraw my hand that was touching Kinoshita-san The next moment, she glared at me with a grimace.

“Thank you for the co-coffee!”

I was so freaked out by Ms. Kinoshita’s glare that I accepted the coffee and left the place as if I were running away.

What was that? What was that?!

I went through the crowded stalls and ran down the hallway, not caring about the students I had passed by.

I arrived at the side of the school gate, which was completely empty. I try to calm down, but my heart is still beating at an alarming rate.

“That Yayoi Kinoshita… was she talking about me?”

I stare at my left hand and utter the cause of my excitement.

The words I heard the moment I touched Kinoshita-san. Those words were not the ones she uttered out loud.

That was the voice of Kinoshita-san’s mind that I heard directly in my head.

Psychometry.

It is the ability to read the residual thoughts that remain in an object.

When I, Satsuki Fukase, touch someone with my left hand, I can read what that person is thinking about.

People tend to think it’s a useful ability to understand people’s feelings, but most of the voices in my mind are just bad words or negative ones. There is nothing good in revealing other people’s secrets one way or the other.

I was usually careful to avoid using this kind of ability as much as possible…

The most important thing to remember is that you can’t just take a chance on a person and expect to be able to get away with it.

‘Satsuki-kun, you’re so cool.’

The voice of Kinoshita’s heart, which I read from her cold hand that I had touched, repeated endlessly in my head like a broken record.

How does she know about me?

What does she mean by “cool”?

Maybe she likes me?

I don’t know how to suppress my fantasies, which leaped at an accelerated rate. This is the first time I’ve heard positive feelings about myself using psychometry, and I was elated.

And the person I was talking to was the most beautiful girl in school, Yayoi Kinoshita……

Half a year has passed since that shocking encounter with Kinoshita-san We are now in the second year, and today was the opening ceremony of the first semester.

Otomachi High School is a public high school in the Hiroshima Prefecture, and it’s one of the best preparatory schools in the prefecture, boasting some rather excellent results in higher education.

It’s located in the center of Hiroshima City and has excellent accessibility. Hiroshima Castle is right next to the school, and there’s also a river nearby, so there’s a lot of nature around. It is said that a soccer stadium will be built nearby in a couple of years.

The four-story school building is a novel design, with classrooms lined up in a “B” shape, the center of which has an atrium from the ground floor to the roof, and an escalator, which is unusual for a public high school. I like the sense of openness of the school building, where the light floods in through the glass roof and you can always feel the color of the sky and the wind as you walk around.

At the opening ceremony held in the gymnasium, the vice principal was just about to speak.

The new second-year students are aware of their status as upperclassmen, and each of them are working toward their own goals…..

Pretending to be listening to the vice principal’s appreciative talk, I was absentmindedly watching a girl a little ahead of me in the line.

She was as tall as a model, and even though we were lined up in order by our attendance number, her head stuck out one notch from the others, and her beautiful black hair was shimmering with angel rings surrounding her appearance.

She is Yayoi Kinoshita, who is in the same class as us from today onwards.

She stands out in this gymnasium of several hundred people, and as I stare at her, I think back to last year’s school festival.

“Satsuki-kun, you’re so cool.”

That day, I accidentally heard her voice in my mind through psychometry, and I have not been able to get those words out of my head ever since.

I think Yayoi Kinoshita might like me.

There were nights when I couldn’t sleep thinking about why she would like me, and I was quite delusional during those times.

I thought that I wanted to get close to her, but that was impossible. In the first year, we were in different classes, and Kinoshita-san had an air that no one could even try to get close to.

But in the second year, here we are in the same class together, and I would be lying if I said I had no expectations.

Even now, I’m listening to what the vice principal has to say and fantasizing about my new sweet life in high school.
